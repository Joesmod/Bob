## Overview
**Location:** 548 5th Avenue, San Diego, CA 92101 (Gaslamp District)

**Website:** https://prohibitionsd.com  

## About
Prohibition is San Diego's original speakeasy bar, hidden underground in the Gaslamp District. The venue transports guests to the 1920s Prohibition era with period-appropriate atmosphere, masterfully-crafted cocktails, and stylized live music. Located behind an unmarked "Eddie O'Hare's Law Office" door on 5th Avenue, Prohibition offers an immersive sensory experience reminiscent of when drinking spirits was illegal.
