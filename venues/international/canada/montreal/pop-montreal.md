# POP Montreal International Music Festival

## Overview
POP Montreal International Music Festival is an annual not-for-profit curated cultural event that champions independence in the arts by presenting emerging and celebrated artistic talents from around the world. Founded in 2002 by friends and colleagues from an impulse to create meaningful and vibrant experiences in the city, the festival is now a fixture in the Montreal festival circuit and the international music scene.

**Location:** 5585 ave du Parc, Montreal (QC) H2V 4H2

## Festival Details

### Format & Programming
POP Montreal is a multi-day festival (September 23-27, 2026) featuring:
- Music performances across multiple venues
- Comedy programming
- Film screenings (Film POP)
- Art exhibitions (Art POP)
- Symposium discussions (POP Symposium)
- Children's activities (Kids POP)
- Craft market (Puces POP)
- Marché des Possibles

### History & Notable Artists
- **Founded:** 2002
- **Status:** Registered charity / 501(c)(3) nonprofit
- **Years Active:** 23+ years (as of 2026)
- **Notable Past Performers:** Alanis Obomsawin, Patti Smith, Mavis Staples, Grimes, Patrick Watson, Kaytranada, SOPHIE

### Application Process
Unique feature: POP Montreal has been listening to every artist that applies to the festival since its creation 23 years ago. Year after year, music curators, musicians, and professionals gather for listening sessions to vote on submissions. These outstanding submissions make up a central portion of the festival's programming.
