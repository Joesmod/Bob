# End of the Road Festival

## Overview
End of the Road is an annual music festival held in Dorset, UK, known for its eclectic programming and laid-back atmosphere. The festival has earned critical acclaim as one of the UK's premier boutique music festivals.

**Location:** Larmer Tree Gardens, Dorset, UK

## Festival Details
