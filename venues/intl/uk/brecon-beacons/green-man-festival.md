## About

Major independent music and arts festival held annually in the Brecon Beacons National Park, Wales, UK. Green Man is one of the UK's most respected boutique festivals, known for its beautiful natural setting, family-friendly atmosphere, diverse programming, and commitment to sustainability.

**Dates**: August 20-23, 2026  
**Status**: GM26 tickets SOLD OUT

**Location**: Glanusk Park, Brecon Beacons, Wales

## History

Green Man Festival has been running for over 20 years, growing from a small gathering to one of the UK's premier independent festivals. It maintains an intimate, community feel despite its growth, with a strong emphasis on environmental sustainability ("Being Green" is a core pillar).
