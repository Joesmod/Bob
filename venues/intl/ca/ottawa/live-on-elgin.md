# LIVE! on Elgin

## Overview
All-ages multi-purpose venue in Ottawa focused on live theatre and music. Created to fill a gap in the Ottawa market for an affordable, accessible space for local artists to perform and develop their craft. Emphasizes supporting local talent across music, theatre, film, and performance arts. Owned and operated by the Evenchick family.

## History
Opened following two years of discussions, debates, and community polling about the future of live entertainment in Ottawa. Founded by Lawrence Evenchick (theatre background since 1979) and Jon Evenchick (Ottawa Music Bus founder, music community advocate). Created as a home for local artists without requiring them to leave town to perform.
