# Yuk Yuk's Comedy Club Ottawa

## Overview
Canadian comedy club chain location in Ottawa West. Part of Yuk Yuk's national network of comedy venues. Presents weekly stand-up comedy shows featuring touring Canadian and international comedians, plus monthly new talent showcases.

## History
Yuk Yuk's is Canada's longest-running chain of comedy clubs, founded in Toronto in 1976 by Mark Breslin. The Ottawa West location is part of their national expansion bringing professional stand-up comedy to cities across Canada.

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 30/100 — Poor to Moderate Fit**
Part of a national comedy club chain, which significantly reduces switching likelihood. Chain operations typically have corporate-mandated systems and national ticketing partnerships. Decision-making happens at corporate level, not individual venue level. While they do regular ticketed shows (good operational fit), corporate structure makes them a challenging prospect. Would require corporate-level partnership discussions rather than individual venue sales. Better to focus on independent comedy venues or smaller local chains where venue operators have decision-making authority.
