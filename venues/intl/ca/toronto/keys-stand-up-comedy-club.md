## About

Key's Toronto Stand-up Comedy Club is a premier destination in Toronto for unfiltered humor and high-energy stand-up comedy. The club features globally recognized comedians with millions of social media views who have performed at top venues like Joe Rogan's Comedy Club (The Mothership) and Just For Laughs Festivals. Regular roster includes comedians with appearances on the Kill Tony podcast. Shows consistently sell out.

## History
