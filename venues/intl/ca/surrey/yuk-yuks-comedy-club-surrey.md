# Yuk Yuk's Comedy Club Surrey

## Overview
Comedy club in Surrey, British Columbia (Greater Vancouver area), part of Canada's iconic Yuk Yuk's comedy chain. Features national touring comedians and local Pacific Northwest talent in a classic comedy club setting.

## History
Part of Yuk Yuk's, Canada's oldest and largest comedy club chain. Founded in 1976, Yuk Yuk's has been the launching pad for many Canadian comedy legends. The Surrey location continues the tradition of showcasing top stand-up talent.
