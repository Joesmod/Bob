# Waterton Lakes Opera House (Movie Theater & Concert Hall)

## Overview
Historic movie theater and opera house in Waterton Park, Alberta, Canada. Primarily operates as a cinema showing current films, with occasional live events. Part of the Waterton Lakes National Park tourism infrastructure.
