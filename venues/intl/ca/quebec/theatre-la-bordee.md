# Théâtre La Bordée

## Overview
French-language professional theater company in Québec City, presenting plays, musicals, and contemporary theater. One of Québec's established cultural institutions with season subscriptions and diverse programming.

## History
Established theater company in Québec's cultural scene. Recently appointed new artistic director Charles-Étienne Beaulne. Presents a full season annually with subscription packages.
