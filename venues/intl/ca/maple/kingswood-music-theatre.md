# Kingswood Music Theatre

## Overview
Outdoor amphitheater located within Canada's Wonderland theme park in Maple, Ontario (Vaughan, north of Toronto). Part of Six Flags Entertainment Corporation. Hosts concerts and live entertainment as part of the park experience.

## History
Kingswood Music Theatre is one of Canada's Wonderland's entertainment venues. Canada's Wonderland opened in 1981 and features over 200 attractions including 18 roller coasters and the 20-acre Splash Works water park. Six Flags acquired the park and operates it as a four-season amusement destination.
