# Centre des arts de Baie-Comeau

## Overview
Major performing arts center in Baie-Comeau, Québec. Professional multidisciplinary presenter with two performance spaces and extensive programming. One of the region's primary cultural hubs for Manicouagan.

## History
Originally opened as "Théâtre de Baie-Comeau" on September 12, 1992, responding to community demand for a professional performance hall. In summer 2010, underwent major expansion that nearly doubled the facility size, prompting name change to Centre des arts de Baie-Comeau. Expansion added music school, string orchestra, dance academy, and library service point in addition to second performance space.

## Facilities
- **Salle principale (Main Hall)**: 840-seat Italian-style theater
- **Espace Alcoa**: Variable geometry space with capacity of 240 (cabaret), 330 (tiered seating), or 500 (standing)

Additional tenants:
- École de musique Côte-Nord
- Orchestre à cordes de Baie-Comeau
- Académie de danse de Baie-Comeau
- Bibliothèque Alice-Lane service point

- **Series**: Multiple series including Série-Famille Hydro-Québec (family series), Série Danse (dance series)
