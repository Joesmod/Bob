## About

Historic 900+ seat opera house in downtown Orillia, Ontario. Built in 1895, this venue is operated by the City of Orillia and hosts a diverse range of ticketed live entertainment including tribute shows, Broadway productions, comedy, music, and theatrical performances. A cornerstone of Orillia's cultural scene with active year-round programming.

## History

Established in 1895, the Orillia Opera House has been serving the community for over 130 years. Owned and operated by the City of Orillia, it stands as one of Ontario's historic performance venues. The theatre has been continuously presenting shows and events for well over a century.
