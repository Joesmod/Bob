## About

Harvest Festival is a four-day electronic music, art, and community gathering held annually each September at Midlothian Castle and its famous "Screaming Heads" outdoor art installation near Burk's Falls, Ontario. Running since 1998 (celebrating 25th anniversary in 2024), this festival showcases electronic music on multiple stages, circus performances, and multimedia art in a unique outdoor setting. Combines intimate gathering vibes with high-production festival energy.

## History

Established in 1998, Harvest Festival has been bringing together friends and electronic music communities for nearly three decades. Produced by alienInFlux Sound System & Event Productions. The festival has evolved into an annual ritual for many attendees, taking place during the fall equinox with a backdrop of rolling hills, forests, and unique sculptures at Midlothian Castle.
