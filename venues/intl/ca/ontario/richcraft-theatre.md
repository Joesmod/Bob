## About

Richcraft Theatre is a flexible black box studio at the Shenkman Arts Centre in Orléans (Ottawa). 162-seat capacity with multiple seating configurations including italienne, cabaret, and custom layouts. Features acoustic treatment, movable catwalk, lighting, sprung floor, and covered pit. Designed as a minimal-service turnkey rental space for a wide range of performances and events.

## History

Part of the Shenkman Arts Centre, a municipal facility operated by the City of Ottawa. The Richcraft Theatre is one of several performance and rental spaces within the larger arts complex.

## Programming & Shows

Primarily a **rental venue** available for:
- Concerts
- Plays and musicals
- Poetry readings
- Dance performances
- Recitals
- Film/TV studio use
- Photography studio
- Rehearsals and auditions
- Fashion shows
- Fundraising events
- Presentations, seminars, workshops

This is not a presenting venue with its own programming—it's a rental space for external producers and organizations.
