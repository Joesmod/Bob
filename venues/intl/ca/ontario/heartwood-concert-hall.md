## About

Heartwood Hall is a 185-capacity concert hall and event space located on the second floor in downtown Owen Sound. A warm, naturally lit venue that presents carefully curated roots rock, folk, blues, soul, and reggae artists. Known for intimate, high-quality performances in a welcoming atmosphere. Also operates as Parkwood location with restaurant/bar programming.

## History

Established as a dedicated live music venue in Owen Sound's downtown core. Connected to Heartwood Home retail store. Has become a key venue for roots and Americana music in the Grey-Bruce region of Ontario.
