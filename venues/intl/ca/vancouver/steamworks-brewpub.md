# Steamworks Brewing - Gastown Brew Pub

## Overview
Canada's first urban brew pub (opened 1995) in Vancouver's Gastown neighborhood. Two-floor brewpub with 20 rotating taps and private event spaces. Not a ticketed music venue.

## Venue Details
- **Size**: 8,191 sq ft across two floors
- **Capacity**: Large brewpub with private event rooms
- **Features**: 20 rotating taps, copper fermentors, Vancouver Harbour views

## Programming & Shows
