# 3rd Floor Comedy Club

## Overview
English-language comedy club in downtown Montreal. Located on the third floor above Stogies on Crescent Street. Intimate comedy venue showcasing Montreal's stand-up talent with regular weekly shows plus Sunday open mics. Self-described as "the city's most intimate club."

## History
Established comedy venue in Montreal's entertainment district. Known as hidden gem showcasing "the level of Montreal stand-up." Intimate setting provides professional platform for local and touring comedians.
