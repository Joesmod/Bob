# Caf'Conc' (Café Concert)

## Overview
Intimate speakeasy-style performance venue located in the Château Champlain hotel in downtown Montreal. Describes itself as "the hidden gem of Château Champlain" and "Montreal's best-kept secret," offering an immersive experience inspired by 1920s speakeasies.

## Atmosphere & Concept
Elegant, intimate venue with:
- Impeccable acoustics
- Warm, intimate ambiance
- High-quality service
- Speakeasy aesthetic from the Roaring Twenties
- Located within a historic Montreal hotel

## Programming & Shows
- **Type**: Live performances and shows
- **Frequency**: Regular programming (details limited)
- **Vibe**: Intimate performances where "stage art and hospitality meet in perfect harmony"
- **Focus**: Curated shows in elegant speakeasy setting
