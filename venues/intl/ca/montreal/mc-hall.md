# MC Hall

## Overview
Private event rental space in Côte-des-Neiges, Montreal. Not a ticketing venue — hosts weddings, corporate events, private parties, and receptions. Capacity up to 140 people. Features catering, bar service, and AV equipment.

## Facility
- **Capacity**: 140 people
- **Size**: 1,800 sq ft
- **Location**: 2 minutes from metro, Côte-des-Neiges
- **Amenities**: Full bar, catering kitchen, high-quality sound system, projector, rustic/urban decor

## Services
- Corporate events (meetings, seminars, conferences, workshops, retirement parties)
- Private celebrations (weddings, birthdays, showers, celebrations of life)
- Catering services and bar packages
- Custom event planning
- AV/tech support
