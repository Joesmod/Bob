# Cabaret Lion d'Or

## Overview
Historic cabaret venue in Montreal's Quartier latin on rue Ontario Est. Active calendar of live music, improv, comedy, and cultural events. Intimate venue with full bar and restaurant (Au Petit Extra) next door. Part of Montreal's vibrant French-language arts scene.

## History
Established cabaret on rue Ontario Est in Montreal's Quartier latin district. Long-standing venue in the neighborhood cultural landscape.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Live music (indie, folk, singer-songwriter), improv (LA LIM weekly), experimental performances, comedy, cabaret
- **Frequency**: 25-30 shows per month (nearly nightly programming)
- **Ticket prices**: $10-$40 typical
- **Notable programming**: 
  - Francouvertes (emerging artist showcase)
  - LA LIM (weekly improv)
  - Harry Potter drinking show
  - Burlesque performances
  - Open mic nights
  - Them Fatale (trans/non-binary cabaret)
- Very active calendar with diverse, culturally engaged programming
- General admission (first come, first served seating)
