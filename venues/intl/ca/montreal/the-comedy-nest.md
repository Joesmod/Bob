# The Comedy Nest

## Overview
Montreal's premier English-language comedy club. Located in downtown Montreal at the Forum. Voted Montreal's #1 Comedy Club in CultMtl's 2025 Readers Poll. Nightly stand-up shows featuring local talent and touring comedians.

## History
Well-established comedy venue in downtown Montreal, housed in the Forum complex. Long-standing reputation as Montreal's top English comedy club.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Stand-up comedy (English)
- **Frequency**: 20-25 shows per month (nightly, often 2 shows on weekends)
- **Ticket prices**: $6-$17.50 (Newbie Tuesdays $6, weekend headliners $15-$17.50)
- **Notable acts**: 
  - Joey Elias (MatchGame, Just For Laughs)
  - Derek Seguin (CBC's The Debaters, 2x Juno nominee)
  - International touring comedians
  - Rising talent from Just For Laughs, Comedy Central, CTV Comedy Channel
- **Regular programs**:
  - Newbie Tuesday ($6 + free pass to future show)
  - Comedy Lab Show (Wednesday new material nights)
  - All-Star Stand-Ups
  - Weekend headliner showcases (8pm + 10:30pm)
- 90-minute shows, 18+ venue
- $6 parking at the Forum
