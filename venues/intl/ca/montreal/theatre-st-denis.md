# Théâtre St-Denis

## Overview
Historic 2,100+ seat theater in Montreal's Quartier latin, now part of Espace St-Denis entertainment complex. Opened March 4, 1916. Undergoing major renovation/expansion as part of ambitious multi-venue complex including Le Studio-Cabaret, restaurants (Le Molière, Trattoria Del Teatro, Le Marie-Louise), and future VR/AI experience center.

## History
Opened March 4, 1916 (110 years old in 2026). One of Montreal's most iconic theaters with ornate architectural details, historic chandeliers, and orchestra pit. Underwent numerous transformations over its century-plus history. Currently being transformed into Espace St-Denis, a major cultural/entertainment complex while preserving historic architectural elements.
