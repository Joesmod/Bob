# Union Française de Montréal

## Overview
French cultural and social organization founded in 1886. Not a ticketing venue — primarily a community center, cultural hub, and support organization for French immigrants in Montreal. Features artist residencies, cultural programming, and social services.

## History
Founded 1886 (140 years old). Historic French cultural organization serving Montreal's French community. Mission: strengthen ties between France and Quebec, provide meeting space and cultural programming.

## Programming
- Artist residencies (visual artists, painters, sculptors, photographers)
- Cultural center and exhibition space
- Community support services (Centre d'aide et d'écoute)
- French immigrant support and resources
- Not a ticketing venue for public shows
