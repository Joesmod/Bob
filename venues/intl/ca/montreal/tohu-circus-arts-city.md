# TOHU (Circus Arts City)

## Overview
Cultural complex dedicated to circus arts, environmental sustainability, and community engagement. Located in Montreal's Cité des arts du cirque. Founded 2004. Primarily hosts circus performances, exhibitions, and community programs rather than traditional ticketed shows.

## History
Founded in 2004 as a convergence point for culture, environment, and community involvement. Located in the heart of the Cité des arts du cirque. Mission: "the earth, the circus, the human being." Major focus on sustainable development through culture.

## Facility
- Circus performance space
- Exhibition galleries
- Frédéric-Back Park (former landfill, now major environmental rehabilitation project)
- TOHU Winter Center (skating rink, seasonal activities)

## Programming
- Circus arts performances (seasonal)
- Exhibitions (permanent and temporary)
  - "L'art... du cirque" (permanent exhibition)
  - "Terre avenir" (outdoor exhibition)
  - "Circoluza" (virtual exhibition)
- Seasonal activities (skating rink, sledding, kites)
- Community programs and workshops
- **Current status (March 2026)**: Limited performance calendar, mostly free community activities and exhibitions
