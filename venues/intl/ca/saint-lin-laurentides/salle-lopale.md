# Salle L'Opale

## Overview
Multi-purpose community center and cabaret-style venue in Saint-Lin-Laurentides, Quebec. Hosts comedy shows, tribute concerts, and French-language performances in an intimate cabaret setting. Capacity approximately 200-300 for seated shows.

## History
Community center serving Saint-Lin-Laurentides since at least the early 2000s. Located at 510, rue Saint-Isidore. Functions as both a performance venue and community gathering space.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Comedy (French), tribute shows, crooner concerts, themed events
- **Frequency**: 6-10 shows per month
- **Ticket prices**: $15-$45 typical
- **Notable acts**: P-A Méthot (major Quebec comedian), Francis Degrandpré (country), Anthony Kavanagh, tribute shows (Elvis, Beatles, Shania Twain, Red Hot Chili Peppers)
- Active calendar with mix of local and touring Quebec talent
- Cabaret-style seating with table service
