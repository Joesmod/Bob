# Paul Davenport Theatre

## Overview
Concert hall and performance venue at the Don Wright Faculty of Music, Western University in London, Ontario, Canada. Part of a university music school presenting 350+ concerts and recitals annually. Wheelchair accessible (except balcony), equipped with elevators and ramp access.

## History
Located in the Music Building at Western University (founded 1878), the Paul Davenport Theatre is part of the Don Wright Faculty of Music's performance facilities. Named after Paul Davenport, former President of Western University (1994-2009).

## Programming & Shows
- **Primary Use**: University music faculty concerts, recitals, and performances
- **Genres**: Classical music, opera, chamber music, student recitals, faculty concerts, guest artists
- **Major Productions**: Fully staged operas (e.g., Sweeney Todd, March 2026)
- **Special Series**: 
  - Opera at Western (fully staged productions)
  - Chamber Music Recital Series
  - Faculty and Guest Artist Concerts
  - Fridays at 12:30 Series
  - Masterclasses and Residencies
- **Frequency**: 350+ events per year across all venues
- **Ticket prices**: Most concerts FREE; ticketed events (operas, special concerts) vary, door sales +$5
- **Season**: Academic year programming (September-April)
