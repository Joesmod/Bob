# Midland Cultural Centre

## Overview
Multi-use cultural facility in Midland, Ontario featuring Rotary Hall (274-seat concert venue), Indigenous art gallery, Quest Art School + Gallery, and Huronia Players Theatre (121 seats). Presents concerts, theater, art exhibitions, and community programming. Built 2012 through $11M private donation from Weber Foundation.

## History
Founded 2012 by philanthropist Reinhart Weber through the Weber Foundation, which provided 95% of construction costs. Built without public funding. Ownership transferred to Town of Midland, operated by Midland Cultural Centre Inc. on a 49-year lease. Celebrating 10+ years of operation as of 2022.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Concerts (various genres), theater, art exhibitions, community events
- **Rotary Hall Capacity**: 274 for concerts (floor + balcony), 130 for seated events
- **Huronia Players Theatre**: 121 seats for theater productions
- **Events**: Concerts, weddings, corporate events, conferences, celebrations
- **Community**: Open mics, jams, gallery exhibitions
- **Frequency**: Regular programming throughout the year
