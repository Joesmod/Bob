## About

Expo Chihuahua is a large-scale convention and exhibition center, not an entertainment ticketing venue. The facility features 22,000 m² of rentable space including exhibition halls and meeting rooms for trade shows, conventions, banquets, corporate events, and expositions.

## Facility Details

- **Exhibition Space**: 12,000 m² with 9-meter ceilings
- **Convention Space**: 6,000 m² with 18 simultaneous meeting rooms
- **Additional**: 2 VIP rooms, lobby, atrium, press room, loading docks
- Recognized as one of Mexico's top 10 convention facilities
