# Alchemy Comedy Theater

## Overview

Intimate comedy venue in Coffee Underground (basement of downtown Greenville), operating since 2011. Features weekly improv, stand-up, and sketch comedy with local performers and special guests. Also offers improv classes. Open Thursday-Saturday, 7-10pm.

## History

Founded in 2011, Alchemy Comedy Theater has been Greenville's primary dedicated comedy venue for 15 years. The name plays on "alchemy" meaning "transforming the common into the special" — their mission of elevating local comedy talent and building community around live performance.

Located in Coffee Underground, a well-known downtown Greenville gathering spot at 1 E Coffee St.
