## About

The Greenville Event Center is a rental venue operated by the Hejaz Shriners. They offer two spaces for private events: the ballroom holds 100-1000+ guests and features a full-size stage, large dance floor, bar area, 4 bathrooms, 2 greenrooms, chandeliers, and disco balls. The dining room accommodates 115 people with bar, dance floor area, and space for entertainment.

The venue is designed for flexibility — clients can bring their own catering, entertainment, and alcohol, or have it provided. Rental includes table/chair setup, free ice, and access the day before for setup/decorating.
