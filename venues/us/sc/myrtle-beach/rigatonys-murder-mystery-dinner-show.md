## About

RigaTony's Murder Mystery Dinner Show combines Italian food with live interactive theater. Guests witness comical crimes, gather clues, mingle with suspects, and try to solve the mystery before the final reveal. The experience is highly interactive with actors engaging the audience throughout the show.

The production features professional actors who stay in character throughout, rotating shows (so guests can return without seeing the same performance), and seasonal specials. Known for family-friendly entertainment suitable for all ages.

## History

Multi-location dinner theater concept with flagship in Myrtle Beach. Recently expanded to Branson, MO and Pigeon Forge, TN markets.
