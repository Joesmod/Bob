# Holy City Brewing

## Overview
Craft brewery and taproom in North Charleston, SC. Operating since 2011, Holy City features live music, trivia nights, and ticketed events. The venue includes multiple event spaces: The Mezzanine (private upstairs dining), Aqua Lounge (21+ game room), Courtyard (open-air space for 100+ guests), and Porter Room (largest space with prep kitchen and marshside views). Also home to The Walrus raw bar serving oysters and seafood.

## History
Founded in 2011 by a team of four craft beer enthusiasts dedicated to serving their community. Has grown from a brewery to a full entertainment destination with in-house food, raw bar, and multiple event spaces.
