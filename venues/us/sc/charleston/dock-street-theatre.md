## About Charleston Stage

Charleston Stage is a professional nonprofit theater company presenting theatrical productions, musicals, and educational programming. The organization operates two venues in Charleston and offers season subscriptions, group tickets, and educational programs including TheatreSchool.

## History

Dock Street Theatre is a historic venue in Charleston. Charleston Stage has operated as the resident company since becoming the area's largest professional nonprofit theater.
