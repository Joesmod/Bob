# Brookgreen Gardens

## Overview
Historic sculpture garden and botanical garden in Murrells Inlet, SC (near Myrtle Beach). Founded in 1931 by Archer and Anna Hyatt Huntington. 9,100-acre property featuring American sculpture collection, gardens, zoo, and Lowcountry history exhibits. Accredited by American Alliance of Museums and Association of Zoos and Aquariums. Designated National Historic Landmark.

## History
Founded in 1931 on former rice plantation lands. One of the first public sculpture gardens in America, featuring the work of Anna Hyatt Huntington and hundreds of other American sculptors. Expanded over decades to include botanical gardens, wildlife exhibits, and cultural programming focused on South Carolina Lowcountry heritage.
