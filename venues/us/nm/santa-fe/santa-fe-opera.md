## About

The Santa Fe Opera is a world-renowned opera house presenting high-quality ensemble performances in a unique outdoor amphitheater setting. Known for its mission to advance the operatic art form, the company presents a varied repertoire of new works, rarely performed pieces, and standard opera classics. The venue is recognized internationally as one of the world's great centers for performing arts.

## History

The Santa Fe Opera has a longstanding reputation in the international opera community, presenting professional productions in their distinctive open-air theater. The venue is listed among significant performing arts institutions and attracts both established opera stars and emerging talent through its apprentice programs.
