# Center for Contemporary Arts Santa Fe

## Overview
Community-supported arts center featuring cinema, contemporary chamber music, immersive theater, visual arts, and poetry. Two cinema screens (109-seat and 49-seat), plus Muñoz Waxman Gallery and Spector-Ripps Gallery. Year-round schedule of films, Chatter concerts, Exodus Ensemble theater, and Renesan educational courses. Located at 1050 Old Pecos Trail.

## History
Established as Santa Fe's only independent, nonprofit cinema. Hosts resident organizations including Chatter (chamber music), Exodus Ensemble (immersive theater), and ColLAB (community collaboration initiative). Participates in Santa Fe International Film Festival and Jewish Film Festival. Free parking, open seven days a week.

## Programming & Shows
- **Cinema**: Independent, foreign, documentary films (first run)
- **Film Series**: Closer Looks, Amplified (music films), Retrospectives, Indigenous Film, Black Cinema
- **Chatter**: Saturday 10:30am contemporary chamber music concerts
- **Exodus Ensemble**: Immersive theater productions (18+)
- **Renesan Courses**: Educational presentations on history, art, culture ($20-$66)
- **Poetry**: Workshops and readings
- **Visual Arts**: Free Cinema Gallery exhibitions
