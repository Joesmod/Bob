## About

University performing arts series at New Mexico Institute of Mining and Technology (New Mexico Tech). Presents multicultural performances and events in the Macey Center. Mission is to present excellent, innovative and engaging performances that strengthen and educate the community in central New Mexico. Acknowledges the land as ancestral Piro and Mescalero Apache territory.

## History

Long-standing performing arts program at New Mexico Tech serving Socorro and central New Mexico communities.
