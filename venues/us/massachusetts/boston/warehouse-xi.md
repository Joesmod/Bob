# Warehouse Xi

Event rental space in Somerville, MA (Boston area). 3,225 sq ft venue for weddings, parties, workshops, photoshoots, and pop-up events. Not a ticketed entertainment venue.

## Venue Details
- **Capacity**: 150 people
- **Space**: White brick walls, black concrete floors, oversized industrial windows
- **Location**: 11 Sanborn Ct, Somerville, MA 02143

## Programming & Shows
This is a private event rental space, not a venue that hosts ticketed shows or concerts. Available for:

- Private parties
- Workshops
- Photoshoots
- Pop-up shops
