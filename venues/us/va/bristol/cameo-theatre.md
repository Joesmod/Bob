# Cameo Theatre

## Overview

Theatre Bristol operates the historic Cameo Theatre as part of a three-venue community theater organization serving Bristol, TN/VA and the surrounding region. Founded in 1965, Theatre Bristol is the oldest continuously-run children's theatre in northeast Tennessee, now celebrating 61 years of performances.

## History

Originally established in 1965 as the Bristol Children's Theatre by Catherine "Cathy" DeCaterina, a Juilliard-trained singer who wanted to build confidence in children through performance. The organization grew to become Theatre Bristol in 1980 to accommodate adult-themed productions. DeCaterina was instrumental in the 1983 rescue and restoration of the historic Paramount Theatre. She founded the Catherine F. DeCaterina School of the Arts in 1977. DeCaterina received numerous honors including the 2009 Mayors' Award of Distinction and Tennessee Arts Commission recognition in 2007 and 2008.
