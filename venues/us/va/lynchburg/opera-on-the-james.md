# Opera On the James

## Overview
Professional opera company in Lynchburg, Virginia, celebrating its 21st season. OPERA AMERICA Level Four Professional Company presenting fully staged opera productions. Focused on bringing live opera to Central Virginia and beyond.

## History
Founded by city leaders and opera lovers to share the power and beauty of live opera with diverse audiences in Central Virginia. Now in its 21st season, the company has grown into a Level Four Professional Company recognized by OPERA AMERICA, the national service organization for opera.
