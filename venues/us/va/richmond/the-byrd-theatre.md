# The Byrd Theatre

## Overview
Historic single-screen movie palace in Richmond's Carytown district. Built in 1928, now operated by The Byrd Theatre Foundation (501c3 nonprofit). Features ornate French Empire architecture, 1,396 seats, and a restored 1928 Wurlitzer organ that plays before Saturday night shows.

## History
Opened December 24, 1928. One of the oldest continually operating movie theaters in the US. Acquired by the nonprofit Byrd Theatre Foundation in 2001 to preserve and maintain the historic venue. Underwent major restoration work on seating and interior details throughout 2024-2025.
