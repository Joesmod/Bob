# Black Iris Social Club

## Overview
Artist-run social club, gallery, and performance space in Richmond's Arts District. 5,000+ square feet includes private bar, game room, lounges, cigar club, and rotating gallery space. Membership-based model with public First Friday exhibitions. Multi-disciplinary programming: art, performances, screenings, experimental music, social events.

## History
Originally established as Black Iris Gallery. Moved to Broad Street and evolved into collaborative workspace and laboratory. Launched as Black Iris Social Club in May 2018 to create member-supported space for art and community. Rejects high vs. low art distinctions. Focus on radical imagination and community engagement.
