# After Hours Concerts (Atlantic Union Bank After Hours at the SERVPRO Pavilion)

## Overview
Major outdoor concert venue in Doswell, VA operated by EventMakers-USA. Part of the acclaimed After Hours concert series that has been bringing top touring acts to Central Virginia for over a decade. Nominated for ACM "Best Outdoor Venue of the Year" in 2020.

## History
Produced by EventMakers-USA, a company with over 40 years in the special event industry. The After Hours Concert Series has been voted Richmond's "Best Concert Series" for eight consecutive years (2013-2020) by the Richmond Times-Dispatch. EventMakers-USA currently operates two After Hours venues:
- **SERVPRO After Hours Concerts** at The Innsbrook Pavilion (Glen Allen, VA)
- **Atlantic Union Bank After Hours** at the SERVPRO Pavilion (Doswell, VA) ← This location

Previously operated SERVPRO of Chesterfield After Hours at River City Sportsplex in Midlothian, VA.

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 35/100 — Poor Fit**
Already using ETIX, a major enterprise ticketing provider. ETIX is deeply integrated into major outdoor venue operations and not easily switchable. This is a professionally-operated concert series by a 40+ year special events company with established infrastructure. Not a good candidate for Opendate - they're at the enterprise tier with ETIX and unlikely to switch to a startup platform.

**Note**: EventMakers-USA operates multiple After Hours venues. This entry is specifically for the Doswell, VA location (SERVPRO Pavilion). The Glen Allen location (Innsbrook Pavilion) would have the same ticketing setup.
