# Lagoon

## Overview
Major amusement park in Farmington, UT. Operating since the late 1800s, Lagoon is a traditional theme park with rides, attractions, and seasonal events (including Frightmares Halloween event).

## Venue Type
Theme park / amusement park - NOT a live music or entertainment venue in the traditional sense.

## Programming & Shows
- **Type**: Theme park shows and attractions
- **Shows**: Live entertainment includes theatrical productions (Nightmare Cabaret, Hackenslash), kids shows, and seasonal Frightmares content
- **Pricing**: Park admission + add-on experiences (e.g., Seance show for $17)

Shows are included with park admission or available as add-ons. This is fundamentally different from a concert venue or ticketed entertainment space.

## Ticketing
Sells park admission tickets and season passes, not individual show tickets. Uses their own ticketing system integrated with park operations.
