## About

OperaDelaware is a professional opera company based in Wilmington, Delaware. They are the only professional opera company in Delaware and the 11th oldest opera company in the United States. Founded in 1945, the company produces bold, vibrant opera performances that "empower artists, enrich the community, entertain audiences, and encourage curiosity."

The company employs nearly 250 full- and part-time local artists and staffers throughout the season and operates on a $1.5 million annual budget. They perform at The Grand Opera House for mainstage productions and operate studios on the Wilmington Riverfront with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the Christina River.

## History

Founded in 1945 by a group of local artisans under the guidance of Chick Laird to present classic opera with the finest voices in Wilmington at affordable prices. The original philosophy was "Opera for Everyone" — a mission the company continues today.

OperaDelaware has received numerous awards and recognitions including the National Endowment for the Arts, Opera America, Governor's Award for the Arts, and National Opera Association's Service to Children's Opera award.
