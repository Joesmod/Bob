## About

Arizona Broadway Theatre is the Valley's premier dinner theatre destination, delivering high-quality musical theatre productions with table service dining. Founded by Kiel and Cassandra Klaphake in 2005, ABT enriches lives through the performing arts. 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 87-0794123).

## History

- **Fall 2004**: Groundbreaking
- **December 2005**: Grand Opening with "Anything Goes" at Peoria Sports Complex; permanent facility opened one month later
- **November 2012**: Penelope Elyse Brown Klaphake created 14-foot high by 84-foot long mural in grand lobby featuring 100 historical figures from performing arts
- **June 2018**: 100th production - "Mary Poppins"
- **January 2021**: Grand reopening after COVID-19 closure
- **November 2025**: 20th anniversary celebration

## Programming & Shows

- **Type**: Dinner theatre, concert series, murder mystery theatre
- **Format**: Musicals, concerts, specialty shows
- **Ticket Types**: 
  - Dinner & Show (includes soup/salad + entrée, show, fees, tax)
  - Performance Only (beverages available at lobby bar)
- **Arrival**: 90 min before curtain for dinner shows; 30 min for performance-only
- **Group Tickets**: 12+ ($80/person incl. entrée, iced tea/coffee, gratuity); 20+ get one complimentary leader ticket
- **Discounts**: Season subscriptions, Essential Discount (military/first responders/educators/healthcare), RUSH tickets ($25+fees for students/industry/educators/first responders)
- **Education**: HyRev Troupes (teen performance), Academy for Young Performers, Master Class Workshops
