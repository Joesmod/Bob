# Benderly-Kendall Opera House

## Overview
Intimate classical music venue operated by the Santa Cruz Foundation for the Performing Arts (SCFPA) in Patagonia, Arizona. Presents world-class chamber music, jazz, and diverse cultural performances in the scenic Santa Cruz Valley.

## History
The historic opera house was renovated and is now managed by SCFPA, which has been presenting professional performances for over 30 years. In 2010, expanded significantly to include additional performance spaces.

## Facilities
Two performance spaces managed by SCFPA at the Benderly-Kendall Opera House location in Patagonia.

## Programming & Shows
- **Series**: Benderly Concert Series, Showcase Series
- **Genres**: Chamber music (classical), jazz, folk, world music
- **2025-2026 Season highlights**: 
  - The Crux Duo (piano/clarinet French repertoire)
  - Gregory Lewis (violinist with Callisto Quartet)
  - Captain Wilson Conspiracy (jazz/improvisation)
  - Mackenzie Melemed ("Keys Across America")
  - Grupo Arizona (Mexican/Latin traditional)
- **Frequency**: Monthly concerts throughout season
- **Ticket prices**: Concert packages and single tickets available
- **Notable**: Gift certificates available, rotating international artists
