# The Helen K. Mason Performing Arts Center (Black Theatre Troupe)

## Overview
State-of-the-art theater facility operated by Black Theatre Troupe, the oldest continually operating African American theater in the Southwestern United States. The venue opened in 2013 in downtown Phoenix's historic Eastlake Park.

## History
Black Theatre Troupe founded around 1970. After decades of operation, completed a state-of-the-art facility in 2013 at 1333 E Washington St. Recognized nationally in 2013 for longevity as one of the longest continuously operating Black theatre companies in the U.S. Notable for completing August Wilson's entire 10-play Pittsburgh Cycle.
