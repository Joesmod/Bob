Upscale lounge in downtown Clarkdale, Arizona with regular live music. Features comfortable leather seating, large outdoor patio with shade and heaters, large screen TVs, and cozy booths. Known for having the best selection of beers in the Verde Valley.

## History

The 10-12 Lounge has been in the same location since 1959. The venue underwent a complete rebuild with a new interior featuring upscale furnishings that transformed it from its previous incarnation. Now operates as an upscale lounge while maintaining its historic Main Street location.
