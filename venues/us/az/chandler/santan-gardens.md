# SanTan Brewery & Distillery Tours

## Overview
Brewery and distillery tour operation in Chandler, Arizona. This is a MANUFACTURING FACILITY offering paid tours of their brewing and distilling operations, not an entertainment venue. Part of SanTan Brewing Company.

## History
SanTan Brewing Company is Arizona's largest combined brewery and distillery. Known for beers like MoonJuice and Devil's Ale, and spirits like Sacred Stave American Single Malt Whiskey. They've implemented sustainable brewing practices including a Mash Filter Press system that saves 72,000 gallons of water per year.
