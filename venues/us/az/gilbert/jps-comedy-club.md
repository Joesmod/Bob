# JP's Comedy Club

## Overview
Active comedy club in Gilbert, Arizona presenting national touring headliners and local talent. High-frequency venue with multiple shows per week including headliner weekends, showcases, and comedy competitions. Offers comedy education classes and workshops. Located at 860 E. Warner Road, Gilbert, AZ 85296.

## History
Established comedy venue in Gilbert's East Valley area. Built reputation for bringing national touring comedians to Arizona. Hosts comedy education programs teaching stand-up to aspiring comedians and civilians wanting to improve humor skills.
