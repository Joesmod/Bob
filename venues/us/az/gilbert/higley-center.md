# Higley Center for the Performing Arts

## Overview
Large performing arts center in Gilbert, AZ with two performance spaces: 1,235-seat Concert Hall and 186-seat Little Theatre. Connected by spacious lobby with floor-to-ceiling windows. Professional venue serving Phoenix metro area with diverse programming and rental opportunities.

## Facilities
**Concert Hall**: 1,235 seats, fully equipped with:
- Digital sound console controlling JBL Vertec speakers
- Bosendorfer 9.5 foot concert grand piano
- Marley floor available for dance
- Quick-connect power outlets
- Motor coach hook-up
- Scene shop for load-in/load-out

**Little Theatre**: 186 seats, intimate space

**Support facilities**: Male/female dressing rooms, spacious lobby, scene shop

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Concerts, theatrical productions, dance, family shows
- **Recent show**: "The Wizard of Oz on Ice" (March 30, 2026)
- Professional touring productions and local performances
