## About

Borderlands Theater is a professional theater company building equitable, joyful, and meaningful collaborations with the local community through innovative theater and responsive cultural programs ingrained in the heritage, narratives, and lived experiences of peoples rooted across the Sonoran Desert. After 39 years of powerful storytelling in the Southwest and 11 years of community-centered leadership, recently unveiled a new brand reflecting their transformation.

## History

- **Founded**: Approximately 1987 (39 years of operation as of 2026)
- **2015**: Current community-centered leadership era began
- **2026**: New brand launch reflecting internal transformation
- **Focus**: Latino/Chicano/Mexicano voice as core, working interactively with all voices of the U.S./Mexico border region
