## About

Intimate magic theater experience featuring world-class illusionists and magicians as seen on America's Got Talent, Penn & Teller's Fool Us, Masters of Illusion, and more. Operates multiple boutique theater locations (125+ seats each) in Phoenix, Tucson, and Mesa. The show is an up-close, interactive parlour-style magic experience lasting approximately 90 minutes with intermission. Founded by husband-and-wife team Roland Sarlot and Susan Eyed.

## History

- **2002**: Sarlot & Eyed partnership begins, creating Dance of Illusion
- **2003**: First Arizona event with escape illusions
- **2004-2005**: Touring performances, Roland becomes house magician at Miraval Resort
- **2008**: Founded during Great Recession
- **2009**: Original 30-seat boutique theater opens; won "Excellence in Magic" award
- **2010**: Featured performers at Magic Castle in Hollywood
- **2012**: Headlined national Society of American Magicians convention in Vegas
- **2013**: Sarlot & Eyed marry (1/3/13)
- **2016**: Becomes longest-running Arizona theater show
- **2020**: Surpasses 1,000th performance milestone
- **2023**: Expands to "Carnival of Illusion Theater" showcasing rotating guest magicians
