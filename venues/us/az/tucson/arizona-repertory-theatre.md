## About

University theater program run by the School of Theatre, Film & Television (TFTV) at the University of Arizona. Produces multiple theatrical productions per academic year in two venues: the Marroney Theatre and the Tornabene Theatre. Season includes both subscription-based performance season shows and non-subscription workshop productions. Mobile-only ticketing with paper-free campus policy.

## History

Part of the University of Arizona's School of Theatre, Film & Television, providing educational theater opportunities for students while presenting professional-quality productions to the Tucson community. Long-standing academic theater program.
