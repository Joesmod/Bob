# Cimarron Circuit Opera Co

## Overview
Opera company in Norman, Oklahoma with a powerful legacy. Founded by Dr. Thomas Carey and Carol Brice Carey to provide a platform for Black artists during segregation. Located upstairs in First Presbyterian Church, continues mission of supporting Black and Brown artists through opera and community programming.

## History
Created by visionary founders Dr. Thomas Carey and Carol Brice Carey during a time when Norman, Oklahoma was not yet racially integrated. Cimarron Opera was established to serve as a platform for Black artists. The company continues this legacy with commitment to providing avenues for Black and Brown artists and allies to express voices through opera. Company made strong public statement in 2020 reaffirming commitment to fight against systemic racism and injustice.
