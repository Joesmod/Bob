# Lyric Theatre of Oklahoma

## Overview
The Official State Theatre of Oklahoma. Professional musical theatre company producing Broadway shows in Oklahoma City's Plaza District. 501(c)(3) nonprofit with full season programming, educational academy, and community outreach.

## History
Lyric Theatre has been producing musicals in Oklahoma City for decades and was designated the Official State Theatre of Oklahoma. Located in the Plaza District at 1727 NW 16th St, the theatre operates multiple spaces including the main theatre (Civic Center Music Hall) and the Thelma Gaylord Academy for training.
