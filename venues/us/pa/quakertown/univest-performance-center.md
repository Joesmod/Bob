# The Univest Performance Center

## Overview
Outdoor amphitheater in Quakertown, PA, hosting the "Sounds of Summer Concert Series." Operated by Quakertown Borough Parks & Recreation Department. Located within the Park at 4th, a passive park that transforms into a lively concert venue with temporary fencing, reserved seating, food/drink vendors. Known for intimate and scenic outdoor setting.

## History
The Sounds of Summer Concert Series began in 2017 and has grown into a must-attend summer series attracting over 12,000 attendees each summer. The series holds a mission of promoting community pride, boosting tourism, stimulating economic growth, and supporting revitalization efforts to make Quakertown a destination town through music. Supported by over 90 volunteers and local vendors.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Classic rock, country, rock, yacht rock, tribute acts
- **Frequency**: Summer concert series (typically 4-8 shows per season)
- **Ticket prices**: $30-$150 depending on artist
- **2026 Lineup**: The Beach Boys (June 5), Skillet (June 12), Black Stone Cherry (July 10), Yachtley Crew (July 23)
- **Past Acts**: Pat Benatar, Blue Oyster Cult, Scotty McCreery, Blues Traveler, 38 Special, Marshall Tucker Band, Chris Janson, The Hooters (frequent)
- All concerts are rain or shine, outdoor setting
