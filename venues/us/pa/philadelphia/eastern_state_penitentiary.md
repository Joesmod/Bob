# Eastern State Penitentiary

## Overview
America's first penitentiary, now operated as a historic site and museum. National Historic Landmark offering tours, educational programming, and special events. Strong focus on criminal justice education and social impact.

## Programming & Events
- **Audio Tours**: Narrated by Steve Buscemi, included with admission
- **Guide-Led Tours**: 45-minute educator-led tours, available at scheduled times
- **Special Events**: Constitution Day, Juneteenth Festival, Halloween Nights, Wednesday Nights series
- **Educational Programs**: Justice 101 series, workshops, teacher institutes
- **Exhibits**: Rotating exhibits on justice, civil rights, mass incarceration
- **Ticket prices**: Audio Tour admission, Guide-Led Tour admission, Wednesday Nights $17 online/$20 door
- **Private Events**: Site rentals for events, film/photo rentals

## Programming Series
- **Justice 101**: Monthly programs on criminal justice topics
- **A Time for Liberty**: Year-long programming on liberty and justice
- **Finding Justice**: Civil rights movement programming
- **Scout Days**: Badge-earning programs for scouts
- **Wednesday Nights**: Summer evening events with themes ($17-20)
- **Halloween Nights**: Seasonal attraction
