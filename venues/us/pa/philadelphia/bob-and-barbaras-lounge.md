# Bob and Barbara's Lounge

## Overview
Iconic Philadelphia dive bar serving cold beer, cocktails, and live entertainment since 1969. Located in South Philly, known for its legendary Thursday night drag show (Miss Lisa Lisa), nightly live music, and authentic neighborhood vibe. Open every day 4pm-2am, 21+.

## History
Operating continuously since 1969 — over 55 years as a South Philadelphia institution. Survived decades of changes while maintaining its character as a true neighborhood bar with live music. Miss Lisa Lisa's drag show became a citywide institution.

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 50/100 — Low-Moderate Lead**

Limited fit for Opendate. While they host nightly live entertainment, only the Thursday drag shows charge admission ($8 cover + optional Eventbrite table reservations). All other events (6 days/week) are free shows that rely on bar revenue.

**Pros:**
- Established venue (55 years)
- Consistent weekly programming
- Strong local reputation
- Miss Lisa Lisa show is a citywide draw

**Cons:**
- Only 1 day/week has paid admission
- $8 door cover is low-ticket-value
- Eventbrite used only for table reservations, not primary ticketing
- Business model is bar-first, not ticket-dependent
- Free shows 6 nights/week

**Decision**: Not a priority lead. They're a dive bar with live music, not a ticketed venue. The drag show is the exception, and even that is $8 at the door with optional table reservations. Opendate would only handle a small slice of their business (Thursday tables).

**Why they're in the leads**: Likely flagged because they have Eventbrite links on their events page, but those are for optional premium seating, not core ticketing operations.
