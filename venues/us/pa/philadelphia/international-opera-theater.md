## About

International Opera Theater (IOT) is a **non-profit opera production company** (NOT a venue) based in both Philadelphia and Italy. Founded in 2003 by Karen Saillant, the company is dedicated to the transformative power of spontaneity-driven art, specifically in opera and musical theater, taking into consideration the fundamentals of commedia dell'arte and the principles of complexity, swarm, and emergence theories.

The company creates and produces **original operas**, many based on texts of Shakespeare and social justice issues. They primarily premiere works in Italy (Teatro degli Avvaloranti in Città della Pieve, Teatro Mancinelli in Orvieto, etc.) with occasional presentations in Philadelphia venues.

**Notable Productions:**
- Annual world premiere Italian opera in Italy (15+ years)
- "Shim Chung" (2019) - First opera ever set in Korea
- "Brundibar" (2003) - Holocaust memorial opera performed by children
- "JAGO" (2013) - Based on original story by Karen Saillant
- "Camille Claudel" (2013)
- "AZAIO" (2014) - For Shakespeare's 450th anniversary

## History

Founded in 2003 by soprano/director Karen Saillant. The company has produced 15+ world premiere operas in Italy, with performances also at major Philadelphia venues including the Philadelphia Museum of Art, the Kimmel Center, and the Academy of Music.

## Programming & Shows

IOT is a **producing company**, not a venue. They create original operas and stage them at rented venues in Italy and Philadelphia. Their work focuses on:
- World premiere operas
- Social justice themes
- Shakespeare adaptations
- Italian-language productions
- Cantatas for sacred music festivals
