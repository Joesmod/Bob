# CSz Philadelphia - Home of ComedySportz

## Overview
100-seat comedy theater in Center City Philadelphia's Rittenhouse area. Home to ComedySportz, Philadelphia's longest-running comedy show (30+ years). Improv comedy venue offering ticketed shows, private events, and educational workshops. Located at The Adrienne Theater. Part of CSz Worldwide network.

## History
Founded over 30 years ago, making ComedySportz Philadelphia's longest-running comedy show. Part of CSz Worldwide, a global network of comedy companies. Highest-rated comedy show in Philadelphia on Google reviews.

## Programming & Shows
- **Main show**: ComedySportz - improv comedy match format (two teams compete: New Jersey Turnpikes vs Philadelphia Whiz Wits)
- **Frequency**: Regular weekend shows plus special events
- **Format**: Fully-produced show with lights, staging, music, professional cast
- **Venue**: 100-seat ADA-accessible theater
- **Other offerings**: Private events, corporate workshops, improv education (Philadelphia School of Improv)
- **Ticket prices**: Variable with student and group discounts; ACCESS Card holders $2/ticket
- **BYOB**: No alcohol service on-site but BYOB permitted
