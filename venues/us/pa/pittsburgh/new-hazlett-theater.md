An incubator for artists and performing arts theater in Pittsburgh's North Side. Focuses on nurturing emerging artists and creating dynamic art through innovation and collaboration. Provides vital resources to creators from diverse backgrounds to enrich Pittsburgh's cultural community.

## History

Established as a platform for supporting emerging artists and experimental performance work. The theater's mission centers on listening to what artists need and helping them think about the big picture without worrying about logistics, technology, personnel, or funding constraints.
