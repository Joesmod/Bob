# Knoebels Amusement Resort

## Overview
America's largest free-admission amusement park. Family-owned and operated since opening as a local swimming hole. Features 60+ rides including Phoenix, the world's top wooden coaster. National Historic Landmark.

## Venue Details
| | |
|---|---|
| **Location** | Elysburg, PA |
| **Type** | Amusement park / Resort |
| **Model** | Free admission, pay-per-ride or ride packages |
| **Capacity** | Large-scale amusement park |

## Features
- **Rides**: 60+ attractions, classic and modern thrill rides
- **Signature Attraction**: Phoenix wooden coaster (world's top wooden coaster)
- **Pool**: 900,000 gallons of mountain stream water
- **Camping**: On-site campsites available
- **Food**: Award-winning menu including chicken & waffles
- **Awards**: USA Today 10Best, Amusement Today Golden Ticket Awards, NAPHA recognition
