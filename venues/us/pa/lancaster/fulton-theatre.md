# Fulton Theatre

## Overview
Historic professional regional theatre in Lancaster, PA. National Historic Landmark producing full seasons of musicals, comedies, and dramas on their Mainstage, plus Studio shows and Family series. Active year-round programming with subscription packages.

## History
A National Historic Landmark and one of the oldest continuously operating theaters in the United States. The Fulton has been a cornerstone of Lancaster's cultural scene for generations. As stated on their site: "The evolution of the Fulton is a fascinating reflection of the history of American theatre."

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Broadway musicals, dramas, comedies, family shows
- **Venues**: Mainstage auditorium, Ellen Arnold Groff Studio Theatre, Family Series
- **Season**: Year-round professional productions
- **Current/Upcoming**: And Then There Were None, The Shark is Broken, Hairspray, West Side Story, Jekyll & Hyde, Elf, Dear Evan Hansen
- **Frequency**: Multiple shows per month across three series
- **Ticket prices**: Variable by show and seating; senior discounts ($2), youth discounts available, "Pay What You Want" preview performances
- Professional regional theater employing directors, designers, actors, and playwrights from across the country
