## About

Blues-focused tavern in New Alexandria, PA. Operating since 1933 (90+ years!). Tagline: "Home of Blues, Booze and Big Ass Wings." Offers live music programming with weekly open mic nights. Located at the intersection of Route 119 and Route 22.

## History

**Pouring since 1933** — nearly century-old tavern with deep roots in the community.

## Opendate Fit

**Score: 25/100 — Poor Fit**

Speal's Tavern is a traditional bar/tavern with free live music and open mic nights. They generate revenue from food and beverage sales, not ticket sales. While they have consistent live music programming (which is great!), it's all free admission. The business model is incompatible with ticketing — they want people to walk in freely and buy drinks, not pay cover charges.

**Not suitable for Opendate** unless they decide to transition some shows to ticketed events (unlikely for a tavern business model).

**Recommendation**: Remove from lead pipeline. Potentially revisit if they ever launch ticketed concerts.
