# The Amphitheater at Poconos Park

## Overview
Outdoor amphitheater and events park in the Pocono Mountains region of northeastern Pennsylvania. Part of the larger Poconos Park entertainment complex that includes an RV park, festival grounds, and seasonal attractions like drive-through Christmas light displays.

## History
Poconos Park has been operating as a seasonal events venue, hosting festivals, concerts, and community events throughout the year. The venue emphasizes family-friendly programming and seasonal attractions.
