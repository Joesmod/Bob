# Kelly Center for Music, Arts, and Community

## Overview
Community arts center in Havertown, PA. Family-run nonprofit focused on giving artists a platform for creative expression through music performances, 2D and 3D art presentations, and student engagement. Available for private rentals and produces BYOB concerts.

## History
Operated by Kelly Music for Life, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. The organization is built on community engagement and arts education, bringing together people of all ages through music and arts programming.
