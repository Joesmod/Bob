# Fire in the Mountains

## Overview
Annual heavy music festival held at Red Eagle Campground on the Blackfeet Nation in Montana. Multi-day outdoor camping festival combining heavy music (metal, doom, experimental) with wilderness immersion, art, education, and adventure. Not a year-round venue — single annual event.

## History
Created to highlight the relationship between heavy music and mountain landscapes. Evolved into a "rewilding" experience that intentionally curates music embodying the primal wonder of nature. Featured in Rolling Stone Magazine and ABC News Live Prime. Partners with Firekeeper Alliance addressing teen suicide prevention in Native American communities.
