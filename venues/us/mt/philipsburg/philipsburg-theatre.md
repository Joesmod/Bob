# The Philipsburg Theatre

## Overview
The Philipsburg Theatre is a non-profit operating the historic Philipsburg Opera House Theater, presenting diverse programming including live music, theater, movies, and special events in a small Montana town. The venue is known for its exceptional acoustics and intimate setting.

## History
The Opera House Theater building dates to the late 1800s/early 1900s when it was originally built for theater. Locals recall seeing classic films like Jaws, Ghostbusters, and E.T. in the venue during the 1980s-90s. The current non-profit organization has revived the venue to offer artistic entertainment to the Philipsburg community.
