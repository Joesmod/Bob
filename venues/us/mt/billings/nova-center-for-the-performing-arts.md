## About

NOVA Center for the Performing Arts is a community theatre organization bringing live theatrical productions to Billings, Montana. The organization welcomes performers of all skill levels — from seasoned actors to first-timers — and produces a diverse season of plays and musicals throughout the year. NOVA is volunteer-driven with opportunities to perform, join production crews (sets, costumes, lighting, sound, stage management), or support through donations.

## History

NOVA has established itself as a cornerstone of Billings' performing arts community, providing quality live theatre and fostering local talent development.

## Opendate Fit

**Score: 65/100 — Moderate Prospect**

NOVA is an active community theatre with regular ticketed productions. They currently use Ludus, a popular community theatre platform. While they're a functioning venue, there are considerations for Opendate fit.

**Pros:**
- Active season with multiple productions per year
- Established ticketing infrastructure (Ludus)
- Volunteer management and donation features in use
- Community engagement through auditions and crew opportunities
- Consistent weekend performance schedule

**Considerations:**
- **Community theatre model** — volunteer-driven, smaller budgets, less commercial focus
- **Ludus is specialized** for community theatre and offers tight integration (ticketing + volunteer management + donations)
- May have lower ticket volume per show compared to professional venues
- Likely limited budget for new platform investment
- Community theatres often have strong loyalty to platforms that serve their unique needs

**Competitive Analysis:**
- Ludus is a strong competitor in the community theatre space with purpose-built features
- Switching cost includes volunteer management system migration, not just ticketing
- Would need compelling value prop beyond basic ticketing (e.g., better marketing tools, lower fees, integrated fundraising)

**Recommendation:**
- **Lower priority** compared to professional venues with higher volume
- If pursuing, emphasize cost savings and integrated marketing/fundraising features
- Would need to match or exceed Ludus functionality for volunteer coordination
