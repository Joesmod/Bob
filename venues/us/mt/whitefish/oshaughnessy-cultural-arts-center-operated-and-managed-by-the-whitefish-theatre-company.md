# O'Shaughnessy Cultural Arts Center - Whitefish Theatre Company

## Overview
Community theatre and performing arts center in Whitefish, MT. Opened in 1998, funded by the I.A. O'Shaughnessy Foundation. Long-term lease from the City of Whitefish. Hosts WTC productions, Metropolitan Opera Live in HD broadcasts, concerts, and community events. Premier arts center for the Flathead Valley region.

## History
Whitefish Theatre Company was founded in 1978, initially mounting two productions per year. By 1988, they had hired a full-time artistic director and established a building fund. The O'Shaughnessy Cultural Arts Center opened in 1998 with generous support from the I.A. O'Shaughnessy Foundation and community funding. The facility provides space for theatre, music, visual arts, and community gatherings.

## Programming & Shows
- **Theatre Productions**: 4-6 mainstage shows per season (musicals, dramas, comedies)
  - Past shows: Into The Woods, Sweeney Todd, Little Shop of Horrors, Elf The Musical
- **Metropolitan Opera Live in HD**: 8 performances per season (co-hosted with Whitefish PAC)
- **Summer Concert Series**: 3-show season, $40 per show or $105 season pass
- **Special Events**: Adult Prom, community events, fundraisers
- **Youth Programs**: Summer camps in music, dance, and theatre
- **Visual Arts**: Lobby displays, art shows, photography exhibits
