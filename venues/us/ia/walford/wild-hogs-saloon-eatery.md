## About

Wild Hogs Saloon & Eatery is a family-friendly restaurant and live music venue located just outside Cedar Rapids in Walford, IA. The venue features a large dance floor, stage, full-service restaurant, and fully stocked bar. Known as "the premier events venue in the Cedar Rapids area," Wild Hogs hosts live music Thursday through Sunday along with private events like birthday parties, corporate events, anniversaries, and fundraisers.

## History

Established as a destination venue for live music and dining in the Cedar Rapids area. Built reputation on bringing national touring acts to eastern Iowa along with regular regional live music.
