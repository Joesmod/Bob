## About

Des Moines Metro Opera (DMMO) is a nationally acclaimed professional opera company performing in a unique 467-seat theater in Indianola, Iowa. Every seat in the theater is closer to center stage than the first row at the Metropolitan Opera, creating an intimate, immersive experience. DMMO is known for adventurous repertory and has become "one of America's boldest smaller companies" (The New Yorker, 2024) and "the Midwest's must-see summer opera festival" (Dallas Morning News, 2025).

## History

DMMO has built a reputation as one of the country's most successful smaller opera companies doing adventurous repertory. The organization operates out of the Lauridsen Opera Center and has earned national critical acclaim from The New York Times, The New Yorker, and other major publications.
