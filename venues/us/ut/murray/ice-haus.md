# Ice Haus

## Overview
Lively gastropub and bar in Murray serving American comfort food with German-inspired flavors. Features year-round heated Biergarten, live music on weekends, and extensive beer selection. More of an entertainment venue with free live music rather than a traditional ticketed venue.

## Venue Details
- **Address**: Murray, UT (exact address not listed on website)
- **Type**: German-style American gastropub with live entertainment
- **Capacity**: Large seating areas, communal tables, big beer garden

## Programming & Shows
- **Live Music**: Friday and Saturday nights (8pm-12:30am)
- **Drag Brunch**: Monthly ticketed special events (e.g. Lucky Pop Drag Brunch)
- **Weekly Events**:
  - Monday: Trivia Night (7:30pm) + Open Mic (9:30pm, Utah Comedy)
  - Tuesday: Breaking Bingo (7:30pm)
  - Wednesday: Poker Night (7:30pm, Grinders Poker Tour)
  - Thursday: Karaoke (8pm, ZimZam Entertainment)
  - Sunday: Karaoke (8pm) + Weekend Brunch (10am-2pm)
- **Special Events**: Holiday celebrations (St. Patrick's Day, Easter, Cinco de Mayo, Mother's Day, Memorial Day)
- **Ticket prices**: Most events free; Drag Brunch $19 online / $24 door

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 82/100 — Weak Fit**

**Not a good match for Opendate.** Primarily a gastropub/bar with free entertainment. Ticketing is minimal (occasional drag brunch or special events only). Revenue model is food/beverage sales, not ticket sales. 

While they use Ticket Leap for occasional special events, the volume is too low to justify a dedicated ticketing platform. Better suited for their current setup (minimal ticketing needs, free entertainment model).

**Recommendation**: Skip outreach. Not a ticketing-focused venue.
