# Kensington Theatre

## Overview
Community theater company in South Jordan, UT producing family-friendly musical theater and plays. Founded in 2007, now located in partnership with Early Light Academy charter school. Focus on educational theater opportunities for children and adults with strong emphasis on family values and community building.

## History
Originally founded as Daybreak Community Theatre in 2007 with a production of The Wizard of Oz involving over 200 community members. Renamed South Jordan Community Theatre to reflect broader community reach. In 2014, entered partnership with Early Light Academy and rebranded as Kensington Theatre Company (inspired by Kensington Gardens from Peter Pan). Now operates from Early Light Academy's theater facility at 11709 S. Vadania Dr.
