# Utah Festival Opera & Musical Theatre

## Overview
Professional summer festival presenting grand opera and musical theatre in Logan, UT. Multiple performance venues including Ellen Eccles Theatre, Utah Theatre, and Dansante Building. Runs seasonal programming with focus on world-class musical productions. Founded by Michael Ballam.

## History
Established festival company presenting professional-level opera and musical theatre. Has grown to become a significant cultural institution in northern Utah with multiple venues and educational programs.

**Pros:**
- Professional operation with box office director
- Multiple venues and active programming

**Cons:**
- Already using ArtTix and Salt Lake County Arts partnership
- Nonprofit festival model (budget constraints)
- Seasonal operation (not year-round revenue)
- Would require breaking existing partnerships
- Strong focus on series packages and phone discounts

**Approach:**
Would need to demonstrate significant advantages over current ArtTix setup. Best angle might be multi-venue management across their three performance spaces, better mobile experience, or enhanced series/subscription management.
