# Alliance Theater - Utah Arts Alliance

## Overview
**VENUE TYPE: Primarily Rental Venue with Some Hosted Shows**

100-seat main theater and 50-seat Black Box (Sister Dottie S Dixon Memorial Blackbox Theater) operated by Utah Arts Alliance nonprofit. Located in Trolley Square, Salt Lake City. Primarily rents to local artists/companies but also produces some cultural events.

## Facility Details
- **Main Theater**: 100 seats
- **Black Box**: 50 seats (flexible layout)
- **Location**: Trolley Square, 600 East (west side), Salt Lake City, UT
- **Parking**: Free underground parking
- **Access**: Elevator and stairs to plaza level

## Equipment & Amenities
- Professional theatrical lighting and sound in both spaces
- Optional livestream capability (main theater)
- World-class green room with full-length mirrors, lit vanities, private bathroom
- Optional box office support
- Optional bar service

## Programming
**Partnership Model**: Alliance supports local artists to produce their own events. Past events include:
- Community theater productions
- Plays, musicals, cabaret shows
- Comedy showcases
- Dance performances and recitals
- Music concerts and voice recitals
- Drag shows, fashion shows, magic shows
- Art shows, experimental theater
- Classes, auditions, rehearsals
- Self-help seminars

## Rental Services
- Box office and ticketing (can be client's or venue's)
- Professional crew and technical support
- Livestreaming services
- Bar/concessions
- Event planning assistance
