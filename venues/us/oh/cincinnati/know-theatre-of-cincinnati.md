# Know Theatre of Cincinnati

## Overview

Contemporary 99-seat black box theater in Cincinnati's historic Over-the-Rhine neighborhood. Know Theatre is in its 28th season, focused on building belonging through bold, experimental theater. The venue includes the Underground, a full-bar cabaret space for second-stage programming and special events.

## History

Know Theatre has been producing contemporary, experimental theater in Cincinnati for 28 seasons. The organization relocated to the David C. Herriman Center for the Performing Arts in Over-the-Rhine, featuring a 99-seat MainStage theater on the second floor and the Underground cabaret/bar space in the lobby. The theater has elevator access for mobility needs.

Know Theatre hosts the annual Cincinnati Fringe Festival, providing a safe space for experimentation and artistic risk.

## Opendate Fit

**Score: 55/100 — Long Shot**

Know Theatre is a professional contemporary theater company, not a volunteer-run community group, which is in their favor. They have dedicated box office hours, sell subscriptions, and produce 3-4 mainstage productions plus special events annually. However, at 99 seats, their capacity is very limited, constraining revenue. As an experimental/contemporary theater focused on "bold" and artistic work, they're mission-driven rather than commercially focused. They also host a Fringe Festival (very low/free admission model). The emphasis on accessibility (low/no cost tickets) and belonging suggests they won't prioritize maximizing ticket revenue. Possible prospect but would need significant convincing of value proposition.
