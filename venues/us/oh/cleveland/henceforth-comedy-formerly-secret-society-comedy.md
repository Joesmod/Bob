## About

Henceforth Comedy (rebranded from Secret Society Comedy in 2026) is a Cleveland-based comedy production company that books and produces stand-up comedy shows at multiple venues across the Cleveland area. Rather than operating a single dedicated venue, they curate and promote shows at partner locations including Winchester Music Tavern, Mahall's Apartment, and Stella's Art Gallery. They also operate an open mic at Pins & Needles in Lakewood every Tuesday.

**Tagline:** "Live Comedy | Curated Acts | Unforgettable Nights"

## History

Formerly known as Secret Society Comedy, the company rebranded to Henceforth Comedy in 2026 while maintaining the same curators and vision. They've built a reputation for bringing quality stand-up comedy to Cleveland's indie venues and bars, creating a circuit of comedy shows across the city.

## Opendate Fit

**Score: 65/100 — Interesting But Non-Traditional Model**

**Unique Characteristics:**
- **Not a venue, but a production company/promoter** - books shows at multiple venues
- Multi-venue model creates complexity (different capacities, layouts, bar arrangements)
- Currently using Eventbrite (easily switchable platform)
- Active show calendar with 4-6 shows per week
- Growing brand recognition in Cleveland comedy scene

**Strengths:**
- High volume of shows across multiple venues
- Already using third-party ticketing (Eventbrite) - switching is feasible
- Independent operation (not chain-owned)
- Strong community engagement (open mics, performer development)
- Rebranding shows growth/expansion mindset

**Challenges:**
- Multi-venue model means different venue owners/agreements
- Ticketing needs to work across different venue capacities/setups
- Each venue partnership may have different terms
- Not a single venue operator - lower revenue per client

**Switch Likelihood:** Medium-High. As a production company using Eventbrite, they could benefit from Opendate if:
- Platform handles multi-venue management elegantly
- Pricing is competitive with Eventbrite
- Better reporting across all venues
- Can integrate with venue partners' systems

**Strategic Note:** This is a "promoter/producer" not a traditional venue. If Opendate serves promoters who work across multiple spaces, Henceforth is a good prospect. If Opendate is venue-focused, this might not be the right fit.
