# Clifton Opera House

## Overview
Historic 1893 opera house in the village of Clifton, OH. Intimate venue hosting live music concerts every Friday and Saturday night during operating season, plus available for private events. Designed by noted architect Charles Cregar.

## History
**Built**: 1893
**Architect**: Charles Cregar (Springfield architect known for municipal buildings and churches)

The Opera House was designed by Charles Cregar with many of his favorite architectural elements, though originally much grander. The tower on the front of the building has been removed due to damage and deterioration.

**Historical Use**:
- Minstrel shows
- Town offices
- After-school sports
- Community meetings
- Currently: Live concerts and events

**Recognition**: Featured in Summer/Fall 2019 issue of Ohio Humanities Pathways magazine.

## Programming & Shows
- **Regular Programming**: Live concerts every Friday and Saturday night during operating season
- **Types**: Variety of musical offerings and entertainment
- **Private Events Available**: Performances, recitals, weddings & receptions, corporate meetings, class reunions, art exhibits, music festivals, theater presentations, movies, workshops, and more
