## About

Skullys Music-Diner is a Columbus institution located in the Short North Arts District. Originally opened as a bar and grill on Ohio State's campus in 1994, they moved to a cafe at Morse and High Street before settling into their current Short North location. Celebrating 20+ years in the Short North as of 2021.

Multi-award-winning venue known for their signature "Ladies 80s" night, as well as hosting rock, hip-hop, and electronic acts. The venue features a main floor, stage, and private lofted balcony room that can accommodate up to 60 people for private events.

**Notable:** Named "The Best Diner in America" by Travelog.us in 2015. Kitchen closed in March 2020 due to COVID shutdowns and has not reopened.

## Programming & Shows

- **Genres:** Rock, hip-hop, electronic/EDM, indie
- **Frequency:** Multiple shows per week
- **Ticket prices:** Varies by act; $5 surcharge for under-21 attendees
- **Notable acts:** Black Keys, Tokyo Police Club, plus touring and local acts
- **Events:** Weekly "Ladies 80s" dance party (signature event), DJ nights, live bands

**Stage Specs:**
- 23' wide x 13.5' deep x 3' high
- Professional sound system (Midas M32 console, QSC line array)
- Full lighting rig with moving heads, strobes, lasers
- 13' projection screen behind stage (HDMI/VGA)
