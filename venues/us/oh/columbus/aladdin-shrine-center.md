# Aladdin Shrine Center

## Overview
Fraternal organization and event center in Grove City (Columbus area), OH. Hosts Shriners events, circus performances, and member gatherings. The organization operates ASHAC (Aladdin Shriners Hospital Association for Children), a 501(c)3 charity supporting children's medical care.

## History
Aladdin Shriners is a Masonic fraternal organization that operates a multi-purpose event facility. The center serves as headquarters for member activities and hosts public events including their annual Shrine Circus.
