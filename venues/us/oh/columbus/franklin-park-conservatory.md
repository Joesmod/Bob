# Franklin Park Conservatory and Botanical Gardens

## Overview
Botanical garden and conservatory in Columbus, OH. Admission-based attraction featuring tropical plants, seasonal exhibitions (orchids, butterflies), gardens, and a glass art hot shop. Also operates as a wedding and event rental venue.

## History
Historic conservatory with Victorian-era glass architecture. Long-standing botanical institution in Columbus with educational mission and garden collections.
