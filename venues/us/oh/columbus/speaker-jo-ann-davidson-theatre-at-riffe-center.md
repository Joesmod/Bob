# Speaker Jo Ann Davidson Theatre At Riffe Center

## Overview
Part of the Riffe Center Theatre Complex in downtown Columbus, Ohio. The Riffe Center (opened 1989) houses the Speaker Jo Ann Davidson Theatre plus three studio theatres, all managed by CAPA (Columbus Association for the Performing Arts) since 1994. Located in the Vern Riffe Center for Government and the Arts, a state government building.

## History
The Vern Riffe Center for Government and the Arts opened in 1989. CAPA took over management of the theater complex in 1994. The complex includes the main Davidson Theatre, Studio One, Studio Two, and Studio Three, plus scenery and costume shops.
