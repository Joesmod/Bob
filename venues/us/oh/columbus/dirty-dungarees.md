# Dirty Dungarees

## Overview

Unique laundromat/dive bar hybrid in Columbus' Old North district. Hosts underground punk, hardcore, and indie shows in a raw back room. Operating since 1978.

## History

Opened in 1978 as a neighborhood laundromat. Evolved into a unique "laundrobar" combining full-service laundry, dive bar, and live music venue. Located at 2584 N High St, just north of OSU campus in the Old North University District.
