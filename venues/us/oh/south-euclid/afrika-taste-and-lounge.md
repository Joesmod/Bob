# Afrika Taste and Lounge

## Overview
African restaurant and nightlife lounge in South Euclid, Ohio. Serves African cuisine (Nigerian/West African food) with DJ nights featuring Afrobeats, Hip-Hop, Reggae, and Soca music.

## Venue Type
Restaurant and lounge with weekly events (Wing Wednesdays, Suya Sundays) and DJ-hosted nightlife.

## Programming
- **Food**: Nigerian specialties (Jollof Rice, Egusi, Red Snapper, Afro Tacos)
- **Nightlife**: DJ sets (Afrobeats, Hip-Hop, Reggae, Soca)
- **Events**: Wing Wednesdays, Suya Sundays, Brunch, Catering
