# BFT Huntington Beach

## Overview
Body Fit Training (BFT) is a fitness studio franchise offering group strength training classes. NOT a ticketed event venue.

## What They Do
- Science-backed progressive training programs
- Group fitness classes with heart rate monitoring
- Personal training in a group environment
- Multiple coaches per session

## Business Model
Membership-based fitness studio. Members book classes through the BFT booking app, but this is gym membership enrollment, not event ticketing.
