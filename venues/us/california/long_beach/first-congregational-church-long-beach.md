# First Congregational Church of Long Beach

## Overview
Progressive United Church of Christ congregation in downtown Long Beach. Occasionally hosts benefit concerts but primarily a place of worship. NOT a regular ticketed event venue.

## What They Do
- Sunday worship services at 10am
- Adult education programs
- Community events and social justice work
- Occasional benefit concerts (UCO fundraiser with Ken Medema on March 14, 2026)

## Programming
- **Primary Function**: Church services and religious programming
- **Occasional Events**: Benefit concerts for charitable causes (e.g., Urban Community Outreach)
- **Frequency**: Very occasional ticketed events, not regular programming

## Contact
Located in downtown Long Beach with free validated parking at Civic Center parking structure on Broadway between Chestnut and Cedar.
