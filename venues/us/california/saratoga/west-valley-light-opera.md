# West Valley Light Opera Association

## Overview
**STATUS: CLOSED — November 2025**

Community theater company that operated in Saratoga, CA (South Bay) for 60 years. Specialized in musical theater productions. Shut down in November 2025 after their 60th Diamond Celebration final production.

## History
Founded in 1964 as West Valley Light Opera, the company served the South Bay community for six decades presenting musical theater. Final performance was the 60th Diamond Celebration in November 2025.

## Closure Notice
From their website:
> "The landscape of community theatre has changed significantly. Despite our best efforts, the combination of sharply increasing production costs and decreasing attendance has made it impossible to sustain the high-quality performances you've come to expect."
