# Light Opera Theatre of Sacramento (LOTS)

## Overview
Community theater company specializing in Gilbert & Sullivan light opera, performing at 24th Street Theater in Sacramento. Founded 1982, presents 2-3 productions per season. All-volunteer organization with open auditions. Active and producing shows for the 2025-2026 season.

## History
Founded in 1982 by Mike and Debbie Baad and Dolores Allen, originally as "St. Mary's Players" with the St. Mary's Church Choir. First production was "H.M.S. Pinafore" which received rave reviews. Renamed Light Opera Theatre of Sacramento in its fourth year. Moved to 24th Street Theater in 1988, where they continue to perform.
