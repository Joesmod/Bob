# SAFE Credit Union Performing Arts Center
(formerly Community Center Theater / Sacramento Community Center Theater)

## Overview
Major municipal performing arts venue in downtown Sacramento with 3,000+ seat capacity (Performing Arts Center) and historic 3,849-seat Memorial Auditorium. Part of the SAFE Credit Union Convention & Performing Arts District. Recently renovated, city-owned and operated. Hosts Broadway tours, concerts, and major productions.

## History
Built in 1927 as Sacramento Memorial Auditorium. The entire complex was rebranded as SAFE Credit Union Convention & Performing Arts District following a 25-year naming rights deal with SAFE Credit Union. The Performing Arts Center underwent recent renovation focusing on accessibility and audience comfort.
