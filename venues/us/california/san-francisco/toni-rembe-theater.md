# Toni Rembe Theater (American Conservatory Theater)

## Overview
Historic 400+ seat theater in San Francisco's Union Square district, home to American Conservatory Theater (A.C.T.). Built after the 1906 earthquake and immediately hailed as the "perfect playhouse," the Toni Rembe Theater (formerly Geary Theater) has been A.C.T.'s home since the company's founding. Professional regional theater presenting contemporary and classic works.

## History
Built in 1906 after the catastrophic earthquake and fires, the theater rose from the rubble to become one of San Francisco's most important cultural venues. Underwent seismic retrofit after 1989 Loma Prieta earthquake. Renamed Toni Rembe Theater (previously known as Geary Theater).
