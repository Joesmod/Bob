# Brick And Mortar Music Hall

## Overview
Brick & Mortar Music Hall is one of the few remaining independently owned music venues in San Francisco, nestled in the heart of the vibrant Mission District. Opening its doors in March 2011, the venue has become a staple of the city's live music scene, hosting more than 300 events annually.

**Location:** 1710 Mission Street, San Francisco, CA 94103
