# Kanbar Performing Arts Center

## Overview
Performance and rehearsal facility serving as the home base for San Francisco Girls Chorus (SFGC). Located in San Francisco, hosts rehearsals, open rehearsals for prospective families, and occasional performances. Primary function is as a training and rehearsal space for SFGC's multiple ensemble levels.

## History
Serves as the performance arts center for San Francisco Girls Chorus, which was founded in 1978. The facility provides dedicated space for one of the nation's premier youth choral organizations.

## Programming & Shows
- **Primary use**: Rehearsal and training space for San Francisco Girls Chorus
- **Events**: Open rehearsals for prospective families (Prep Chorus, Ensemble B, Concert Group, etc.)
- **Ensembles**: Multiple levels from Prep Chorus through Premier Ensemble
- **Performance venues**: SFGC performs at many venues around the Bay Area (War Memorial Opera House, Herbst Theater, SFJAZZ, SF Symphony, Scottish Rite Center, etc.) rather than primarily at Kanbar
