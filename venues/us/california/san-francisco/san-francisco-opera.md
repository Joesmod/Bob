# San Francisco Opera

## Overview
One of America's premier opera companies, founded 1923. Performs at the 3,146-seat War Memorial Opera House and the 299-seat Dianne and Tad Taube Atrium Theater in the Diane B. Wilsey Center for Opera. Presents 40+ performances of 6+ operas annually. Major professional arts organization with extensive education and training programs including the renowned Merola Opera Program and Opera Center.

## History
Founded in 1923 under the direction of Gaetano Merola. The War Memorial Opera House opened October 15, 1932 with a performance of Puccini's Tosca under Merola's baton, broadcast nationally. California Historical Landmark. Underwent seismic retrofit in 1996 following the Loma Prieta earthquake. Over 100 years of continuous operation, making it one of the oldest and most prestigious opera companies in the United States.
