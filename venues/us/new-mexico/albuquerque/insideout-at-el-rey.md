# Inside Out at El Rey Theater

## Overview
Inside Out is the smaller side venue/room at the Historic El Rey Theater complex in downtown Albuquerque. Hosts indie, punk, metal, and underground shows in a more intimate setting than the main El Rey Theater room. Part of the El Rey Live operation.

## History
Part of the Historic El Rey Theater building at 622 Central Avenue SW. The El Rey Theater building dates back to 1941 (architect Joseph B. Burwinkle, Lembke Construction). Has served as grocery store, saloon, shops, movie theater, and since 1981 has been used exclusively as a concert venue. Inside Out functions as the smaller room/club space for emerging and underground acts.
