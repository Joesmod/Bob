# Patricia Reser Center for the Arts

## Overview
Modern performing arts center in Beaverton, OR (Portland metro area). Opened in 2022 as a cultural hub serving the diverse Beaverton community. Features multiple performance spaces with over 220,000 sq ft of event space. City of Beaverton-owned facility operated by Cultural Affairs Department. Focuses on presentations, resident companies, and arts education.

## History
The Patricia Reser Center for the Arts (The Reser) opened its doors in 2022 after years of community advocacy and public-private partnership. Named for philanthropist Pat Reser, who provided significant founding support. Built by decision of Beaverton city leadership to serve as the cultural heart of the growing Portland-area community. Represents a $100M+ investment in arts infrastructure for Washington County.
