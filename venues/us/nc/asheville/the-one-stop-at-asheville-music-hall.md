# The One Stop at Asheville Music Hall

## Overview
Multi-venue live music complex in downtown Asheville, NC. Consists of Asheville Music Hall (main venue) and The One Stop (upstairs 21+ lounge). Combined operation provides two-floor events, kitchen, and extensive live music programming. Part of larger venue network including French Broad River Brewery stages.

## History
Established live music destination in Asheville's thriving music scene. Located in the heart of downtown at two adjacent addresses on Patton Avenue and College Street. Part of the Rockhouse Partners / Etix venue family.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Jam bands, tribute acts, rock, reggae, indie, electronic, country
