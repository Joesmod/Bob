# New Belgium Brewing - Asheville

## Overview
National brewery brand with Asheville, NC location (also Fort Collins, CO). Not a ticketed performance venue - primarily a brewery taproom and tour facility with free promotional events.

## Programming & Shows
- **Tour de Fat** - Annual free bicycle festival with live music (August in Fort Collins, October in Asheville)
  - Free admission
  - Bands included (e.g., Polyrhythmics, Murder by Death)
  - Bicycle parade, games, contests
  - Beer sales benefit local bike nonprofits
- Food trucks and casual music at taproom locations
