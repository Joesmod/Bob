# Bent Creek Lodge Bed & Breakfast

## Overview

Bed & breakfast and wedding venue in Arden, NC (near Asheville). 10 guest rooms, event space for intimate weddings and elopements. NOT a ticketed public events venue.

## About

Bent Creek Lodge is a hospitality business offering overnight accommodations and private event rental for weddings, elopements, corporate retreats, and group gatherings. Wedding packages range from $1,495 to $11,995 and include ceremony, accommodations, breakfast, and catering.

No public ticketed events or performances.
