# Thalian Association Community Theatre

## Overview
Historic community theater organization founded in 1788 in Wilmington, NC. One of the oldest continuously operating theater companies in America. Provides performances, youth education, and manages the Hannah Block Historic USO/Community Arts Center for the City of Wilmington.

## Venue Details
- **Founded**: 1788
- **Location**: Wilmington, NC (performances at Historic Thalian Hall + Hannah Block Historic USO/Community Arts Center)
- **Type**: Community theater (non-profit)
- **Youth Programs**: Youth Academy, Youth Theatre, Creative Arts Camp

## History
Founded in 1788 to bring theatrical performance and youth education to the Cape Fear region - making it one of America's oldest theater companies. Provides cultural foundation for Southeastern North Carolina through arts education, performing and visual arts programming. Manages the Hannah Block Historic USO/Community Arts Center for the City of Wilmington.
