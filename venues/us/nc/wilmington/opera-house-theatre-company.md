## History

Opera House Theatre Company (OHTC) was founded in 1985 with the mission of enriching Wilmington's arts scene through live theater. For 41 years, the company has nurtured local talent while bringing professional theater to the community.

The company blends professional guest artists with local performers, providing a platform for actors, musicians, and technicians to grow alongside industry professionals. OHTC has produced dozens of major musicals and plays, from classic Broadway shows to contemporary works.

Founders Lou Criscuolo and Mary James Morgan established a legacy of sustaining a vibrant arts community in Wilmington through accessible, high-quality theater.
