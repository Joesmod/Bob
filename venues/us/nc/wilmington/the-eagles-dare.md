## About

Bar and music venue in downtown Wilmington featuring a beer garden, craft cocktails, and live music programming. Home to "Free Concert Friday" (FCF) — a 14-week free summer concert series running every Friday from May through July. Also hosts ticketed concerts and private events in the Wanda Jackson Room, a 2,200 sq ft event space with full bar and multiple rental options. Venue features outdoor beer garden/parking lot space for large concerts, with indoor backup for inclement weather.

## History

Established venue in downtown Wilmington's revitalized arts district. Known for community-focused programming including charity concert series supporting local non-profits (United Way Cape Fear Area, Marine Raider Foundation, Cape Fear Literacy Council).
