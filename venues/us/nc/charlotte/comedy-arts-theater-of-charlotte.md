# Comedy Arts Theater of Charlotte (CATCh)

## Overview
Improv comedy theater in Charlotte, NC offering ticketed shows and improv classes. Intimate theater space at 4128 South Blvd with active performance calendar featuring both house troupes and guest performers.

## History
Established comedy arts theater focused on creating an open, inclusive space for improvisers and improv teams in Charlotte.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genre**: Improv comedy (short-form and long-form)
- **Notable Shows**:
  - **Hoover Improv** - Friday shows with rotating guest performers
  - **Swell Music Improv** - Saturday 8:30pm - Charlotte's premier music improv troupe with guitar/piano accompaniment
  - **Dear Diary & Friends** - Saturday 7:30pm - Audience-interactive show using photos and diary submissions
- **Frequency**: Multiple shows per week (Fridays and Saturdays regularly scheduled)
- **Ticket Prices**: $10 typical for shows
- **Classes**: 
  - "Unlocking the Self: Improv Skills for Life" (6-week beginner course, Tuesdays 7pm)
  - No experience required, focuses on life skills through improv
