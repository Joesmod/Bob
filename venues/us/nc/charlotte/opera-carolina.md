# Opera Carolina

## Overview

Professional opera company celebrating 76 years in Charlotte, NC. Performs in Charlotte's premiere venues — the Belk and Knight Theaters in Center City. One of the largest regional opera companies in the United States, founded in 1948 as Charlotte Opera.

## History

Founded in 1948 by Dorothy Allen and Herbert Carson with a budget of $150. Premiered with "Rosalinda" (Die Fledermaus) at Piedmont Junior High School. Grew from all-volunteer troupe to fully professional company.

- **1955**: Moved to 2,400-seat Ovens Auditorium
- **1989**: Rebranded from Charlotte Opera to Opera Carolina to serve broader region
- **1992**: Moved to Belk Theater
- **2025-2026**: Celebrating 76th season
