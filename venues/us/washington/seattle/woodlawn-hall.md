# Woodlawn Hall

## Overview
Event space operated by Abbey Arts Presents (same nonprofit that runs Fremont Abbey Arts Center). Located in the Greenlake neighborhood near the Greenlake library. Used for live music concerts, poetry events, and community gatherings. All-ages venue with mostly seated format.

## History
Newer venue in Abbey Arts' portfolio. Added as an additional performance space for Abbey Arts programming alongside Fremont Abbey (their main venue since 2005), Ballard Homestead (partnership since 2014), and other Seattle venues.

## Relationship to Abbey Arts
Woodlawn Hall is one of several venues curated by Abbey Arts Presents, the nonprofit 501(c)3 based at Fremont Abbey Arts Center. Abbey Arts operates:
1. **Fremont Abbey Arts Center** (main venue/headquarters)
2. **Ballard Homestead** (partnership venue)
3. **Woodlawn Hall** (this venue)
4. **St. Mark's Cathedral/Bloedel Hall** (partnership for select events)
5. **University Heights Center Auditorium** (partnership for select events)
