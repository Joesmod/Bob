# Venue 906

## Overview
Multi-purpose 5,000+ sq ft event venue in Kingsford, Michigan. Accommodates up to 400 guests for weddings, concerts, drag shows, family events, and community gatherings. Features two luxury suites, on-site catering, and flexible event space. Active programming includes ticketed concerts, drag shows, festivals, competitions, and fundraisers.

## History
Modern event venue established to serve the Kingsford/Upper Peninsula community with a diverse mix of entertainment and event hosting.

- **Events**: Drag shows, live music festivals, kids' events, competitions (puzzles, chili cook-offs), fundraisers, weddings, private events

- **Capacity**: Up to 400 guests
