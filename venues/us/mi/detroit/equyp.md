# EQUYP

## Overview
Multi-functional studio space in Detroit for photography, screen printing, and private events. Flexible creative space designed for hourly rental with retro flooring and string lights. NOT a ticketed entertainment venue.

## Venue Type
Studio space rental for creative work and private events. Available by the hour or through membership.

## Amenities
- Bluetooth sound system
- 50" Smart TV
- Hanging string lights
- 32 folding chairs
- 5 rectangular tables (24"x72")
- 2 metal bar stools
- 2 wireless microphones
- 2 restrooms
- Rolling desks

## Programming & Events
**Private rentals only** - no ticketed public shows. Events hosted include:
- Photo shoots
- Pop-up shops
- Board game nights
- Engagement parties
- Karaoke & networking events
- Private dinner parties
- Workshops and classes (screen printing, "Sip N' Print" events)
- Team meetings
- Book signings

## Pricing
- **Hourly rate**: $100/hour (Friday-Sunday)
- **Cleaning deposit** required
- 50% deposit to reserve
- BYOB for private events
- No required caterer

## Partnership
Hosts a program with College for Creative Studies for custom t-shirt printing events (summer seasonal).
