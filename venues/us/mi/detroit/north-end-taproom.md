# North End Taproom

## Overview
Self-pour taproom in Royal Oak, MI featuring 40 taps of craft beer, cider, seltzer, and wine. Pay-by-the-ounce system using smart wristbands. Full kitchen and dog-friendly patio. NOT a ticketed entertainment venue.

## History
Opened August 2024 by Jon and Katheryn, Michigan-born owners who helped change Michigan law to legalize self-pour bars. After nearly two years of advocacy, they successfully helped pass Senate Bill 656 in Spring 2023, making self-pour legal in Michigan.
