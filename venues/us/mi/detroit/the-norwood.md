# The Norwood

## Overview
Detroit's newest industrial event venue located at 6531 Woodward Ave in New Center. Features 7,000 sq ft across 3 floors including downstairs bar lounge (Intermission). Restored historic theater building originally built in 1915, now operating as a private event rental space.

## History
- **1915**: Original Norwood Theatre designed by William S. Jay & Co.
- **1941**: Demolished for road widening
- **1941-1949**: New Art Deco Norwood Theatre built on site, later closed
- **Recent**: Restored as industrial event venue

## Venue Details
- **Square Footage**: 7,000 sq ft
- **Floors**: 3 levels
- **Downstairs**: Intermission bar lounge
- **Style**: Industrial event space, Art Deco heritage
- **Sister Venue**: The Eastern (also private events)
