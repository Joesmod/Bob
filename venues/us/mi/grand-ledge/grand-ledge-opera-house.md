## History

Built in 1884, the Grand Ledge Opera House stands as a historic landmark in the heart of downtown Grand Ledge. Beautifully restored and maintained, it has been preserved as a cultural centerpiece for the community. Set against the scenic backdrop of the Grand River with landscaped grounds.
