# The Extra Event Center | By: Pro Sports Extra

## Overview
Live entertainment venue in downtown L'Anse, Michigan that launched in November 2023. Brings national touring artists, country music stars, and top comedians to the Upper Peninsula. Operated by Pro Sports Extra with support from Friends of The Extra, a nonprofit dedicated to bringing world-class entertainment to rural Michigan.

## History
Since November 2023, The Extra has transformed L'Anse into a must-stop destination for national touring acts. The venue fills a cultural gap in the U.P., bringing performances from America's Got Talent finalists, The Voice stars, and arena-selling comedians to a region that rarely gets touring talent.

## Facilities
- Intimate venue with "every seat is great" layout
- Full bar (alcohol available on show nights via Friends of The Extra nonprofit)
- BYOB policy allowed for guests
- Weekly members-only club events (Football Sundays, Kill Tony Tuesdays)
