# Stage 12 at Brookshire Brothers

## Overview
State-of-the-art outdoor amphitheater event venue attached to a Brookshire Brothers grocery store in College Station, TX. Features a 14-foot HD screen for sports and movies, open-air seating, full bar service, and live music programming. Located near Kyle Field (Texas A&M stadium). Unique hybrid venue combining grocery retail with entertainment.

## History
Opened to demonstrate that Brookshire Brothers is "more than a grocery store." Created as a community gathering space for live music, sports viewing, and social events. Became the Official Aggieland Watch Party location for Texas A&M Athletics.
