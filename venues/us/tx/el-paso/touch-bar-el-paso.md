# Touch Bar El Paso

## Overview
Premier LGBTQ+ nightclub and drag show bar in El Paso, TX, known as the "#StageOfTexas" and "Southwest's premier show bar." Features state-of-the-art lighting, sound, and special effects for high-production drag shows featuring RuPaul's Drag Race and Dragula stars. Open 7 days a week with full kitchen, bar, and outdoor patio. All-inclusive venue welcoming "every gender, every race, every orientation."

## History
- **August 2014**: Opened in downtown El Paso with vision of providing upscale, safe alternative for nightlife entertainment
- **Spring 2017**: Moved to larger eastside venue with bigger stage to accommodate more ambitious events
- **January 2021**: Final move to current location ("Touch 3.0") after surviving pandemic
- **Current**: Established as one of the most renowned drag venues across the US

Touch 3.0 features full kitchen, large patio with full-service bar, and maintains signature state-of-the-art production values.
