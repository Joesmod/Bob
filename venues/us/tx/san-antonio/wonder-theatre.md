## About

Nonprofit musical theater company located in the Wonderland of the Americas mall. Produces musicals and offers year-round performing arts education through Wonder Theatre Academy for ages 5-17.

## History

Professional musical theater company focused on engaging the San Antonio community with live musical productions and educational programming. Part of the local performing arts scene with a mission to grow and sustain local theater by building opportunities for local creatives.
