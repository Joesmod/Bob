# Sanchos Cantina y Cocina

## Overview
Mexican cantina and restaurant in San Antonio's Five Points neighborhood with **free live music** 7 nights a week. Full bar featuring award-winning margaritas and scratch-made Mexican food. Not a ticketed venue.

## History
Opened February 14, 2015 by Samuel Asvestas and Rob Barnett, converting a small residence into a cantina. Open every day since launch (4040+ consecutive days as of March 2026). Founders lived "south of the border" and created an authentic Mexican atmosphere.
