## About

Historic theater in downtown Midland operated by Midland Community Theatre (MCT). Purchased by MCT in 1999, the Yucca hosts theatrical productions including musicals, plays, and the famous Summer Mummers melodrama series (running since 1949). MCT also operates the Cole Theatre at 2000 W Wadley Ave.

## History

The Yucca Theatre was purchased by Midland Community Theatre in 1999, becoming one of their primary performance venues. MCT itself was founded in 1946 by Art Cole and has been providing quality theater arts to the Permian Basin for over 75 years. Notable achievements include being the first community theater in the USA to produce The Producers, Rent, Les Miserables, and Shrek The Musical. Summer Mummers began at the Yucca in 1981.
