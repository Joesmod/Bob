# Amarillo Opera

## Overview
Opera company serving the Texas Panhandle region. Produces full-scale opera productions at Globe News Center. Season includes classic and contemporary works with professional casts.

## History
Opera company bringing professional opera performances to the Amarillo region. Mission is to celebrate the voice by providing high caliber operatic performances, nurturing local talent through community outreach, and building passion for opera.
