# Toyota Music Factory

## Overview
Mixed-use entertainment district in Irving, TX (Las Colinas area) featuring multiple venues, restaurants, bars, and entertainment options. The centerpiece is The Pavilion at Toyota Music Factory, an outdoor amphitheater for concerts. The district also includes Alamo Drafthouse Cinema, The Plaza (outdoor event space), and various dining/entertainment tenants.

## History
Developed by Brookfield Properties as a modern entertainment destination. The district combines live music, dining, movies, and retail in an urban walkable environment.

## Programming & Shows
- **The Pavilion**: Major touring concerts (country, rock, pop)
- **The Plaza**: Free community events, seasonal programming (ice rink, festivals)
- **Alamo Drafthouse**: Movies and cinema events
- **Restaurants/Bars**: Live music and entertainment at various venues
- **Frequency**: Multiple events weekly across all venues
- **Ticket prices**: Varies by venue - concerts $30-$150+, movies $10-$15, Plaza events often free

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 35/100 — Low Priority**

Mixed-use entertainment district, not a single independent venue. The Pavilion is Live Nation-operated (locked into Ticketmaster ecosystem). Smaller Plaza events use Eventbrite (switchable), but these are often free community events. Not a typical ticketing client - too large/corporate with existing major partnerships. Better targets are independent single-venue operators.
