# Lexus Box Garden at Legacy Hall

## Overview
Major live music and entertainment venue inside Legacy Hall food hall in Plano, Texas. Features tribute bands, original touring artists, karaoke, trivia, and specialty events (Bingo Loco). Extensive calendar with 15-20 events per month, ranging from free admission to premium ticketed shows. Part of the Legacy Hall dining and entertainment complex.

## Programming & Shows

### Event Types:
- **Tribute Bands**: Queen, Journey, Elton John, Taylor Swift, Luke Combs, Shania Twain, Pink Floyd, Matchbox Twenty, Paramore, Morgan Wallen, Michael Jackson, and more
- **Touring Artists**: Reverend Horton Heat, Old 97's, Gary P. Nunn, Paul Thorn, The Band of Heathens
- **90s/Era Shows**: Live 90, The Clinton Years, The Spazmatics (80s new wave)
- **Specialty Events**: Bingo Loco (party bingo), trivia nights, karaoke
- **Free Events**: Some tribute shows (Matchbox Twenty, Shania Twain, Paramore, Morgan Wallen)

### Genres:
Country, rock, pop, 80s/90s, indie rock, tribute bands

### Frequency:
- **4-6 events per week**
- Wednesday karaoke (free)
- Thursday-Sunday: ticketed shows or free events
- Special trivia nights (sports, Taylor Swift)

### Ticket Prices:
- **Free**: Select tribute shows, trivia, karaoke
- **Paid**: $15-$45 typical for ticketed shows
- **Premium**: Up to $75+ for major acts (Reverend Horton Heat, Old 97's)

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 85/100 — Strong Lead**

Excellent candidate for Opendate. High-volume ticketed venue (15-20 events/month) currently using Prekindle, a less-known platform that could be switched. Mix of free and paid events creates marketing funnel. Strong tribute band circuit connections. Food hall setting provides built-in audience and F&B revenue stream.

**Pros:**
- **High volume**: 4-6 events/week, year-round calendar
- **Diverse pricing**: Free to $75+ accommodates different acts
- **Established booking**: Major tribute bands + touring artists
- **Prekindle migration**: Less entrenched than Ticketmaster/AXS
- **Food hall advantage**: Diners become concertgoers
- **Strong calendar**: Published months in advance

**Cons:**
- **Large operation**: May have existing contracts or corporate relationships
- **Food hall complexity**: Might be managed by larger parent company
- **Competition**: Likely approached by many platforms
- **Free events**: Some shows are loss-leaders for F&B, not ticket revenue

**Decision**: Priority lead. The combination of volume, current platform (Prekindle), and food hall model is ideal for Opendate. Worth a direct outreach to venue management or Legacy Hall ownership.

**Competitive Angle**: Opendate could offer better community integration, artist tools, and pricing flexibility than Prekindle while maintaining the food hall's dual revenue model (tickets + F&B).

**Key Contact Strategy**: Identify venue manager or Legacy Hall Director of Entertainment. Emphasize Opendate's ability to handle complex calendars, mixed free/paid events, and integration with F&B operations.
