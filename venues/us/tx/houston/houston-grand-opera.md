## About

Houston Grand Opera (HGO) is one of the world's leading opera companies. HGO's mission is to enrich the diverse community through the art of opera by creating, curating, exploring, and producing outstanding experiences centered around the human voice. Performs at the Wortham Theater Center (Brown and Cullen Theaters) in Houston's Theater District.

## History

HGO is the only opera company in the world to win an Emmy, a Grammy, and a Tony Award:
- **Three Grammys**: Nixon in China (1987), Porgy and Bess (1976), Intelligence (2026)
- **One Tony**: Porgy and Bess (1976)
- **Three Emmys**: The Ring Cycle (2017), Hitting the High Cs (1998), Nixon in China (1987)

Decades of artistic excellence and community engagement. Recognized nationally and internationally for world-class productions.
