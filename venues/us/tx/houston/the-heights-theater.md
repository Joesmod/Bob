## About

The Heights Theater is an independent music venue in Houston's Heights neighborhood, hosting live concerts primarily featuring indie, folk, americana, and alternative acts. The venue operates on an event-driven schedule, typically open Thursday through Saturday evenings for ticketed shows. Known for booking touring acts like The Jayhawks, Bob Schneider, Watchhouse, and Magic City Hippies.

The venue has a full bar and emphasizes intimate live music experiences.
