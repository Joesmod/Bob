# Longhorn Ballroom

## Overview
Historic 1,500-capacity music venue in Dallas, TX. One of Texas's most legendary music venues with a rich history spanning seven decades. Features both the main ballroom and a backyard amphitheater. Hosts touring national acts across country, rock, folk, and alternative genres. Active concert calendar with major label artists.

## History
Opened in the 1950s, the Longhorn Ballroom has been a cornerstone of Texas music history. Over its 70+ year history, the venue has hosted virtually every major country and rock artist who's toured through Texas. Notable first performances include:
- **1950s**: Bob Wills, Hank Thompson, Nat King Cole, Fats Domino
- **1960s**: Willie Nelson, Waylon Jennings, B.B. King, Patsy Cline, Ray Price, Johnny Cash
- **1970s**: Sex Pistols, Jerry Jeff Walker, Merle Haggard, David Allan Coe
- **1980s**: Stevie Ray Vaughan, Ramones, Red Hot Chili Peppers, George Strait
- **1990s-Present**: Old 97's, Lucinda Williams, Old Crow Medicine Show, Morgan Wade

The venue is a designated Texas music landmark and remains one of the most important independent music venues in the state.
