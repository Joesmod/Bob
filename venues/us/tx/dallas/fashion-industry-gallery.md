# Fashion Industry Gallery

## Overview
Wholesale fashion trade show venue in Dallas, TX. NOT a consumer entertainment venue. Hosts B2B apparel and accessories markets for fashion industry buyers and exhibitors five times per year.

## History
FIG is a contemporary wholesale venue focused on curated women's fashion collections from Los Angeles, New York, and Dallas. Operates as a professional trade showroom facility.
