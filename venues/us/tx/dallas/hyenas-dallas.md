# Hyena's Dallas

## Overview
Established comedy club at Mockingbird Station in Dallas, part of the Hyena's Comedy Nightclubs chain (30+ years in operation). Books nationally touring comedians and rising stars for weekend shows and special events. Full bar, food menu, and intimate showroom designed specifically for stand-up comedy.

## History
Part of the Hyena's Comedy Nightclubs chain that has been presenting live stand-up comedy in the Southwest for over 30 years. The Dallas location is one of four venues operated by the company, alongside two Fort Worth locations (Main Room and Red Room) and an Albuquerque location.
