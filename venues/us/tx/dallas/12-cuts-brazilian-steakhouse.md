# 12 Cuts Brazilian Steakhouse

## Overview
Brazilian churrascaria restaurant in Dallas, TX. Upscale dining focusing on rodizio-style service with carved meats tableside, salad bar, and sides. Not an entertainment venue.

## History
Founded by Juarez De Conto and Alda Boiani, originally from Brazil. The owners moved to the United States in 1997 to bring the churrascaria concept to the U.S. After 30+ years in the restaurant industry, they opened 12 Cuts with their own twist on the traditional Brazilian steakhouse.
