# OCHRE HOUSE THEATER

## Overview
Intimate 55-seat storefront theater in Dallas's Expo Park neighborhood. Specializes in original, experimental "Suavant-Garde" productions — surrealist, sophisticated, and highly acclaimed indie theater. Founded and run by Matthew Posey.

## History
Founded by Matthew Posey (actor, writer, director with credits in JFK, No Country for Old Men, Rectify, Lonesome Dove). Operating for 10+ years with 50+ original productions. Built a reputation as Dallas's most innovative and experimental theater space.

## Programming & Shows
- **Format**: Original productions only — written, directed, and performed by in-house company
- **Style**: "Suavant-Garde" — experimental, surrealist, with music, puppetry, original storytelling
- **Frequency**: Multiple productions per season
- **Current Show**: "Blood Hammer Girl" (Feb 18-Mar 7, 2026)
- **Accessibility**: ASL interpretation nights
- **Pricing**: Standard tickets + "Donate-What-You-Can" nights
- **Capacity**: 55 seats
- **Awards**: Numerous Dallas theater awards and critical acclaim (Dallas Morning News, Dallas Observer, CultureMap Dallas)
- **Audience**: Hip, adventurous theater enthusiasts; "underground Dallas" crowd
