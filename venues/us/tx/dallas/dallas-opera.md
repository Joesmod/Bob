## About

The Dallas Opera connects local and global audiences through world-class performances of both new and traditional operatic works. A major professional opera company performing at the Margot and Bill Winspear Opera House in the AT&T Performing Arts Center. Presents classical and contemporary operas with international stars and emerging talent.

## History

Founded in November 1957 with an unforgettable opening performance by legendary Greek soprano Maria Callas. The Dallas Opera has presented many international stars in their American debuts, including Dame Joan Sutherland, Montserrat Caballé, Jon Vickers, Plácido Domingo, as well as designer/director Franco Zeffirelli.
