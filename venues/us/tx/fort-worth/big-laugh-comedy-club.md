# Big Laugh Comedy Club

## Overview
Active comedy club in downtown Fort Worth with two rooms (Panther Room and Funky Room). High-volume venue presenting multiple shows per week including national touring headliners, local showcases, open mics, and special events. Part of Big Laugh Comedy media company. Full bar and Big Laugh Bistro restaurant on-site.

## History
Media company and comedy club focused on bringing laughter "all the time." Operates multiple rooms for simultaneous programming. Recently expanded into live streaming with "Big Laugh Live: Thursday Night" multi-camera broadcast show.

## Programming & Shows
- **Event Types**: National headliners, local showcases, open mics, improv shows, live streaming
- **Frequency**: 7 nights per week, multiple shows per night
- **Genres**: Stand-up comedy, improv, comedy competitions
- **Recurring Weekly Shows**:
  - Sunday: Sunday Funday Comedy Jam, After Hours: Down & Funny
  - Monday: The Big Laugh Open Mic ($2 tickets)
  - Tuesday: Petty Tuesday, Comedians Against Humanity
  - Wednesday: Whiskey + Laughs Wednesdays (30% off whiskey), Wizop Wednesday Open Mic
  - Thursday: Big Laugh Live (live streamed), Tequila Thursday Showcase
