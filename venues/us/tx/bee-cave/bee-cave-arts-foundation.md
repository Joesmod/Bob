# Bee Cave Arts Foundation

## Overview
501(c)(3) nonprofit arts foundation with community art center and exhibition space called "The Hive" at Hill Country Galleria in Bee Cave, TX. Founded in 2009 and celebrated 15th anniversary in 2025. Mission is to improve quality of life through art awareness, education, and integration throughout public spaces. Offers year-round art classes, summer camps, exhibitions, and public art projects.

## History
Founded in 2009 by Deby Childress and Chad Bockius (former Bee Cave City Council member). Over 15+ years, the Foundation has:
- Built the Bee Cave Sculpture Park (30 sculptures, trails, picnic areas)
- Placed artist-created benches across Hill Country Galleria
- Hosted hundreds of exhibitions of student and professional artists
- Offered continuous classes and summer art camps for youth
- Created "The Benches of Bee Cave" and "The Bees of Bee Cave" outdoor public art projects

Named Bee Cave Chamber of Commerce Non-Profit of the Year 2023. Holds GuideStar Gold Seal for transparency and GreatNonprofits Top-Rated Award 2022.

- **Public Art Projects**: Sculpture park, benches, community installations
- **Special Events**:
