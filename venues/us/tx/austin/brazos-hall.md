# Brazos Hall

## Overview
Historic warehouse-turned-event venue at 4th & Brazos in downtown Austin. 8,400 sq ft main hall plus 3,500 sq ft rooftop deck with skyline views. Event rental facility for weddings, corporate events, and private parties — NOT a ticketed entertainment venue.

## Capacity
- **Main Hall**: Up to 500 standing / 300 seated
- **Rooftop Deck**: Up to 150 seated
- Total: 8,400 sq ft interior + 3,500 sq ft rooftop

## Programming & Shows
**None** — This is an event rental venue for:
- Weddings (ceremony & reception)
- Corporate events (product launches, offsites, activations)
- Social events (private parties, fundraisers, reunions)

No public ticketed entertainment. Space is rented to private clients.
