# Theater Latté Da

## Overview
Theater Latté Da is a professional musical theater company operating the historic 240-seat Ritz Theater in northeast Minneapolis. Founded in 1998, they specialize in creating and presenting new musical theater works alongside reimagined classics, focusing on bold, innovative productions that explore the intersection of story and music.

## History
Founded in 1998, Theater Latté Da performed in various Twin Cities venues for nearly two decades before finding a permanent home. In 2016, they acquired and restored the historic Ritz Theater in northeast Minneapolis, a 240-seat venue with administrative offices, rehearsal space, dressing rooms, and box office. The Ritz now serves as their artistic home and community hub.
