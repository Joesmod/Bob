# Graze Food Hall by Travail

## Overview
Food hall in Minneapolis' North Loop featuring seven culinary concepts under one roof, including four James Beard-nominated chefs. Hosts regular community events including trivia nights, drag shows, open mic nights, and themed parties. Mix of free events and ticketed offerings.

## History
Launched by the Travail Collective to bring diverse culinary voices and community programming to the North Loop neighborhood. Emphasizes creativity, diversity, and accessible dining experiences.
