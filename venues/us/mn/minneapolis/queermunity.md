# Queermunity

## Overview
LGBTQ+ and BIPOC-focused event venue, café, and gathering space in Minneapolis' Uptown neighborhood. Produces ticketed events, hosts free community programming, and operates a café with locally sourced food. Open daily 8 AM-8 PM with free general entry.

## History
Founded in 2022 by Hilary Otey and Kayla Barth to create an inclusive, community-driven space for LGBTQ+ and BIPOC individuals. Developed comprehensive business plan in 2023 with support from FINNOVATION Institute. Opened as a queer and Black-owned social enterprise operating outside traditional nonprofit models.

## Programming & Events
- **Ticketed Events**: One-person shows ($15-20), puzzle competitions ($20-60), special performances
- **Free Weekly Programming**: GayAA (Tuesdays 6-7pm), Knit Happens (Mondays 4-8pm), Beats and Breath yoga (Saturdays 11am-12pm)
- **Free Monthly Events**: Trans Resilience Night (3rd Sunday), Reading Rainbow Storytime (2nd Sunday), Segundo Sabado (2nd Saturday), Fruit Salad Open Mic (4th Thursday)
- **Regular Events**: Let's Do Coffee (1st Monday & 3rd Thursday, 60+ LGBTQ+ folks), Trans Health Insurance Help (2nd & 4th Tuesday), Writing for Recovery (2nd Tuesday)
- **Special Events**: Art exhibits, community gatherings, workshops

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 75/100 — Strong Fit**

Queermunity is a solid candidate for Opendate. They actively produce ticketed events and currently use Givebutter (a fundraising/ticketing platform that's switchable). Revenue mix includes ticketed events, café sales, space rentals, and memberships — ticketing is a meaningful revenue stream. They operate as a social enterprise reinvesting proceeds into free programming.

**Why they'd switch**:
- Need integrated platform for events + memberships + space rentals  
- Current platform (Givebutter) is fundraising-focused, not venue-optimized
- Growing event calendar with mix of free and paid offerings
- Community-focused mission aligns with supporting independent platforms

**Considerations**:
- Some revenue goes to free programming (social mission)
- Ticket volumes moderate (not a high-volume concert venue)
- Value add would be membership integration + space rental + event ticketing in one platform

**Outreach angle**: Support for LGBTQ+/BIPOC-owned businesses, mission-aligned partnership, integrated platform for their hybrid revenue model.
