# Impact Hub Minneapolis

## Overview
501(c)(3) nonprofit coworking space and social impact entrepreneurship network in Minneapolis-Saint Paul. Part of the global Impact Hub network with 100+ locations worldwide. **Not a ticketing venue** — hosts free community events for members and partners rather than producing ticketed public shows.

## History
Established as part of the global Impact Hub network founded to accelerate social impact entrepreneurship. Impact Hub MSP opened to serve the Twin Cities social innovation community, connecting entrepreneurs to resources, coaching, and collaborative workspace.
