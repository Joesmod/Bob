# The Gilbert & Sullivan Very Light Opera Company

## Overview
The Gilbert & Sullivan Very Light Opera Company (GSVLOC) is a Twin Cities theater company specializing in productions of Gilbert & Sullivan operettas and light opera. They present fully-staged, costumed productions with live orchestra.

## History
Information about founding date not prominently featured on website. The company has established itself as the premier Gilbert & Sullivan company in the Twin Cities region.
