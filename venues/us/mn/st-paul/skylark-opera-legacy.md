# Skylark Opera Theatre

## Overview
Opera and musical theater company in St. Paul offering "cutting edge, classical work re-imagined, modern work exposed." Performs in intimate venues throughout the Twin Cities with affordable ticket prices and accessible programming (all performances in English).

## History
Long-standing Twin Cities opera company housed at Landmark Center in St. Paul. Known for wide-ranging repertoire including familiar favorites and new work, many productions new to the Twin Cities stage.

## Mission
Provide Minnesota audiences with highest artistic quality accessible performances of opera and music theater while developing and highlighting finest local and regional talent.

## Programming Philosophy
- **Repertoire**: Mix of familiar favorites and new/contemporary work
- **Balance**: Strong emphasis on both musical and theatrical values
- **Accessibility**: All performances in English in intimate venues
- **Affordability**: Year-round programming at affordable prices
- **Discounts**: Students, seniors, and other selected patron categories
- **Goal**: Create and develop appreciation for opera/musical theater across multiple generations
