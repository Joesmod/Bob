## About

North America's largest interactive true crime murder mystery dinner show. Franchise location in St. Paul, MN held at the DoubleTree by Hilton. Unlike traditional murder mystery shows, actors are not in costume and are hidden among the audience, creating an immersive, comedic detective experience. Each ticket includes a full plated dinner, interactive show, and waitstaff gratuity. Guests compete to solve the crime and win Top Sleuth prize. Shows feature improvisation from top talent (Second City, Improv Olympic, UCB), keeping each performance fresh and spontaneous.

## History

The Dinner Detective brand was founded on November 6, 2004 by Scott and Kelly O'Brien in Culver City, CA. Scott was a screenwriter for ABC's "The Practice" and Fox's "Ally McBeal" and "Boston Public." After attending a disappointing traditional murder mystery show, Scott and Kelly decided to reinvent the genre with sharper improv, real cold case inspiration, and hidden actors. First show was at San Gennaro Restaurant in Culver City. The concept became the first murder mystery dinner show to franchise, now operating in over 100 cities throughout North America.
