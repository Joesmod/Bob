## About

The Historic Holmes Theatre is an 800-seat performing arts venue that serves as the cultural heart of Detroit Lakes and the surrounding region. Part of the DLCCC multi-use facility, the theatre combines 1930s Art Deco elegance with modern amenities. The venue hosts a diverse year-round calendar of professional touring acts, community theatre productions, concerts, comedy shows, dance performances, and educational programming.

## History

The Historic Holmes Theatre officially opened in May 2002 following a remarkable $9+ million community fundraising effort. The facility was built by transforming a dilapidated former school into a state-of-the-art complex. The 800-seat theatre was carefully designed to preserve the elegance and charm of 1930s Art Deco architecture while incorporating modern technical capabilities. The DLCCC complex also includes fitness and aquatics facilities, a senior nutrition center, child care facilities, and commercial space — making it a true community hub that nurtures "body, mind, and spirit."

Since opening, the theatre has become a vital regional arts center, drawing visitors from across Minnesota and serving as a launchpad for emerging artists while supporting established local talent.

## Programming & Shows

The Holmes Theatre presents a rich variety of year-round programming:

- **Music**: Classical, country, rock, tribute bands, jam bands, big band swing
- **Theatre**: Community theatre productions (Holmes Community Theatre), children's theatre, touring productions
- **Comedy**: Stand-up and variety comedy shows
- **Dance**: Professional dance companies and community performances
- **Family Programming**: Children's activities, seasonal productions
- **Special Events**: Flight Notes intimate concerts, Concerts in the Park, art exhibitions

Recent/current shows include:
- Annie (musical, March 2026) — $21 adults, $13 students
- Collective Unconscious performs Bridge Over Troubled Water
- Electric Avenue: The 80s MTV Experience (tribute)
- Songwriting workshops with Clayton Ryan & Molly Brandt
- Swing Dance Nights with Beach Front Big Band
- Lakes Area Community Concert Band performances
- The Fabulous Armadillos

**Event Frequency**: Multiple shows per week year-round  
**Ticket Range**: $13-$25 typical for most shows
