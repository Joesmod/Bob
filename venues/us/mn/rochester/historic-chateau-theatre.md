## About

A community gathering place in the heart of downtown Rochester showcasing culture, entertainment, and events. Features a "Parlour stage" for intimate concerts and regular community programs including weekly lunch gatherings, puzzle and game nights, and music festivals.

## History

Historic theater building that has been restored as a community performance and events center.

## Programming & Shows

- **Genres**: Live music (rock, blues, folk), comedy, community events
- **Notable acts**: Teeth (local band), The Hazy Phase (Minneapolis blues rock)
- **Recurring events**: 
  - Weekly community lunch hour (Feb-Apr)
  - Puzzle & Game Night (monthly)
  - Rochester Thaw Music Festival (30 performances across seven downtown stages)
- **Variety**: Classical performances (Renaissance madrigals), big band (Glenn Miller Orchestra), local and touring acts
