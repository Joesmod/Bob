# Heart of the City Music Factory

## Overview
Victorian-style concert venue and event center on the second floor of the Old Milk Factory in historic Anoka, MN. Intimate 130-seat capacity venue operated by Heart of the City Ministries, a Christian non-profit. Features vintage wallpaper, fireplace on stage, balcony, and high-quality production. Regular concert series featuring diverse artists from classical to reggae, plus private event rentals.

## History
Part of Heart of the City Ministries, a non-profit that has been operating for over 30 years (founded ~1996, celebrated 17th anniversary in 2026 based on InspiredWordNYC event). The Music Factory serves both as a concert venue and rentable event space. The ministry also operates a touring band that performs at Native American reservations and correctional facilities.

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 65/100 — Moderate Lead**

Mixed suitability. The venue hosts regular ticketed concerts (2-4/month) with established Christian music artists, which fits Opendate's model. However, as a 501(c)(3) ministry, they may have unique requirements around nonprofit ticketing, donation integration, and tax handling.

**Pros:**
- Regular ticketed concert calendar
- Established artist booking (national Christian acts)
- Quality production and venue
- Advance planning (events scheduled months ahead)

**Cons:**
- Non-profit status may require specialized features
- Lower frequency (2-4 shows/month vs. weekly)
- Niche audience (Christian music)
- Dual-use venue (concerts + private rentals)
- May prefer donation-based or ministry-integrated platform

**Decision**: Worth a conversation to understand their ticketing needs, especially if Opendate can support nonprofit features (donation add-ons, tax receipts, ministry integration). The quality of the operation and established calendar are strong positives.

**Ministry Context**: Heart of the City also operates a touring band performing at Native American reservations and correctional facilities, plus CD production. The Music Factory is one revenue stream supporting broader ministry work.
