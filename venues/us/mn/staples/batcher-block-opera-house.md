# Batcher Block Opera House

## Overview
The Batcher Block Opera House is a historic 1907 opera house in Staples, Minnesota, currently undergoing restoration and accessibility upgrades. The building is being preserved as a cultural and performance venue while working to ensure accessibility for all guests through a capital campaign focused on elevator installation.

## History
Built in 1907 by Charles Batcher along the historic Jefferson Highway (a north-south route connecting Winnipeg to New Orleans), the opera house served the thriving railroad town of Staples when it hosted 25,000 train passengers and 1,000 automobiles per day. Like many rural opera houses of the era, it offered space for artistic performance and public assembly. The building is being restored to preserve this legacy while adapting it for modern use.
