## About

Rockwoods is a full-service American restaurant with a large bar, outdoor seating, and a small stage for entertainment. The venue hosts community fundraising nights (donating 10% of sales to local organizations), holiday events, and occasional ticketed comedy shows featuring touring stand-up comedians.

## Programming & Shows

- **Primary Focus**: Restaurant dining and bar service
- **Secondary Programming**: Stand-up comedy shows (2-4 times per month)
- **Comedy Shows**: Two shows per night (early: doors 5:45pm, show 6:30pm; late: doors 8:30pm, show 9:30pm)
- **Recent Acts**: Blake Wexler, Chloe Radcliffe (nationally touring comedians)
- **Ticket prices**: Varies by comedian, sold via Eventbrite
- **Other Events**: Community fundraiser nights (weekly), St. Patrick's Day, Easter dinner, Mother's Day specials

## Opendate Fit
