# Uptown Comedy Corner

## Overview
Established comedy club in the Atlanta metro area, billing itself as "Atlanta's Original Uptown Comedy Corner." While the website says "Uptown Comedy Corner is not a Building. It's a BRAND!!", they host regular ticketed stand-up comedy shows at various locations. Known for featuring both national headliners and local talent in an intimate, urban comedy setting.

## History
Over 20 years in operation. Has hosted major names including Chris Rock, Kevin Hart, Dave Chappelle, Kevin Hart, Damon Wayans, Chris Tucker, Mike Epps, Steve Harvey, Ricky Smiley, and many others. The venue has built a reputation as a key stop for urban comedy in the Southeast.
