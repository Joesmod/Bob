# Shenanigans Irish Pub

## Overview
Irish pub and restaurant in downtown Dahlonega, GA. Traditional Irish pub with restaurant seating, offering Irish cuisine, pub fare, and seafood. Hosts free live entertainment but does not operate as a ticketed venue.

## Programming & Shows
- **Type**: Free entertainment at restaurant/bar
- **Regular Events**:
  - Karaoke: Wednesdays 6:30-9:30pm
  - Trivia: Thursdays 7pm & 8pm (prizes for winning teams)
  - Live music: Friday & Saturday at 6:30pm
  - Tom & Juli: Saturday afternoons 1-4pm
  - Sunday Wing Night: Sundays starting 4pm
  - Sunday Brunch: 11am-2pm
- **Notable**: St. Patrick's Day celebrations with bagpiper and live bands
