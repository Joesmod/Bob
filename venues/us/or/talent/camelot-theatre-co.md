Professional theater company operating the state-of-the-art James M. Collier Theatre in Talent, Oregon (3 miles north of Ashland). Non-profit 501(c)(3) producing high-quality affordable plays, musicals, and musical events.

## About

Located at the corner of Talent Avenue and Main Street in the Rogue Valley. Accessible from Interstate 5 exit 21. The organization provides a supportive environment for professional and amateur theater artists and technicians while training adults, teens, and children in the theater arts.

## History

Founded as a community theater organization. Now operates in the modern James M. Collier Theatre, a state-of-the-art performance facility.
