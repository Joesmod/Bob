## About

The Grants Pass High School Performing Arts Center (the "PAC") is a multi-venue facility operated by Grants Pass School District. Features two performance venues (730-seat main theater and 105-seat studio), plus a large drama room that can function as a black box theater, band room, choir room, and orchestra room. Hosts approximately 180 events per year, split between school programs (band, choir, orchestra, drama) and community rentals (dance studios, concerts, forums, film festivals, symphonies, world-class touring artists).

## History

Opened in 1999. Celebrated 25th anniversary in 2024 with preservation and enhancement initiatives for the next 25 years.

## Opendate Fit

**Score: 30/100 — Not a Fit**

This is a school district-operated facility that primarily serves as a rental venue for community organizations and student programming. While they host ~180 events/year, the PAC doesn't sell tickets directly — renters (dance studios, touring artists, community groups) handle their own ticketing. The venue uses a Google Forms-based reservation system appropriate for their rental model. Not a strong candidate for Opendate, which is designed for venues that self-produce ticketed events. Better served by facility rental management software.
