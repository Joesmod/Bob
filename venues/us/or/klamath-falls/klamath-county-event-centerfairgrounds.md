# Klamath County Event Center/Fairgrounds

## Overview
County fairgrounds and event complex in Klamath Falls, OR. The region's premier venue for public events and private gatherings. Includes the John Hancock Arena, outdoor spaces, and a newly opened RV park. Hosts rodeos, concerts, festivals, weddings, corporate events, and trade shows.

## History
County-operated facility serving the Klamath Falls region. Recently expanded with new RV park accommodations for event attendees and visitors.
