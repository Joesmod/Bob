## About

The Mission Theater is a historic church converted into a live music venue, operated by McMenamins (a well-known Pacific Northwest brewery and hospitality company). Originally home to the Swedish Mission Covenant congregation in the early 20th century, it now hosts live music, video dance nights, and special events. The venue features an open floor and a mezzanine on three sides with first-come, first-served seating.

## History

Built in the early 20th century as the Swedish Mission Covenant church. Later converted by McMenamins into a multi-purpose entertainment venue, preserving its historic architecture while adding modern sound and lighting systems. Part of McMenamins' portfolio of historic property conversions across Oregon and Washington.
