## About

Historic music venue and ballroom in downtown Portland, part of the McMenamins family of properties. Features live music performances in a legendary space with unique "floating" dance floor. Part of a larger property that includes the Crystal Hotel across the street.

## History

The Crystal Ballroom is one of Portland's most iconic music venues, known for its historic architecture and legendary "floating" dance floor built on ball bearings. Has hosted countless major national and regional touring acts over decades. Now owned and operated by McMenamins, a Pacific Northwest hospitality company known for preserving and repurposing historic properties.
