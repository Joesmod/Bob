# Artichoke Community Music

## Overview
Nonprofit venue, music school, and instrument store in Portland's folk music tradition. 55-seat capacity venue (80 standing) featuring full PA system, DMX lighting, and professional video streaming setup. Combined venue/school/retail model serving the community since 1971.

## History
Founded in 1971 (originally 1973 per website tagline), Artichoke has been a Portland folk music institution for over 50 years. Rooted in the belief that "music builds bridges between people, generations, and cultures." Recently upgraded venue with modern sound/video systems while maintaining community-focused mission.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Folk, bluegrass, roots, Americana, storytelling, poetry, dance
- **Frequency**: Regular live events, classes, workshops
- **Ticket prices**: Low-cost or free (mission-driven pricing)
- **Notable programming**:
  - Live concerts featuring local and touring folk artists
  - Music classes (guitar, mandolin, banjo, strings)
  - Community workshops
  - Poetry and storytelling events
  - All-ages programming
- Strong volunteer program (sound techs, video techs, door hosts, bartenders, emcees)
