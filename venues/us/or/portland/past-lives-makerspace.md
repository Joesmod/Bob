# Past Lives Makerspace

## Overview
26,000 sq. ft. membership-based makerspace in SE Portland. Provides 24/7 access to wood shop, metal shop with blacksmithing/welding/casting, textiles studio, stained glass, CNC/3D printing, darkroom, and private studio spaces. Founded by Morlock, a former prisoner who built Past Lives around community redemption and creative collaboration.

## History
Founded by Morlock after his release from prison as a way to provide creatives with tools, space, and community to build sustainable careers in art and craft. The mission emphasizes that "no one is irredeemable" and offers second chances through making. Currently houses 175 members and has completed 176 commissioned projects.
