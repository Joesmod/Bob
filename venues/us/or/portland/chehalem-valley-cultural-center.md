# Chehalem Cultural Center

## Overview
Community cultural center in Newberg, Oregon (Portland metro area) featuring the LaJoie Theatre for live performances. Nonprofit arts organization offering visual arts education, galleries, ceramics studio, and ticketed performances. Hosts theatrical productions, concerts, festivals, and community events. The venue includes multiple gallery spaces, classrooms, and a professional theatre.

## History
Established community arts center serving the Chehalem Valley region. The organization operates as a cultural hub combining arts education, gallery exhibitions, and live performance programming.

## Programming & Shows
- **LaJoie Theatre**: Ticketed performances including theatre, music, comedy
- **Resident Company**: Chehalem Valley Vaudeville (family-friendly variety shows)
- **Festivals**: 
  - NEWGRASS Brews & Bluegrass Festival (March 13-14, 2026) - partnership with Wolves & People Farmhouse Brewery
  - Willamette Valley Lavender Festival (annual)
- **Community Events**: 
  - Dr. Martin Luther King Day Celebration
  - Hispanic Heritage Month programming
  - Artist receptions and gallery openings
- **Additional Programming**: Youth and adult art classes, workshops, ceramics studio
- **Frequency**: Regular performance calendar with ticketed shows
- **Ticket types**: Adult, Senior (65+), Student, Arts for All (Oregon Trail Card holders)
