# SCRAP PDX

## Overview
501(c)(3) nonprofit creative reuse center - essentially a thrift store for art supplies, craft materials, and creative reuse items. Mission is to "inspire creative reuse and environmentally sustainable behavior." This is NOT a venue - it's a retail nonprofit.

## History
SCRAP Creative Reuse has been operating in Portland as a sustainability-focused nonprofit. They accept donations of art/craft/office materials and resell them affordably to the community. They also offer educational programs and workshops.
