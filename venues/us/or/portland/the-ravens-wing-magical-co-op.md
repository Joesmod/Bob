# The Raven's Wing Magical Co-Op

## Overview
Employee-owned cooperative retail shop specializing in magical supplies, located in Portland, OR. Operating since 2022, the shop sells metaphysical supplies, herbs, books, tarot decks, and related items both in-store and via their webstore. Open 7 days a week, noon-7:00pm.

## History
Employee-owned and operated since 2022. Part of the worker cooperative movement in Portland. Affiliated with Bookshop.org for book and deck sales.
