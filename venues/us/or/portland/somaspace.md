## About

SomaSpace is a movement arts and healing center in Portland's Hollywood district. The space is available for rent by instructors and facilitators who host their own embodiment classes, workshops, and events. Programming includes conscious movement, sound healing, breathwork, somatics, contact improv, qi gong, ecstatic dance, yoga, clothing swaps, women's events, authentic relating, and teacher trainings.

## Business Model

Studio rental space — not a ticketing venue. Individual instructors and workshop leaders rent the space and manage their own event promotion, registration, and payment collection. SomaSpace does not operate as a ticketing entity.
