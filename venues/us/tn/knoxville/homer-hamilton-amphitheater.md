## History

The Homer Hamilton Amphitheater is part of Chilhowee Park & Exposition Center, a historic fairgrounds and events facility in Knoxville. The park hosts a variety of events including fairs, expos, concerts, and community gatherings. Legends Global, a large venue management company, operates the facility.

## Opendate Fit

**Score: 15/100 — Very Poor Fit**

Chilhowee Park is a fairgrounds/expo center managed by Legends Global, a massive international venue management and hospitality company that works with major stadiums, arenas, and entertainment venues worldwide. Legends already has enterprise-level ticketing, concessions, and event management systems in place. As an amphitheater within a fairgrounds complex, most events are likely fairs, expos, and community gatherings rather than regular ticketed concerts. The Legends Global contract would make any independent ticketing platform switch impossible. Wrong scale and wrong vertical for Opendate — this is enterprise venue management territory.
