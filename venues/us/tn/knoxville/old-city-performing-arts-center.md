# Old City Performing Arts Center

## Overview

**VENUE CLOSED / RELOCATED:** This venue at 111 State Street was the former home of River & Rail Theatre from 2019-2024 (5 years). River & Rail is relocating to a new theater on Magnolia Avenue in East Knoxville.

## History

**Old City Performing Arts Center** served as River & Rail Theatre's home from 2019-2024. After 5 incredible years at this location, the theater company moved to a new, purpose-built space.

## River & Rail Theatre (New Location)

**New Venue Features:**
- Theater: 140-240 flexible seats (100 more than OCPAC)
- Rehearsal Studio / community room
- Multiple green rooms and 3 dressing rooms
- Lobby with café and full bar
- Located on Magnolia Avenue, East Knoxville
