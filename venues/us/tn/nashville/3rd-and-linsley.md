## About

3rd and Lindsley is a legendary Nashville music venue celebrating its 35th anniversary in 2026. Known for its intimate atmosphere and commitment to live music, the venue has been a cornerstone of Nashville's music scene for over three decades. The venue is partnered with Volume for live streaming and recording services, allowing artists to reach new fans and capture high-quality recordings of their performances.

**Key Features:**
- Intimate music venue atmosphere
- Professional live streaming capabilities via Volume
- High-definition recording available for artists
- Artists retain ownership of all content

## History

3rd and Lindsley has been operating as a premier Nashville music venue since 1991 (celebrating 35 years in 2026). Over the decades, it has become a vital part of Nashville's music community, hosting thousands of performances and helping launch countless careers.

**Notable Milestone:** 35th Anniversary Celebration in March 2026 featuring Pat McLaughlin Band with special guests.

## Programming & Shows

3rd and Lindsley hosts a diverse range of programming:

**Regular Series:**
- **WMOT Finally Friday** - Featuring emerging artists (Fridays at noon)
- **Backstage Nashville** - Daytime hit songwriters show (multiple days, 12:30pm)
- **Lightning 100 Nashville Sunday Night** - Weekly Sunday showcase (7:00pm)
- **The Time Jumpers** - Regular Monday night residency (8:00pm)
- **Bluebird on 3rd** - Songwriter showcases

**Show Types:**
- Singer-songwriter showcases
- Tribute bands (Billy Joel/Elton John, Tom Petty, Steely Dan, etc.)
- Blues and rock acts
- Comedy nights
- Album release shows
- Artist collaborations ("& Friends" format)

**Music Genres:**

**Typical Schedule:** Shows throughout the week with multiple time slots (daytime shows at 12:00-12:30pm, evening shows 7:00-8:00pm)
