# 1865 Club

## Overview
Historic social club and acoustic music venue in midtown Nashville, TN. Converted from a US Tobacco barn and auction house, now features 21+ intimate venue with songwriters showcases and acoustic performances. Blends industrial-historic aesthetic with contemporary lounge atmosphere.

## History
The building served as a tobacco barn and auction house for US Tobacco before being meticulously remodeled into 1865 Club. The renovation preserved historic elements while adding modern sophistication, creating a unique industrial-meets-elegant space in the heart of Nashville.
