## About

The Lab at Zanies is an intimate 100-seat comedy theater that opened in Spring 2024, located next door to Zanies Nashville at 2019 8th Avenue South. Born from Zanies Nashville's success and growth, The Lab represents a strategic "expansion by going smaller" — creating a dedicated space for raw, experimental comedy and talent development.

This venue serves as a creative incubator where emerging comedians can hone their craft alongside established acts trying new material. The Lab features dedicated rooms for teaching comedy and improv classes, serving both aspiring comedians and corporate teams for team-building exercises. The space includes a full kitchen with an upscale menu and full liquor bar with craft cocktails.

## History

Opened in Spring 2024 as Zanies Nashville's sister venue. As Zanies Nashville became one of the hottest comedy clubs in the United States, demand outgrew the original venue's capacity. Rather than expanding the footprint, management created The Lab — a smaller, more intimate space for versatility and experimentation.

In Summer 2024, Zanies launched the Zanies Podcast Network, using The Lab as a key recording venue to capture Nashville's comedy scene and broadcast it globally.

## Programming & Shows

The Lab hosts diverse comedy formats:

- **Nateland Live** - Regular podcast recording series (Mon-Wed, $24)
- **New Material Monday** - Testing ground for comedians ($16)
- **Zanies Comedy Allstars** - Showcase nights ($17-$31)
- **Stand-up headliners** - Josh Sneed, Ian Lara, Ian Fidance ($24.50-$31)
- **Drag Brunch** - Monthly entertainment series ($26-$36)
- **Dark & Dirty Showcase** - Edgier comedy content ($17)

Shows typically run Thu-Sat evenings with doors at 6 PM, shows at 7 PM. All events are 18+ only.
