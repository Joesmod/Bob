# Woolworth Theatre

## Overview
Historic performance venue in downtown Nashville with 600+ capacity. Located in the landmark Woolworth building (opened 1925), site of pivotal civil rights sit-ins. Features multiple entertainment concepts including Love Now Sessions, Shiners Nashville comedy/cirque show, and Marquee Nashville speakeasy.

## History
The Woolworth building opened in 1925 and became a pivotal site in the civil rights movement (lunch counter sit-ins). In 2021, the building came under new ownership. Country music artist Chuck Wicks partnered with investor Rob Bellenfant and 615 Ventures to transform it into a multi-concept entertainment venue. Worked with Nashville architect and Tennessee State Museum to preserve the original storefront and honor the building's history. Opened in 2022.
