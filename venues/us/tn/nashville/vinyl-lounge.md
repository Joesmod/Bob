# The Vinyl Lounge

## Overview
Independent live music venue in Nashville's Wedgewood-Houston neighborhood. Features two stages, a rooftop, three bars, and 100 free parking spots. Hosts a variety of genres including EDM, rock, indie, and artist showcases.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: EDM, rock, indie, artist showcases, theme nights (Harry Styles tributes, Music City Madness rock series)
- **Frequency**: Multiple events per week (4-6 shows weekly)
- **Ticket prices**: $0-$44 (free entry for some shows, paid for others)
- **Notable events**: 
  - Blue House Artist Night (weekly showcase)
  - Disco Occasionally theme nights
  - Music City Madness (monthly Nashville rock celebration)
  - RODEOi Fest
  - Touring acts and local artists
