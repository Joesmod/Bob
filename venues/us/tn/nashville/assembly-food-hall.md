## About

Assembly Food Hall is a multi-vendor food hall in Nashville featuring various restaurants, eateries, and bars under one roof. It has a rooftop Skydeck and hosts private parties and community events.

**Not a ticketed event venue** — This is a dining/social space similar to a food court. Events are primarily dining experiences, not concerts or shows requiring ticketing.
