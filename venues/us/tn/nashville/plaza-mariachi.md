## About

Plaza Mariachi is a multi-cultural entertainment and dining complex in Southeast Nashville that celebrates cultural diversity through music, art, food, and entertainment. The venue offers a unique blend of culture, art, music, entertainment, food, services and retail in one historic location, decorated in the style of traditional marketplace streets from old Mexico.

**Location:** Only 10 minutes from downtown Nashville with easy access to 3 major interstates and plenty of free parking.

**Mission:** "Plaza Mariachi exists to celebrate cultural diversity. A gathering place for all ages and all backgrounds to experience and participate in the beauty of international expressions."

**Features:**
- Multiple event spaces
- Indoor and outdoor areas
- Tito's Playland (family entertainment center)
- Plaza Sports Bar
- Multiple dining options

## History

Plaza Mariachi was established as a cultural experience venue weaving Latin culture into a walk of history. The venue showcases old and new Mexico through food, fashion, music, and art. The Tennessean has described it as "a feast for the senses."

## Programming & Shows

Plaza Mariachi hosts a diverse range of cultural events and entertainment:

**Weekly Events:**
- **Wednesday:** Lotería (Mexican Bingo) - 6pm-9pm
- **Saturday:** Flea Market - 8am-4pm
- **Saturday:** Cozy Winter Market - 11am-6pm
- **Saturday:** Happy Hour at Z Bar - 4pm-6pm (half-price Margaritas & Palomas)
- **Sunday:** Flea Market - 8am-4pm
- **Tuesday-Sunday:** Tito's Playland open

**Special Events:**
- **Salsa Night** - Free lessons + open dance floor (Thursdays)
- **Norteñito Night** - Live regional Mexican music (select Fridays)
- **Noches Norteñas** - Live bands and DJ sets (Saturdays)
- **Cultural celebrations** - African Roots, Grupo Folklorico performances
- **Family events** - Kids parties and activities

**Music Genres:**
- Regional Mexican (Norteño, Banda)
- Salsa and Latin dance music
- International cultural performances
- Live bands and DJs
