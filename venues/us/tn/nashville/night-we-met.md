## About

Night We Met is an independent multi-room nightclub & cocktail lounge centered around dance music in Nashville, Tennessee. The venue brings a modern, immersive & elevated nightclub environment to Music City, equipped with a Void Acoustics Sound System. 

**Hours:**
- Thursday: 8pm-1:00am
- Friday: 8pm-3:00am
- Saturday: 8pm-3:00am

The venue uses the Shotgun app for event promotion and ticketing.

## History

Night We Met was established as a dedicated space to help grow a forward-thinking nightlife culture in Nashville, one of the fastest-growing cities in the US. The venue aims to establish Music City as a premier dance music destination in the south.

## Programming & Shows

Night We Met focuses on building a highly curated nightlife ecosystem highlighting:

The venue specializes in **dance music** across multiple rooms, creating an immersive nightclub experience unlike traditional Nashville venues.
