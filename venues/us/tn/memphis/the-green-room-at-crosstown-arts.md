# The Green Room at Crosstown Arts

## Overview

150-capacity performance venue inside Crosstown Concourse, a historic adaptive reuse project in Memphis. Hosts jazz, R&B, indie, experimental music, theater, and monthly jam sessions. Active calendar with 3-4 ticketed shows per week.

## History

Part of Crosstown Arts, the nonprofit arts organization that moved into Crosstown Concourse when the massive building (former Sears distribution center) was renovated and reopened in 2017. Crosstown Arts has been active in Memphis since 2011, initially operating from a different location before moving to Concourse. The Green Room is their primary performance venue within the building.
