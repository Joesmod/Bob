# Crosstown Concourse

## Overview

Mixed-use adaptive reuse building in Memphis, TN — the BUILDING, not the venue. Crosstown Arts operates "The Green Room" performance venue INSIDE Crosstown Concourse. This is a duplicate entry.

## About

Crosstown Concourse is a 1.5 million sq ft building (former Sears distribution center) renovated and reopened in 2017 as a vertical urban village with:
- Apartments
- Retail
- Restaurants (Crosstown Brewing Co., etc.)
- Healthcare facilities
- Crosstown Arts (nonprofit with performance/gallery spaces)
- Crosstown High School

The building itself is NOT a venue — it's the location. **The actual ticketed venue is The Green Room at Crosstown Arts.**
