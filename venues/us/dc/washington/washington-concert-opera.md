# Washington Concert Opera

## Overview
Professional opera company presenting concert-style opera (no sets, no costumes) at Lisner Auditorium in Washington, DC. Over 30 years of presenting world-class opera focused on the music and vocals. Level of production quality: professional singers from major opera houses worldwide.

## History
Founded over 30 years ago by city leaders and opera lovers to bring professional opera to DC audiences. The company puts the score and singers center stage with a pure musical presentation style.
