# Opera Lafayette

## Overview
Opera Lafayette is the nation's premier period-instrument opera company, performing 17th-19th century repertoire in both Washington, DC and New York City. Founded in 1995, they specialize in rediscovering early music masterpieces and reawakening lost operas with period instruments, creating intimate, transcendent productions.

## History
Founded in 1995 as The Violins of Lafayette by Ryan Brown. Originally focused on chamber music, reincorporated as Opera Lafayette in 2001 to reflect increasing concentration on opera. Debut season took place in the Salon Doré of the Corcoran Gallery of Art. Achieved international fame with sold-out performances at Opera Royal in Versailles, France (2012, 2014). Celebrating 30 years (as of 2025).

Now the only opera company to perform its full season in both Washington, DC and New York City.
