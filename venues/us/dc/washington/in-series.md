# IN Series

## Overview
IN Series is DC's innovative chamber opera company, known for their tagline "Opera that speaks. Theater that sings." Operating as an independent non-profit since 2000, they present intimate, chamber-scale opera productions that transform perceptions of what opera can be. Based at Source Theater in Washington, DC.

## History
Founded by Carla Hübner in 1982 as a concert series at former Mount Vernon College. Became an independent non-profit arts organization in 2000. Timothy Nelson assumed artistic directorship in 2018, establishing the rebranded "IN Series" as DC's home for thought, debate, history, and innovation in opera. Resident company at Source Theater since 2008.
