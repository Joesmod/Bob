# Astra Miami Rooftop

## Overview
NOT A TICKETED VENUE - Mediterranean restaurant and rooftop lounge in Wynwood with private event space.

Restaurant and lounge with rooftop terrace offering Mediterranean cuisine, cocktails, hookah service, and private event hosting. Describes itself as "where Greek soul meets Miami's bohemian spirit."
