# Circle Square Cultural Center

## Overview
Circle Square Cultural Center is one of the largest multi-use indoor public venues in Ocala, Florida. Part of the Circle Square Commons complex, the Cultural Center hosts concerts, banquets, expos, weddings, and a diverse array of events. Built between 2005-2006 and significantly renovated in 2018, the venue has established itself as a premier entertainment destination in Central Florida.

**Location:** 8395 SW 80th St, Ocala, FL 34481

## History

### Origins
The name "Circle Square" comes from an actual cattle brand used on Circle Square Ranch's cattle. The venue sits on part of the land where the ranch operated (and continues to operate as a working cattle ranch raising Brahmas).

### Circle Square Commons
Built 2005-2006 as part of a larger development including:
- The Ranch Fitness Center & Spa
- Master the Possibilities Lifelong Learning Center
- The Town Square

The complex was designed to resemble a historic town center, with unique building styles and colors creating an aged, established ambiance. The 15-acre site features lush landscaping and meticulous attention to detail.

### Expansion & Renovation (2018)
Major expansion adding:
- 2,000 sq ft lobby
- 3,500 sq ft front veranda
- 1,200 sq ft porte-cochere
- Family restroom
- Two built-in bars

### Event History
