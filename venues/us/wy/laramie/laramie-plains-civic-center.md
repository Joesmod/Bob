# Laramie Plains Civic Center

## Overview
Wyoming's first brick schoolhouse (built 1878), now a community arts hub in Laramie. Multi-venue facility housing 95+ tenant organizations, supporting 300+ artists, hosting hundreds of cultural events annually. The Gryphon Theatre serves as primary performance venue. Mix of rentable spaces (studios, offices), performance venues, gallery, and community gathering spaces. Operated as 501(c)3 nonprofit.

## History
Built 1878 as Laramie's first brick schoolhouse. Served as destination for arts, education, recreation for nearly 150 years. Today operates as nonprofit community center with mission to "foster connections, nurture creativity, and ensure an enriched future."
