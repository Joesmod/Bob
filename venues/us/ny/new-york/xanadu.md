# Xanadu Roller Arts

## Overview
Hybrid roller rink, live music venue, and nightclub in Brooklyn, NY. "The wildest roller rink in the universe!" 7,000 sq ft hand-painted Canadian Maple floor with American-made Riedell skates, live music stage curated by The Sultan Room team, and full bar/concessions. Events-only operation featuring skate sessions, concerts, club nights, and private events.

## History
NYC's roller skating heritage dates back to 1863 when James Plimpton developed the four-wheeled skate and established the NY Roller Skating Association. Following the closure of RollerJam USA in Staten Island (2006-2024), NYC's only year-round indoor rink for nearly 20 years, Xanadu emerged as the new home for skate culture in NYC.

## Programming & Shows
- **Skate Sessions**: Regular skating events with DJ music, $7 skate rental
- **Live Music**: Concerts and performances on dedicated stage, curated by The Sultan Room team (all genres)
- **Club Nights**: Skating 7-10pm, dance floor 10pm+
- **Pre-Show Skate Sessions**: 6-8pm before most live shows (non-skating events)
- **Private Events**: Parties, weddings, bar/bat mitzvahs, brand activations, corporate events
- **Age Policy**: 21+ unless otherwise stated (some 16+/18+ events with guardian)
- **Capacity**: Large-capacity shows with coat check
- **Ticket Prices**: Varies by event (available online + at door if not sold out)
