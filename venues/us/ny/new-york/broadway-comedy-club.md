# Broadway Comedy Club

## Overview
Major comedy club in Times Square with three showrooms (Main Room and Red Room visible on schedule). Established venue serving 600+ comedy fans nightly. Part of Al Martin's NYC comedy club portfolio. Bi-level entertainment complex with extensive show schedule.

## History
Opened in 2003 by Al Martin, legendary NYC comedy club owner who also founded New York Comedy Club (1988). Martin is known for launching careers of Sarah Silverman, Lisa Lampanelli, Tracy Morgan, and Wanda Sykes. He was first to embrace diversity with Latino Comedy Night, and welcomed shows for gay, urban, and Asian communities. Broadway Comedy Club is his flagship Times Square location.
