## About

OPERA America's National Opera Center is a communal home for opera and the performing arts in New York City. It is **NOT a performance venue** but rather a rental facility offering flexible spaces for companies, choruses, ensembles, teaching studios, and individuals for performances, rehearsals, auditions, recordings, meetings, special events, and networking.

The facility features:
- Clean, modern, acoustically excellent spaces
- Regularly tuned pianos in all studios (including a nine-foot Hamburg Steinway)
- Affordable recording and technical packages
- Live streaming capabilities
- Spaces ranging from solo practice rooms to recital halls (up to 72 people)

**IMPORTANT:** The National Opera Center explicitly states: **"The National Opera Center does not offer ticketing software, box office, or promotions. Ticketing and event promotions must be managed by the rental client."**

## History

Built and operated by OPERA America, the national service organization for opera companies across the United States. Serves as a resource and gathering place for the opera community.
