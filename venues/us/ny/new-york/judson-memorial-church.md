# Judson Memorial Church

## Overview
Historic Protestant church in Washington Square South, Greenwich Village. Hosts arts programming including theater, dance, music, and community events. While they present ticketed performances, this is primarily a church with an arts ministry, not a traditional ticketing venue.

## History
Founded in the 1890s, Judson Memorial Church has over a century of history as both a place of worship and arts venue. In the tradition of great community institutions, Judson offers a judgment-free space for diverse programming focused on social justice, creative expression, and spiritual community.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Movement research, theater, experimental performance, folk/blues (Peoples' Voice Cafe), classical music (West Village Chorale)
- **Frequency**: Multiple events per week
- **Ticket prices**: $15-$30 typical for ticketed events
- **Notable programs**: Movement Research (free), Peoples' Voice Cafe (weekly), Dzieci Theatre, West Village Chorale
- Mix of free community events and ticketed performances
