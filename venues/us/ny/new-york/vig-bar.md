# Vig Bar

## Overview
Neighborhood bar in New York City with three distinct spaces: front room with bar and fireplace (120 capacity), back room for private events (50-60 capacity), and outdoor seating. Features DJs on weekends and free comedy shows. NOT a ticketed entertainment venue.

## Venue Type
Bar with DJ nights and free entertainment. Private event space rental available.

## Spaces
- **Front Room**: Main bar area, DJ booth, fireplace, large windows, 120 capacity
- **Back Room**: Private event space for birthdays, happy hours, parties, 50-60 capacity
- **Outdoor Area**: Tables for groups, live sports, dog-friendly

## Programming & Shows
- **DJs**: Thursday/Friday nights and all day Saturday (no cover charge)
- **Free Comedy**: Sundays at 7pm (free)
- No ticketed shows
- Private event rentals

## Entertainment
Bar with resident DJs and free weekly comedy show. No admission charges. All entertainment is complementary to the bar experience.
