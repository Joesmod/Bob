# Savvy Bistro And Bar

## Overview

Caribbean and New Orleans fusion restaurant and bar in Crown Heights, Brooklyn. Features dining, cocktails, and late-night entertainment with recurring weekly events.

## History

Opened in Brooklyn's Crown Heights neighborhood. Named as a celebration of the diverse cultural diaspora of American and Caribbean ancestral roots. Known for "fearlessly fusing multiple celebrated cuisines" - Caribbean meets New Orleans spices and flavors.
