# NYC Pride

## Overview
**Not a venue** — NYC Pride (Heritage of Pride, Inc.) is a nonprofit organization that produces annual LGBTQ+ Pride events in New York City, including the Pride March, PrideFest, and related programming. They organize events at various locations throughout NYC but do not operate a dedicated venue.

## Organization Type
501(c)(3) nonprofit event production organization serving the LGBTQ+ community of New York City and visitors worldwide.

## Events Produced
- **NYC Pride March**: Annual parade through Manhattan (free public event)
- **PrideFest**: Street festival with vendors, entertainment (ticketed or free depending on year)
- **Pride Island**: Multi-day music festival and dance parties (ticketed)
- **Various Pride Month events**: Rallies, parties, cultural programming throughout June

## Business Model
| | |
|---|---|
| **Revenue Sources** | Sponsorships, ticketed events, donations, merchandise |
| **Event Ticketing** | Some events ticketed (Pride Island dance parties), many free (March, street events) |
| **Website** | nycpride.org |

## Key Notes
- Events take place at multiple venues across NYC (Pier locations, streets, parks, bars/clubs)
- Organization partners with venues for ticketed parties and events
- Does not operate a permanent venue space
- Large-scale event production requires complex logistics and ticketing
