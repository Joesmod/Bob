## About

Historic 2,894-seat theater on the Upper West Side of Manhattan. Owned and operated by MSG Entertainment (Madison Square Garden Entertainment). Built in 1929, the venue hosts concerts, comedy shows, and special events. Known for ornate Greek Revival interior and excellent acoustics.

## History

Opened December 24, 1929 as a movie palace. Designated a National Historic Landmark in 1979. Underwent major renovation in 2009. Has been owned by MSG Entertainment since 2006 as part of their entertainment venue portfolio.
