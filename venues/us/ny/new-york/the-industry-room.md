## About

The Industry Room is not a standalone venue but rather a signature program/showcase that operates at Broadway Comedy Club. It provides comedians with opportunities to perform in front of club bookers, managers, agents, and casting directors. Each show is attended by Broadway Comedy Club's booker and at least one industry professional (manager, agent, or casting director).

## Programming & Shows

- **Format**: Comedy showcase with professional development focus
- **Genres**: Stand-up comedy (beginner to intermediate level)
- **Frequency**: Regular showcases plus weekly open mics
- **Ticket prices**: Varies by event
- **Notable programs**: 
  - Comic residency program (1-2 months)
  - 6-week beginner/intermediate comedy classes ($395, graduation show included)
  - Open mic nights
  - Industry feedback sessions with club booker
- Performers receive free video recordings of their sets
