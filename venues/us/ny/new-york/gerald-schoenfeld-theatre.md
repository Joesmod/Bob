## About

The Gerald Schoenfeld Theatre is a Broadway theater owned and operated by the Shubert Organization, one of the oldest and largest theater owners on Broadway. Named after Gerald Schoenfeld, longtime chairman of the Shubert Organization. The theater is part of the Shubert Organization's portfolio of 17 Broadway theaters.

**Note**: Website research limited due to site structure. Shubert Organization operates a centralized booking system across all its Broadway properties.

## History

Historic Broadway theater, part of the Shubert Organization's theater empire. Renamed in honor of Gerald Schoenfeld, who led the Shubert Organization for decades.
