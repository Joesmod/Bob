Irondale Ensemble Project is a Brooklyn-based theater company producing original and classic works through multi-year theatrical investigations. The Space at Irondale serves as both their performance venue and a rentable theater space for other productions.

## History

The Irondale Ensemble has been producing theater as an ensemble of citizen-artists since 1983. Their mission is to use theater craft to provoke discussion, raise awareness, and inspire audiences to embrace a socially just world. They present work that speaks to contemporary issues regardless of when it was written.

Recent multi-year projects include "The 1599 Project" (Shakespeare), "Brecht in Exile," and the current "American Century" exploration of great 20th-century American playwrights.
