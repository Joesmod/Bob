# Brooklyn Botanic Garden

## Overview
52-acre urban botanical garden in Brooklyn, NY, founded in 1910. Features diverse plant collections, educational programs, and cultural exhibitions. Major New York City cultural institution with ~900,000 annual visitors. Accredited by American Alliance of Museums.

## History
Established in 1910 on land reclaimed from an ash dump. Became one of America's premier urban botanical gardens over the past century. Home to the first Children's Garden in the United States. Known for its Japanese Hill-and-Pond Garden, Cherry Esplanade, and extensive plant collections.
