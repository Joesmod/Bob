## About

The Morgan Opera House is a restored Victorian gem featuring an original raked stage with pressed tin proscenium, leaded glass windows, original seating, splendidly crafted woodwork, and superb acoustics. The intimately-sized venue hosts a variety of community performances including classical music, string quartets, folk, theater, and storytelling.

Located in Aurora, NY (Finger Lakes region, population ~700), this is a small-town community arts venue that serves the local area and surrounding region.

## History

Historic Victorian-era opera house that has been restored and is now used for community performances and events. Part of Aurora's cultural heritage, particularly active during events like "Christmas in Aurora."
