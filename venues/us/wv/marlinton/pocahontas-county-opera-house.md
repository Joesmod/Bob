# Pocahontas County Opera House

## Overview

Historic 250-seat theater in Marlinton, WV, serving as the cultural heart of Pocahontas County. Restored community theater presenting a diverse annual performance series including local and international musicians, theater groups, and touring performers.

## History

Historic venue restored by the community. The Pocahontas County Opera House Foundation manages programming and operations. Known for "The Story Sessions" — a celebrated performance series now available as album and podcast.

## Programming & Shows

- **Genres**: Bluegrass, folk, old-time, jazz, classical, theater, family shows
- **Notable Series**: "The Story Sessions" (recorded performances)
- **Frequency**: 10-15 ticketed shows per season (Sept-May)
- **Recent Acts**: Minor Swing (gypsy jazz), Hackensaw Boys, Driftwood, Cristina Vane, Black Mountain Bluegrass Boys
- **Theater**: Drama workshops, children's theater camps (Shakespeare, original productions)
- **Ticket Range**: Typically $10-$25, some free will offerings
- **Special Programs**: AMR Bluegrass Sunday series, sensory-friendly shows
