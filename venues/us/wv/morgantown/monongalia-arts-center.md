# Monongalia Arts Center

## Overview
Community arts education center and cultural venue in Morgantown, WV. Historic post office building converted in 1978 housing two galleries (Benedum and Davis) and the Mabel DeVries Tanner Theatre. Offers art classes, camps, gallery exhibitions, and occasional concerts.

## History
Established in 1978 in the restored historic post office building at 107 High Street. Benedum Gallery opened in 1979, Davis Gallery added in 2004 (expanded 2007-2008), and Tanner Theatre dedicated in 1981. The MAC began regularly booking local and touring bands in the theatre space in 2008. Currently under new leadership undergoing renovations.
