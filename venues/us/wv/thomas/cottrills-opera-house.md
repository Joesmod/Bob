## About

Historic theater built in 1902 currently undergoing restoration by Alpine Heritage Preservation Inc., a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. **This venue is NOT currently operational.** The three-story performance space remains vacant while restoration work continues. Once completed, it will serve as a performing arts center and cultural hub for the Potomac Highlands region.

## History

Built in 1902, Cottrill's Opera House served as a cornerstone of cultural life in Thomas, West Virginia, hosting vaudeville shows, movies, and community events. When industrial activity slowed and the region's population declined, the theater closed. Since 1981, Alpine Heritage Preservation Inc. has worked to save and stabilize the structure. The building is structurally stabilized but the interior performance space requires restoration before it can reopen.
