# Old Opera House Theatre Company & Arts Centre

## Overview
Historic live theater house in the heart of Charles Town, West Virginia. The Old Opera House presents six main stage productions, a summer family production, a one-act play festival, and a ballet every year. Also operates the Old Opera House Arts Centre, teaching classes in acting, dance (ballet, jazz, tap, hip-hop, lyrical, Broadway), and scenic art & design.

## History
Historic opera house in Charles Town, WV that has been preserved as a community theater and arts education center. Runs the annual New Voice Play Festival (25th edition in 2026), showcasing new works.
