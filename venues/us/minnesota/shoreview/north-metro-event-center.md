# North Metro Event Center

## Overview
Full-service conference and event center in Shoreview, MN. Specializes in corporate meetings, conferences, and weddings for groups of 10-280 people. Multiple event spaces with complimentary audio-visual equipment.

## Venue Spaces
- **Grand Ballroom**: 4,000 sq ft (40x100) — up to 280 people (rounds)
- **Ballroom**: 2,400 sq ft (40x60) — up to 200 people (rounds)
- **Northwestern**: 1,600 sq ft (40x40) — up to 80 people (rounds)
- **Bethel, Concordia, Hamline**: 800 sq ft each (20x40) — up to 50 people (rounds)
- **Prefunction**: 1,500 sq ft — up to 100 people (rounds)

## Programming & Shows
