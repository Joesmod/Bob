# Lake Harriet Regional Park

## Overview
Public park in Minneapolis, part of the Minneapolis Park & Recreation Board system. Features walking paths around the lake, gardens, beach areas, and an outdoor bandshell for free summer concerts.

## Programming & Shows
- **Type**: Free outdoor concerts and community events
- **Lake Harriet Bandshell**: Historic outdoor performance space
- **Events**: Summer concert series (free admission)
- **Programming**: Parks system events, community gatherings
- **Notable**: Part of the Chain of Lakes park system
