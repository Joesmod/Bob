# Minneapolis Convention Center

## Overview
Large convention and exhibition center in downtown Minneapolis. Provides event space for trade shows, conventions, consumer shows, conferences, and private events. Operated by Meet Minneapolis.

## Facilities
Major convention center with multiple halls and meeting spaces. Features:
- Exhibition halls
- Meeting rooms
- On-site catering by Kelber Catering
- Accessible design throughout
- Central downtown location

## Programming & Shows
- **Type**: Trade shows, conventions, consumer shows, conferences
- **Events**: Boat shows, travel shows, wedding expos, industry conventions
- **Model**: Venue rental to event organizers
- **Notable**: Individual events may sell tickets, but center itself does not operate as ticketing venue
