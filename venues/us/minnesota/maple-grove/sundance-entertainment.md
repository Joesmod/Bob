# Sundance Entertainment

## Overview
Multi-purpose entertainment complex in Maple Grove, MN. Features 9-hole golf course (currently being redesigned), bowling center, sports bar & restaurant, indoor golf simulators, and private event halls. Primarily a golf/bowling/dining facility.

## Facilities
- **Golf**: 9-hole course + driving range (under redesign until 2026)
- **Bowling**: Full bowling center with leagues
- **Sports Bar & Restaurant**: Food and drinks
- **Indoor Golf Simulators**: Year-round golf simulation
- **Banquet Halls**: Private events, weddings, corporate functions

## Programming & Shows
- **Type**: Free community events
- **Regular Events**:
  - Bar Bingo: Tuesdays 6:30-8pm (hosted by Maple Grove Lions Club)
  - Meat raffles
  - Bowling leagues
- **Notable**: No ticketed entertainment events
