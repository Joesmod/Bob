# New York Mills Regional Cultural Center

## Overview
Multidisciplinary arts organization and retreat center in rural north central Minnesota. Dedicated to expanding cultural and creative opportunities for rural Americans for over 25 years. Home of "The Great American Think-Off," an annual philosophy debate competition that has received national media coverage.

## History
Founded by John Davis in the late 1990s. Located in New York Mills, Otter Tail County, Minnesota. The Center has been operating for more than 25 years. Best known for The Great American Think-Off, now in its 28th year, which has been covered by C-SPAN, The New York Times, and The Today Show.
