## About

Roe Green Theatre is one of several performance venues operated by CU Presents at the University of Colorado Boulder. Located in the University Theatre Building, the venue hosts a diverse range of performances including student recitals, faculty concerts, professional touring acts, theater productions, and dance performances.

CU Presents is the university's performing arts presenter, bringing professional artists to campus and showcasing student and faculty work.

## History

Part of CU Boulder's College of Music and performing arts infrastructure.
