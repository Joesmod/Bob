## About

Flipside Theatre at Superior Improv Co. is a comedy theater and training center focused on improv, sketch comedy, and stand-up performance. Operating under the tagline "Get out of your head. And have more fun," the theater provides a "safe, supportive and entertaining space" for performers and audiences of all skill levels.

The theater offers three main programs:
- **Shows**: House teams, guest acts, stand-up comedy, sketch comedy, and special events (tickets starting at $12)
- **Classes**: Four-level improv program from beginner to advanced
- **Drop-Ins & Jams**: Open sessions for practice and community building
