## About

Bar 404 is a neighborhood bar and live music venue in Denver's South Broadway entertainment district. Describes itself as "your Dad's bar, upgraded" — blending old-school neighborhood bar charm with modern amenities. Features a dedicated music room with full stage and PA system, full food and cocktail menu, weekend brunch, and a patio area. Inclusive, community-focused venue supporting local musicians, artists, and nonprofits.

## History

Located in Denver's historic Baker neighborhood along the South Broadway entertainment district, Bar 404 celebrates Denver's rich history while supporting the local community.
