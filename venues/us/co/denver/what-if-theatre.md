## About

What If Theatre is an improv theater focused on creating "stories onstage" with an emphasis on authenticity beyond just comedy. The theater produces original unscripted plays, hosts top Colorado improv troupes, and offers a four-level improv training program.

The venue emphasizes that while humor is present, audiences come for the authenticity of witnessing stories unfold in real-time alongside the performers.

## Programming & Shows

- **Genres**: Improv theater, long-form improvisation, storytelling
- **Shows**: Original unscripted plays, guest troupes from across Colorado
- **Classes**: Four-level improv program from beginner to advanced
- **Drop-ins & Jams**: Open sessions for curious newcomers and practice for experienced improvisers
- **Testimonials highlight**: "90-minute plays 100% improvised," intelligent and hilarious performances
