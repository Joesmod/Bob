# Second Star to the Right Books

## Overview
Independent children's and young adult bookstore in Denver, Colorado. Community-focused bookstore specializing in books for children, middle grade, young adult, and picture books. Strong emphasis on community programming, local authors/illustrators, school partnerships, and book donations. Events use Humanitix (nonprofit ticketing platform) for RSVPs, but events are **free** to attend.

## Programming & Events
- **Event Types**: Author visits, storytimes, book club meetings, book fairs, seasonal celebrations
- **Frequency**: Regular calendar with 5-10 events per month
- **Format**: Free events with optional RSVP via Humanitix (charity ticketing platform)
- **Book Clubs**: Monthly adult book club meetings
- **Special Events**:
  - Dr. Seuss Storytime + Activity
  - World Storytelling Day
  - Grown Up Book Fair (at Fiction Beer Company)
  - Author launch parties
- **Community Focus**: Book donations, school programming, community partnerships, Colorado Book Awards

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 0/100 — Not Applicable**
This is an independent bookstore, not a ticketed venue. All events are free to attend. RSVPs are collected via Humanitix (a charity ticketing platform where fees go to nonprofits), but no admission is charged. Events are author readings, storytimes, and book clubs — standard bookstore community programming. No revenue from event tickets. Not a fit for Opendate. The business model is book sales with free events as community engagement and marketing.
