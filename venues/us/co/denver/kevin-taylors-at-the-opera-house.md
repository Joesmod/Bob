## About

Kevin Taylor's at the Opera House is a fine dining restaurant located in the Chambers Grant Salon inside the Ellie Caulkins Opera House building at the Denver Performing Arts Complex. Part of Kevin Taylor Restaurant Group (KTRG), which operates three restaurants in the Denver metro area including Hickory & Ash.

The restaurant serves pre-theater cocktails and dinner on select evenings when there are performances at the Ellie Caulkins Opera House, Buell Theater, and/or Boettcher Concert Hall. It is open prior to all evening Opera Colorado performances.
