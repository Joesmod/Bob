# Left Hand RiNo Drinks & Eats

## Overview
Brewery taproom, restaurant, and event space in Denver's RiNo (River North Art District) neighborhood, operated by Left Hand Brewing Company. Located next door to Mission Ballroom, making it a popular pre-show and post-show dining destination. Features Left Hand's award-winning beers, full bar, Neapolitan-style pizza, outdoor patio, and private event spaces.

## History
Opened by Left Hand Brewing Company (based in Longmont, CO) in the North Wynkoop development. Positioned as Denver's extension of the brewery brand, specifically located to serve Mission Ballroom concert-goers and RiNo district visitors.
