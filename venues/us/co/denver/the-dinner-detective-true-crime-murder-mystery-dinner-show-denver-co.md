## About

Franchise location of North America's largest interactive true crime murder mystery dinner show. Located in downtown Denver. Actors are not in costume and hidden among the audience, creating an immersive comedic detective experience. Each ticket includes full plated dinner, interactive show, and waitstaff gratuity. Features improvisation from top talent, keeping each performance fresh and spontaneous. High Google rating (4.8/5 with 391 reviews) indicates strong local popularity.

## History

Part of The Dinner Detective franchise founded November 6, 2004 by Scott and Kelly O'Brien. The brand operates in over 100 cities throughout North America with proprietary ticketing system across all locations.
