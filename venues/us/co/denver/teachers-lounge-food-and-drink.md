# Teachers' Lounge Food + Drink

## Overview
Restaurant and bar located inside The Slate Denver hotel in downtown Denver. Full-service dining with breakfast, brunch, lunch, and dinner. No ticketed entertainment venue.

## Venue Type
Hotel restaurant and lounge

## Concept
Modern American cuisine with Colorado influences. Serves hotel guests and local diners. Features craft cocktails, local ingredients, and contemporary takes on classic dishes.
