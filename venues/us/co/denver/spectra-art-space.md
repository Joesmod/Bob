# Spectra Art Space

## Overview
Contemporary art gallery and immersive experience venue in Denver. Owned and operated by 3 young women. Focus on highly curated contemporary art exhibitions, immersive art installations, art classes, music showcases, lectures, and demos. Known for "Spookadelia" seasonal immersive experience (Denver's Best Seasonal Immersive award winner).

## Programming & Shows
- **Primary offerings**: Art exhibitions (rotating monthly), immersive experiences (ticketed), workshops, opening receptions
- **Flagship**: Spookadelia: Unspoken (narrative-driven immersive art/theater experience, October-December, $20-30)
- **Recent shows**: 
  - "The Proof Is In The Print" (printmaking show)
  - "Lean On Love" art show
  - "And Meow This" (fully feline art show)
  - "Curated Cryptids" (mythical creatures)
- **Frequency**: Monthly rotating gallery shows, seasonal immersives
- **Ticket prices**: Immersive experiences $20-30, gallery openings often free
- **Hours**: Mon/Wed-Thurs 11am-9pm, Fri-Sat 11am-10pm, Sun 11am-9pm, Tues varies
