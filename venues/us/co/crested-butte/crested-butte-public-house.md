# Crested Butte Public House

## Overview
Multi-purpose venue in downtown Crested Butte, CO combining a pub/restaurant, live music venue, luxury lofts, and private event space. Colorado saloon heritage meets modern amenities. Features vintage 1955 Ford truck converted into a mobile bar with 5 draft taps.

## History
Built to honor Colorado's saloon heritage while offering modern charm. Has become a trusted gathering spot in Crested Butte's vibrant heart, hosting everything from weddings to film festivals to live music performances.
