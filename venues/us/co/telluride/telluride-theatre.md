# Telluride Theatre

## Overview
Educational community theater producing a year-round season of plays, musicals, Shakespeare, burlesque shows, and fringe performances. Registered 501(c)(3) non-profit offering opportunities for artists of all skill levels. Known for *Shakespeare in the Park*, The House of Shimmy Shake burlesque troupe, FRINGE Projects, and annual fundraisers. Welcomes "never-evers" to seasoned professionals.

## History
Founded from the merger of SquidShow Theatre (founded by Sasha Cucciniello) and Telluride Rep. Committed to advancing theatrical arts through creative performance, artistic development, and education. Mission focuses on truthful human connection, joyful celebration, and accessibility for all audiences.
