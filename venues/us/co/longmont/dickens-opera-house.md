## About

Built in 1881, Dickens Opera House is a historic Victorian-era venue in Longmont, Colorado. The venue features a stunning ballroom with soaring ceilings and elegant period details. Operated alongside The Passenger restaurant (adjoining property), with catering by Italic Catering.

**Business Model**: Primarily private events (weddings, corporate events, milestone celebrations) with occasional public entertainment bookings.
