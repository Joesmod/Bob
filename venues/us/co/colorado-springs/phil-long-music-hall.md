# Phil Long Music Hall at Bourbon Brothers

## Overview
Colorado Springs' premier intimate mid-size live music venue and event center. Capacity venue featuring national touring acts, tribute shows, comedy nights, and special events. Owned and operated by VENU Holding Corporation in partnership with Phil Long Dealerships.

## History
Established by VENU Holding Corporation (multi-venue operator) in partnership with Phil Long Dealerships (Colorado Springs automotive group). Part of VENU's portfolio of entertainment venues. Positioned as the Rocky Mountain front range's premier mid-size music venue.

## Key People
- **Ownership**: VENU Holding Corporation + Phil Long Dealerships
- **Parent company**: VENU (operates multiple entertainment brands)

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Tribute bands, touring acts, comedy, dance parties, country, rock, pop
- **Frequency**: 15-20 events per month
- **Age requirements**: Most shows 14+, some 18+
- **Ticket prices**: $13-$59+ typical

**Sample March 2026 programming:**
- Tribute shows: Van Halen, Elvis, John Denver, Eagles, Maroon 5, Pink Floyd, Grateful Dead
- Comedy nights
