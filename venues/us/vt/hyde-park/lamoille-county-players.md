# Lamoille County Players

## Overview
Community theater company operating in Hyde Park, Vermont. 74 years of history ("Hey, let's put on a show!" - founded circa 1952). Produces musical theater and plays in the historic Hyde Park Opera House. Volunteer-run community theater with ticketed productions.
