# Hyde Park Opera House

## Overview
Historic opera house in Hyde Park, Vermont, home to Lamoille County Players (LCP), a 74-year-old community theater organization. Presents musicals, plays, and youth productions year-round.

## History
Lamoille County Players was founded 74 years ago with the immortal words "Hey, let's put on a show!" Now operates out of the Hyde Park Opera House, presenting community theater productions and hosting touring shows.
