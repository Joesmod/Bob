## About

Historic opera house located on the second floor of Vergennes' City Hall in Vermont's smallest city. Managed entirely by the nonprofit Friends of the Vergennes Opera House (FVOH), a 100% volunteer organization dedicated to preserving the venue as a center for the arts and community activities.

Currently undergoing renovation as part of the **All Access Project**, with shows being presented "off stage" in venues around Vergennes during construction. First on-stage performance post-renovation: March 28, 2026.

## Mission & Vision

**Mission:** Preserve the historic Vergennes Opera House as a center for the presentation of the arts and other activities that promote and celebrate a sense of community.

**Vision:** "To be the glue that holds the spirit of the greater Vergennes community together."
