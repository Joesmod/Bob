# The Opera House at Enosburg Falls

## Overview
Historic community performing arts venue in Enosburg Falls, Vermont. Built in 1892, this Victorian-style opera house serves as a cultural center for Franklin County with exceptional acoustics. Listed on the National Historic Register.

## History
Presented to the Town of Enosburg and Village of Enosburg Falls in 1892 by the Dr. B.J. Kendall Company, a patent medicine manufacturer. Has hosted everything from John Philip Sousa conducting his own marches to contemporary performances. Underwent major restorations in the 1990s (roof, fire safety, exterior painting, interior updates, accessible bathrooms). Managed by Friends of the Opera House (501c3 nonprofit) since 1998.
