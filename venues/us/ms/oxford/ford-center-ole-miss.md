## Programming & Shows

Hosts a variety of events including:
- Touring Broadway shows
- Music concerts
- Theatre productions
- Community musicals
- Youth Music Theatre Workshop
- Speaker series and public affairs events

Box office sells tickets to most music and theatre events on campus (non-athletic events only).

## Opendate Fit

**Score: 20/100 — Not a Target / University Venue**

**Major Challenges:**
- **State university facility**: Owned and operated by University of Mississippi (public institution)
- **State procurement requirements**: Platform changes require RFP process, state approvals, budget cycles
- **Academic integration**: Ticketing tied to student services, faculty benefits, campus card systems
- **UniversityTickets**: Purpose-built for higher education venues (campus integration)
- **No decision authority**: Facilities/IT decisions made by university administration, not venue staff
- **Multi-year contracts**: Universities typically have 3-5 year vendor agreements

**Reality Check:**
University performing arts centers are institutional facilities, not independent venues. They need:
- Campus ID integration (student/faculty discounts)
- University financial system integration
- State procurement compliance
- Academic calendar coordination
- Grant/donor reporting (university foundation)

**Why they won't switch:**
- UniversityTickets is specifically designed for college/university venues
- Platform change would require approval from: venue director, university IT, procurement, finance, legal, possibly Board of Trustees
- Migration timeline: 12-18 months minimum
- Strong preference for vendors experienced with higher ed compliance

**Better targets:** Independent venues that can make their own purchasing decisions — NOT state university facilities.
