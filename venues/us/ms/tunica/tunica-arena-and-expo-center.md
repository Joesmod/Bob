# Tunica Arena and Expo Center

## Overview
Mississippi's largest indoor arena and expo center. 48,000 sq ft of climate-controlled space, seating for 5,000+, plus 150,000+ sq ft of covered pavilion space with 700+ stalls. Built in 2000 as a $25+ million facility. Hosts concerts, rodeos, horse shows, sporting events, livestock shows, trade expos, festivals, and community events. Welcomes hundreds of thousands of visitors annually.

## History
Opened in 2000, the Tunica Arena has become the South's premiere multi-purpose event facility. Located in Tunica County, MS, the venue serves as a hub for entertainment, agriculture, sports, commerce, and tourism, generating millions in economic impact for the region annually.
