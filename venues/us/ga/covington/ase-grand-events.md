# Ase Grand Events

## Overview
3,000 sq ft private event space rental facility in Covington, GA. Street-level, handicap accessible venue for parties, meetings, and corporate events. NOT a ticketed entertainment venue.

## Venue Type
Private event space rental - customers book the space for their own events (weddings, birthday parties, business meetings, pop-up shops).

## Programming & Shows
- **No ticketed shows**
- **No public events**
- Space is rented out to private parties
- Uses HoneyBook for rental bookings

## Pricing
Rental packages:
- **Bronze**: $950 (6 hours, up to 70 guests)
- **Silver**: $1,350 (includes uplighting, projector)
- **Gold**: $2,800 (12 hours, up to 100 guests)
- **Hourly**: $150/hr (3 hour minimum)
