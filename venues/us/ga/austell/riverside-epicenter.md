# Riverside EpiCenter

## Overview
113,000 square foot multi-use facility in Austell, GA (Atlanta metro area). Riverside EpiCenter is a comprehensive lifestyle destination combining an upscale fitness club, professional coworking space with café, recording studio, private bowling alley and gaming center, and event spaces. Primarily operates as an event rental venue for corporate events, weddings, stage plays, film productions, sporting events, and private parties.

## Programming & Shows
- **Event Types**: Corporate events, award ceremonies, weddings, stage plays, film productions, sporting events, charity fundraisers, holiday and birthday parties
- **Community Events**: Free fitness classes (Hip-Hop & R&B Cardio Dance), networking mixers (55+ Mixer), speaking events (EPIC Talks)
- **Model**: Event rental venue + community programming
- **Ticketing**: Free community events observed; rental-based for private events

Website shows upcoming free community events (dance classes, mixers, speaking events) but primary business model appears to be event space rental rather than ticketed public performances.
