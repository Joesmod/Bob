# Westside Cultural Arts Center

## Overview
Versatile special event venue and art gallery in West Midtown Atlanta. Originally designed for private events (weddings, corporate functions), but also hosts ticketed public events including concerts, burlesque shows, and holiday parties. Industrial-modern space with original cement columns, full-service catering kitchen, custom sound/light system, two separate event spaces, and on-site parking.

## History
Founded in 2014 by Dr. James L. Chappuis with a vision to foster arts and cultural community in West Midtown Atlanta. The space serves as both a living gallery and special events venue.
