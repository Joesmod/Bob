## About

Out Front Theatre Company exists to tell stories of the LGBTQIA+ experience and community through theatre and the performing arts. The organization is Georgia's dedicated LGBTQIA+ theatre, providing representation and visibility for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, questioning, intersex, and asexual stories and artists.
