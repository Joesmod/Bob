# WesBanco Amphitheater

## Overview
Outdoor amphitheater located within the Sports Complex at 176 Park View Avenue in Mt. Washington, Kentucky. Part of the city's Parks and Recreation department facilities. Available for rental and community events.

## History
Built as part of Mt. Washington's Community Sports Complex. Managed by the City of Mt. Washington Parks and Recreation department.
