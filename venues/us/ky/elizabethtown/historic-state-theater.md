# The Historic State Theater Complex

## Overview
Historic 1942 Art Deco theater in downtown Elizabethtown, Kentucky. Fully restored with modern amenities, the theater serves as the cornerstone of the downtown revitalization. Operated by the City of Elizabethtown as a full rental facility and event venue.

## History
Built in 1942 with original Art Deco décor. The theater has been fully restored to reflect its 1942 design while adding modern amenities. The venue is now a key component of Elizabethtown's downtown revitalization efforts.
