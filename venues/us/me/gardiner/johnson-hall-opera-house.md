# Johnson Hall Opera House

## Overview
Maine's oldest opera house (since 1864) and a 501(c)(3) nonprofit providing world-class entertainment and performing arts education to the Kennebec River Valley. Currently undergoing renovation to create an accessible 400-seat theater with additional conference space. Strong commitment to arts education and community cultural development.

## History
Originally built as a livery stable in 1864, Johnson Hall has served as a hub of entertainment in Kennebec County for 150+ years. Historic third-floor opera house is being renovated to modernize while preserving historic character. Plans include accessibility upgrades and expanded versatility for business meetings and conferences.
