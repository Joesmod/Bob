## About

Historic 840-seat opera house on the second floor of Skowhegan's Municipal Building. Since July 1, 2024, the venue has been leased and operated by **Lights Up Productions**, a nonprofit dedicated to bringing professional theater, concerts, and community productions to central Maine. The venue hosts community theater, concerts, ghost tours, and special events throughout the year.

## History

Historic opera house that has served Skowhegan for over a century. The Town of Skowhegan holds its annual Town Meeting here each year. In 2024, the venue entered a new era when Lights Up Productions took over operations, bringing renewed energy and a full season of programming. Lights Up is actively fundraising to add new public bathrooms, a glass-enclosed stairway/fire escape, backstage dressing rooms, and two new elevators.
