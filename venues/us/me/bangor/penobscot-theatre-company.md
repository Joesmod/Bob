# Penobscot Theatre Company

## Overview
Professional theater company in downtown Bangor, Maine, now in its 52nd season. Home to a 200+ seat theater presenting plays, musicals, and educational programming year-round. Also runs the Dramatic Academy providing theater education for all ages.

## History
Founded in 1973 (celebrating 52nd season in 2025-26). Has been a cornerstone of Maine's performing arts scene for over five decades, dedicated to diversity, equity, and inclusion in storytelling.
