# Brockton Symphony Orchestra

## Overview
Community symphony orchestra based in Brockton, MA. Performs seasonal concert series at Christ Congregational Church and hosts free chamber music concerts at Brockton Public Library. Supported by Massachusetts Cultural Council and local cultural councils. Not a venue — a performing arts organization that rents performance spaces.

## History
Long-standing community orchestra serving the greater Brockton area. Supported by grants from Brockton, Abington, Easton, Mansfield, Sharon, Stoughton, and Taunton Cultural Councils, all funded by the Massachusetts Cultural Council.

## Programming & Shows
- **Type**: Classical symphony concerts, chamber music series
- **Main Concert Venue**: Christ Congregational Church, Brockton
- **Chamber Series**: Brockton Public Library Main Branch (304 Main Street, Brockton) — 2nd Floor Circulation Lobby, Saturdays at 2pm
- **Frequency**: ~3-4 main concerts per season, monthly free chamber concerts
- **Youth Competition**: Annual youth competition featuring prize-winning soloists
