# Lowell Memorial Auditorium

## Overview
Large 2,800-seat historic venue in downtown Lowell, MA featuring concerts, Broadway shows, festivals, sporting events, weddings, and corporate events. Operated by Lowell Management Group. Versatile main floor configuration allows for theater seating, round tables (up to 50 tables of 10), or trade show booths (up to 90). Mix of classic architecture and modern amenities.

## Facilities
**Capacity**: 2,800 seats
**Main floor versatility**:
- Theater-style seating, OR
- Up to 50 round tables of 10, OR  
- Up to 90 trade show booths

**Venue style**: Historic theater with classic ambiance, ideal for weddings, concerts, and corporate events

## Programming & Shows
**Concert series**: Amy Grant, The Righteous Brothers, Judy Collins, Air Supply, and more
**Broadway Series**: Legally Blonde, Stomp, Rent, Annie, Chicago
**Holiday shows**: The Nutcracker, A Charlie Brown Christmas, Boston Holiday Pops
**Annual festivals**: 
- Lowell Irish Festival (September)
- Lowell Winterfest (February)
**Sporting events**: Annual Golden Gloves boxing (January-March, sells out every year; proceeds benefit Lowell Area Charities)

**Programming mix**: Concerts, Broadway touring productions, holiday performances, cultural festivals, boxing
