# Somerville Music Spaces

## Overview
Boutique rehearsal studio facility in Somerville, MA (Boston area) offering 8 rehearsal studios and a 650 sq ft performance room. Musician-owned small business catering to classical, jazz, and acoustic musicians. Provides hourly studio rentals for lessons, practice, rehearsals, recitals, masterclasses, and recordings. Performance room seats up to 60 guests.

## History
Recently established facility created by musicians for musicians. Designed specifically for teaching, practice, group rehearsals, and intimate performances with professional acoustic treatment and sound isolation.

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 50/100 — Low-Medium Priority**

**Profile:**
Small boutique rental facility with occasional ticketed events. Currently using multiple ticketing platforms (Humanitix, Zeffy, Eventbrite, etc.) on an event-by-event basis - suggests lack of unified system.

**Pros:**
- Multiple ticketing platforms = fragmented solution (pain point)
- Regular monthly programming (BCCM open mics)
- Active event calendar with diverse presenters
- Growing venue with partnerships
- Clean, professional operation

**Cons:**
- Primary business model is studio rentals, not ticketed events
- Very small capacity (60 max)
- Low ticket prices ($20 typical)
- Many events are free or organized by external groups
- Volume too low to justify platform investment

**Assessment:**
Better suited for simple booking tools than full ticketing platform. Current approach (using free/low-cost platforms like Humanitix and Zeffy for occasional events) is probably optimal for their scale. Opendate would be overkill unless they significantly expand public programming.

**Opportunity:**
If they want to grow public events and unify their booking/ticketing across multiple presenters using the space, there could be a fit. But as a rehearsal studio with occasional events, they're not the ideal customer profile.
