## About

The Comedy Scene is a stand-up comedy club located at Patriot Place in Foxborough, MA (adjacent to Gillette Stadium). Part of the John Tobin Presents network of comedy venues across New England and the Midwest, the club brings national touring comedians to the Foxborough area.

## History

Operated by John Tobin Presents, a Boston-based comedy production company with over 30 years of experience in live comedy. John Tobin (former Boston City Councilor, now VP of City & Community Affairs at Northeastern University) founded the company and expanded to multiple markets throughout New England.
