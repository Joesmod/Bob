## History

Since 1938, The Cantab Lounge has been a Cambridge institution and the heart of live music in the area. For over 85 years, it has served as a launching pad for local bands and a stop for touring acts. The venue is known as "Cambridge's Legendary Venue" and has maintained its authentic dive bar character while hosting quality live music every night of the week.

Notable artists who have played early shows at the Cantab include Mt. Joy and Michael Marcagi.
