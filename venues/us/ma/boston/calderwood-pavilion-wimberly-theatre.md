# Calderwood Pavilion Wimberly Theatre

## Overview
One of two major venues operated by The Huntington Theatre Company, Boston's leading professional theater company. The Calderwood Pavilion is a state-of-the-art facility located at 527 Tremont St. in the South End, opened in 2004.

## History
The Huntington Theatre Company was founded in 1982. The Calderwood Pavilion opened in 2004 as part of major expansion. In 2013, The Huntington received the Regional Theatre Tony Award. The organization has presented over 250 plays including 140+ premieres, with 18 moving to Broadway or Off-Broadway. Has served more than 3.5 million audience members and half a million students.

## Facilities
The Huntington operates two venues:
- **The Huntington Theatre**: 264 Huntington Ave (historic venue, restored/reopened 2022)
- **Calderwood Pavilion**: 527 Tremont St. (state-of-the-art, opened 2004)

Both venues active with robust programming.
