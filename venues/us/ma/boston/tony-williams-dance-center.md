# Tony Williams Dance Center

## Overview
Dance school in Boston offering graded ballet programs and classes in multiple dance styles for children and adults. Produces annual performances including the Urban Nutcracker. **Not a ticketing venue** — operates as a dance education institution with occasional recitals and performances.

## History
Founded by Tony Williams, who discovered dance as a means of self-expression and personal development during adolescence. The school was established to provide quality dance instruction to a diverse urban community in Greater Boston, emphasizing cultural education and physical/mental discipline through dance.

## Programming & Dance Offerings
- **Classes**: Ballet (graded program), Folklorico, Hip-Hop, Salsa, Modern, Lyrical, Flamenco, Jazz, Tap
- **Students**: Children and adults from diverse Greater Boston communities
- **Performances**: Annual recital, Urban Nutcracker (partnership with City Ballet of Boston)
- **Summer Programs**: Dance camps (Aug, Ages 5-14)

## Urban Nutcracker
Partnership with City Ballet of Boston for annual holiday production. Modern, multicultural interpretation of the classic ballet featuring Duke Ellington, Tchaikovsky, and fusion of classical and contemporary dance styles. Performed at external venues, ticketed through City Ballet of Boston.
