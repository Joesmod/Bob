## History

The Wang Theatre is a historic theater in Boston's Theater District. Built in the 1920s, it was originally the Metropolitan Theatre and later became known as the Wang Center for the Performing Arts. It is now part of the Boch Center (formerly the Citi Performing Arts Center).
