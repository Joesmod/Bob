## About

Part of the legendary Middle East Restaurant & Nightclub complex in Cambridge/Boston. ZuZu is one of the venue's smaller rooms within the historic multi-room complex. Known for intimate live music, DJs, and electronic music events. 21+ venue with regular weekly programming.

## History

Part of The Middle East complex, a Boston-area institution for live music since the 1980s. The Middle East features multiple rooms (Upstairs, Downstairs, Corner, and ZuZu) hosting everyone from up-and-coming acts to major touring artists. ZuZu focuses on DJs, electronic music, and dance events.
