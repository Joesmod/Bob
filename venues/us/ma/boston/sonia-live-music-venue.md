# The Middle East - Sonia

## Overview
Sonia is a 350-capacity live music room within The Middle East Restaurant & Nightclub complex, one of Cambridge's most iconic music venues. The Middle East complex features multiple performance spaces (Downstairs-575 cap, Sonia-350 cap, Upstairs-194 cap, ZuZu-100 cap) hosting rock, indie, punk, electronic, and emerging artists nearly every night.

## History
The Middle East has been a cornerstone of the Cambridge/Boston music scene for decades, located in Central Square. The venue complex is famous for launching careers of major acts and maintaining a strong DIY/indie ethos while also hosting established touring acts.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Rock, indie, punk, electronic, hip-hop, alternative, experimental
- **Frequency**: 4-6 shows per night across all rooms
- **Age Policy**: Mix of 18+, 21+, and all-ages shows
- **Ticket prices**: $10-$35 typical range
- **Notable feature**: Multiple rooms allow simultaneous shows at different capacity/price points

Active calendar with ticketed shows every night. The venue is known for both up-and-coming bands and established indie/alternative acts.

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 68/100 — Medium Lead**

Established, high-volume multi-room venue complex using TicketWeb (Ticketmaster). Very active calendar (20+ shows per week across all rooms). However, Ticketmaster/TicketWeb contracts are typically long-term and switching costs are high.

**Challenges**:
- Existing TicketWeb relationship likely locked in
- Multi-room operation requires sophisticated system
- High show volume means ticketing is business-critical

**Opportunity**: If they're unhappy with TicketWeb fees or service, the multi-room setup is actually perfect for Opendate's platform. Would be a marquee client if convertible. Worth monitoring for contract renewal windows.

**Approach**: Long-term relationship building. Not an immediate switch candidate, but valuable market intelligence and potential future opportunity.
