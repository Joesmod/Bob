# LSU Student Union Theater

## Overview
University-operated 1,200-seat proscenium theater on the LSU campus. Primarily a rental venue for campus events, performing arts groups, and external performances. Features stadium-style seating and the Meyers Constellation System for acoustical enhancement.

## History
Part of the LSU Student Union complex, the theater serves as the primary performing arts venue for Louisiana State University in Baton Rouge.
