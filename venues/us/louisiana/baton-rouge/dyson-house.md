# Dyson House Listening Room at Zeeland Street Market

## Overview
Intimate grassroots listening room located inside Zeeland Street Market, a 28-year-old food and beverage establishment in Baton Rouge. Founded to enhance the arts, education and cultural scene while providing a space for local, regional and national musical acts. Has hosted over 600 shows featuring diverse genres from singer-songwriters to indie bands and traditional music.

## History
Founded by John Burns and Kevin Sweeney, both Baton Rouge natives who met in high school and share a love for music. Partnership with Baton Rouge Community College's Entertainment Technologies degree program provides on-the-job training for students.
