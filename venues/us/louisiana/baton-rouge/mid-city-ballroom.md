# Mid City Ballroom

## Overview
Unique live music venue in Baton Rouge converted from a former church building. Combines a concert venue with a sell/trade/consignment musical instrument and record store (Baton Rouge Music Exchange). The venue occupies the former church sanctuary while the social hall houses the music store. Focus on independent and alternative music in an intimate setting.

## History
Opened in 2017 to bring together music, art, and culture under one roof. The founders found a former church building in the center of Baton Rouge and transformed it into a dual-purpose space - live music venue where people once prayed and married, and a music store in the former social hall.
