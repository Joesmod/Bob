## About

American Players Theatre (APT) is a professional classical theater company specializing in Shakespeare and classic works. Performances take place in an outdoor amphitheater surrounded by 110 acres of wooded hillside in Spring Green, Wisconsin. Founded in 1977, APT has become one of the nation's foremost classical theater companies.

## History

Founded in 1977 by Randall Duk Kim, Anne Occhiogrosso, and Charles Bright. Built on a wooded hillside near Frank Lloyd Wright's Taliesin, the outdoor amphitheater offers a unique theatrical experience blending professional classical performance with natural beauty.
