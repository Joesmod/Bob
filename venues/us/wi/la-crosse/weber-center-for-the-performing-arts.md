## About

Weber Center for the Performing Arts is a collaborative venue supporting La Crosse Community Theatre and Viterbo University. The facility serves as both a performance and educational space, hosting theater productions, concerts, and community events. The venue features the Lyche Theatre (main auditorium), Veterans Studio Theatre, Dahl Lobby for events, Cleary Terrace, and classroom suites.

## History

Built as a collaboration between La Crosse Community Theatre and Viterbo University to serve as a community performing arts hub. Named after donors Don & Roxanne Weber, with the Lyche Theatre named after donors John and Linda Lyche.
