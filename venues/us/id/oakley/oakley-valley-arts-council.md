# Oakley Valley Arts Council (OVAC)

## Overview
Community theater operating in the historic 1907 Howells Opera House in Oakley, Idaho. Non-profit organization producing musical and theatrical productions year-round plus annual Christmas concerts, powered by devoted volunteers.

## History
Operating in the 1907 Howells Opera House located 20 miles south of Burley in South Central Idaho's Snake River plains. Recent major restoration work:
- 2023: Complete roof truss renovation (saved the building)
- 2024: Air conditioning installed
- 2024: Entryway remodeled for wheelchair accessibility
- 2025: Ongoing sound and light system upgrades

**Planned upgrades**: Brick repairs, new theater seats, facade update, parking lot improvements.

3. Maintain and improve the historic Howells Opera House property
