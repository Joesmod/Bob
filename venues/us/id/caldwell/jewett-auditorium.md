Performing arts venue located on the campus of the College of Idaho in Caldwell. Operated by Caldwell Fine Arts (CFA), a nonprofit organization that presents a season of concerts, performances, and educational programs featuring classical music, folk, world music, and other performing arts.

## History

Caldwell Fine Arts has been presenting performances at Jewett Auditorium on the College of Idaho campus for many years. The organization focuses on bringing professional touring artists to the Caldwell/Treasure Valley community while also operating extensive educational outreach programs.

## Programming & Shows

CFA presents a seasonal concert series at Jewett Auditorium featuring:

- **Classical Music**: Piano recitals, chamber music, orchestral performances
- **Folk & Americana**: Touring folk ensembles, singer-songwriters
- **World Music**: Celtic, Quebecois, international artists
- **Educational Programs**: School outreach, CFA Kids programs, volunteer opportunities
- **Season Structure**: 2025-2026 season includes shows like Take3 (rock meets classical), Bon Débarras (Quebecois folk), and Truman Walker (classical piano)
- **Ticket Prices**: Typically $18-$35 for adults, $9-$19 for children (includes fees)
- **Season Packages**: "Create Your Own" packages offering 25% discount when purchasing 4+ shows
