# Boise Contemporary Theater

## Overview
Professional contemporary theater in Boise producing thought-provoking, original works. Runs mainstage season plus Theater Lab programs featuring new plays. Recently expanded into Academy for Contemporary Theater (A.C.T.) with classes and playwriting programs.

## History
BCT has established itself as Boise's home for contemporary, challenging theater that examines perspectives and human experiences. Known for producing original works and hosting Theater Lab showcases of new plays.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Contemporary theater, original works, new plays
- **Season Theme**: 25|26 Season "Where in the world am I?" - exploring human experience across locations and time
- **Theater Lab**: Fall (Oct) and Spring (May) performances - 2 shows back-to-back, ~80 mins
- **Frequency**: Full season Oct-May plus special programs
- **Ticket prices**: Tiered pricing with member benefits
- **Notable**: Focus on thought-provoking, original contemporary work
