# Watson's Mystery Café and Spirits

## Overview
Interactive mystery dinner theater and entertainment venue in Boise, ID. Combines murder mystery shows with escape room experiences ("Homicided Escape Rooms") and hosts third-party concerts and events. Full bar and pub menu.

## History
Established as Boise's premier interactive mystery theater venue. Features a unique blend of improvisational theater, escape room challenges, and live entertainment in a cafe/bar setting.

## Programming & Shows
- **Mystery Dinner Theater**: Interactive improv murder mystery shows with audience participation
- **Homicided Escape Rooms**: Pirate-themed CSI/escape room hybrid with pub meals, 2-4 person capacity, "Expert" difficulty level available
- **Third-Party Events**: 
  - Boise Blues Society All Ages Blues Jam (monthly, last Sunday)
  - "Raise the Dead" musical jam sessions featuring Rebecca Pearce & Whiskey Tango
  - "Hunks The Show" entertainment events
  - Various live music events (blues, country rock, funk)
- **Frequency**: Multiple events weekly including monthly recurring events
- **Hiring**: Actively seeking improv artists, servers, and bartenders
