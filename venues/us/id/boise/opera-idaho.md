## About

Opera Idaho is a professional opera company producing fully staged operas, concerts, educational programs, and community events across Idaho. They perform at multiple venues including the Egyptian Theatre and Idaho Shakespeare Festival. The organization also runs Critical Mass Vocal Artists (CMVA) and children's choruses, bringing opera to thousands of people each year. Member of Opera America.

## History

Established opera company serving the Boise community and broader Idaho region. Administrative office at 515 South 9th Street, Boise.
