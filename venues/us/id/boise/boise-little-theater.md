# Boise Little Theater

## Overview
Idaho's longest-running community theater, now in its 78th season. Volunteer-driven nonprofit producing 12+ shows annually across two venues: historic mainstage theater and newer Black Box Theater. Serves 12,000+ patrons per year.

## History
Founded in 1948, BLT has been producing quality amateur theater in Boise for 77+ years. Recently added a Black Box Theater (2nd season) to complement their historic mainstage, expanding their programming capacity.
