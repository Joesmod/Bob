## About

The Sapphire Room is Boise's premier listening room, described as "the best kept secret between Austin and Portland." This intimate 150-seat venue at The Riverside Hotel features state-of-the-art sound and lighting, table-side service with a full bar and dinner menu during performances, and regular shows by local and touring artists. The venue is all-ages and family-friendly, operating year-round with performances every day of the year across their three music venues.

## History

Part of The Riverside Hotel, a Treasure Valley icon nestled on 14 acres along the Boise River. The hotel features multiple music venues including The Sapphire Room (premier listening room), BAR365 (lobby bar with nightly local musicians), and The Sandbar (seasonal outdoor patio bar along the Greenbelt). The Sapphire Room has established itself as the region's top venue for intimate performances.
