# Stomping Ground Comedy Theater & Training Center

## Overview
Nonprofit 501(c)3 comedy theater and training center in Dallas. The only nonprofit theater in Dallas dedicated to comedy. Hosts improv shows, stand-up comedy, and musical improv performances while also running comedy classes for adults and youth. Operating capacity around 200 people per show.

## History
Established as Dallas' first nonprofit comedy theater. Grown from hosting a few events per month to producing hundreds of events per year. Expanded educational programming to include therapeutic improv programs for special needs populations.

## Educational Programs
- **Adult Classes**: Improv, stand-up, sketch comedy classes
- **Youth Programs**: Youth improv classes and house teams (ages vary)
- **Improv for Work**: Corporate team-building and organizational psychology training
- **Improv for Life**: Therapeutic improv programs including:
  - Classes for caregivers of Alzheimer's/dementia patients
  - Classes for people with anxiety
  - Classes for people on the autism spectrum
  - Neurodiverse dating and social programs
- **Internship Program**: 12-15 hours/month weekend internships
