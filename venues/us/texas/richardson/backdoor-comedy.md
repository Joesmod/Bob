# Backdoor Comedy Club

## Overview
Stand-up comedy club in Richardson, Texas (Dallas–Fort Worth area). Voted "Best Comedy Club" by D Magazine and Dallas Observer. Traditional comedy club format with weekend showcases, regular shows, and open mic nights. Focuses on local Dallas-area talent with occasional special headliner engagements.

## History
Established comedy club with recognition from local press (D Magazine, Dallas Observer "Best Comedy Club"). Known for having the "longest-running open mic night in the southwest."
