# Bastrop Opera House

## Overview
Historic community theater in downtown Bastrop, operating since 1889. Presents full theatrical seasons including musicals, dramas, and comedies. Also runs the Bastrop Opera House Academy for youth arts education. Recently acquired adjacent building for expansion.

## History
Built in 1889, the Bastrop Opera House has served as a cultural beacon in Central Texas for over a century. Originally a gathering place for vaudeville, music, and traveling productions, the venue has been restored and now presents a vibrant season of live theater.
