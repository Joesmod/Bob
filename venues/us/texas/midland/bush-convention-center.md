# Bush Convention Center

## Overview
Convention facility in Midland, TX owned by the City of Midland and operated by the Midland Chamber of Commerce. Opened in 2019 with Centennial Park following in 2020. Multi-purpose event space hosting business meetings, concerts, weddings, and conventions.

## History
Officially opened in 2019 as the Barbara & George H.W. Bush Convention Center. Named after the 41st President of the United States and his wife, who had ties to Midland.
