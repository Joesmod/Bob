# Granbury Opera House

## Overview
Community theatre company performing in the historic Granbury Opera House, which underwent a $3.5 million renovation in 2012. Presents musicals, plays, tribute concerts, and children's series. Also operates Granbury Theatre Academy for youth education.

## History
The Historic Granbury Opera House is a beautifully restored venue that became a state-of-the-art facility in 2012 after extensive renovation. Now home to Granbury Theatre Company's season of live productions.
