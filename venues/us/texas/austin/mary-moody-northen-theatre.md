# Mary Moody Northen Theatre

## Overview
University theater at St. Edward's University in Austin, presenting a season of student and professional productions. Part of the university's Theatre Arts program. Primarily serves educational mission with ticketed public performances.

## History
Mary Moody Northen Theatre is the performance venue for St. Edward's University's Theatre Arts program in Austin, TX. Named for benefactor Mary Moody Northen.
