# Theatre Under The Stars (TUTS)

## Overview
Major professional musical theatre company in Houston, founded in 1968. Presents Broadway touring productions and original musicals at the Hobby Center for the Performing Arts. Offers subscription packages, youth programs, and educational initiatives. A well-established regional theater with sophisticated ticketing infrastructure.

## History
Founded in 1968 by Frank M. Young to bring timeless classics and bold new musical theatre to Greater Houston. Now in its 6th decade, TUTS has evolved from outdoor park performances to a major regional theater presenting Broadway-caliber productions at the Hobby Center.
