# Morrison Theater (Rice University)

## Overview
Academic performance venue at Rice University's Shepherd School of Music. Part of the Brockman Hall for Opera building. Hosts student recitals, faculty concerts, and chamber music performances. Most events are free and open to the public.

## History
Part of Rice University's Shepherd School of Music, a conservatory-style music program within a leading research university.
