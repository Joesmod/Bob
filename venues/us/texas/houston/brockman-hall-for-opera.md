# Brockman Hall for Opera (Rice University)

## Overview
Academic opera and performance venue at Rice University's Shepherd School of Music. Houses Morrison Theater, Patrons' Suite, and Dean's Rehearsal Hall. Hosts student opera productions, faculty performances, and chamber music. Most events are free and open to the public.

## History
Part of Rice University's Shepherd School of Music, one of the nation's leading conservatory-style music programs within a research university.
