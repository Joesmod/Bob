## About

Baltimore's most unique entertainment experience! A completely immersive, interactive comedy magic show set inside a 1920s Art Deco speakeasy bar. Every Friday and Saturday night, resident entertainer Spencer Horsman presents a 90+ minute show blending comedy, sleight of hand, mentalism, and danger in an intimate setting where every guest becomes part of the action. Voted "Best First Date Bar" and rated one of the top entertainment experiences in the city by Google, TripAdvisor, and Yelp.

Located just south of Baltimore's Inner Harbor, the venue features hand-crafted cocktails and an evolving menu of house specials and classics. Guests welcome to bring their own food during pre-show cocktail hour and intermission.

## History

Established 2007 (18 years in operation as of 2026). Run by the Horsman family for generations. Over 1,000 five-star reviews accumulated over nearly two decades of performances.
