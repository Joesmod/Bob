# Baltimore Center Stage

## Overview
Major nonprofit professional theater company and State Theater of Maryland. Produces plays and theatrical productions across two mainstages and a 99-seat intimate theater. Founded in 1963, this is a THEATER company, not a music venue.

## History
Founded in 1963 and designated the State Theater of Maryland in 1978. Over 60 years of producing professional theater in Baltimore. Focuses on bold new plays, reinterpreted classics, education programs, and community engagement.
