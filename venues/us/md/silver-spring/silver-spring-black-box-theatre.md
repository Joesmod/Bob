## About

Montgomery County's premier black box theater located in downtown Silver Spring, just two blocks from the Red Line's Silver Spring Metro station. Features a 25' x 25' flexible playing area with seating for up to 156 guests. The venue offers an intimate connection between performers and audiences, hosting theatre, concerts, comedy, improv, dance, spoken word, and arts-based events. Montgomery County facility, fully ADA compliant.

Managed by the Theatre Consortium of Silver Spring, a partnership dedicated to supporting innovation, accessibility, and the performing arts.

## History

Managed by the Theatre Consortium of Silver Spring. Received Gold Seal of Approval from Candid-Guidestar for 2024. Supported by Montgomery County Government, Arts & Humanities Council of Montgomery County, and Maryland State Arts Council.
