# Robert Z. Hawkins Amphitheater

## Overview
Unique outdoor venue with intimate garden atmosphere located within Bartley Ranch Regional Park at 6000 Bartley Ranch Road, Reno. Operated by Washoe County Parks. Maximum capacity 1,000 people (403 permanent seats + grass seating for 500-600). Full stage building with dressing rooms, restrooms, and electricity.

## History
Located in Bartley Ranch Regional Park, tucked beneath scenic Windy Hill. Part of a historic park that includes the Huffaker School, Western Heritage Interpretive Center, horse arena, and Flying B picnic pavilion. Named after Robert Z. Hawkins.

## Programming & Shows
- **Types**: Concerts, award ceremonies, film premieres, plays, corporate meetings, fundraisers
- **Season**: Summer concert schedule (2025 schedule available)
- **Setting**: Outdoor garden amphitheater
- **Capacity**: 403 permanent seats + 500-600 grass seating, max 1,000 total
- **Grass Seating**: Patrons bring blankets and low back folding chairs
- **Restrictions**: No pets, no glass bottles in amphitheater

## Technical Capabilities
- **Audio/Lighting/Video**: Contracted exclusively through Starsound Audio Inc. (local company, 30+ years in Reno/Sparks)
- **Requirements**: All large public events require at least one Starsound engineer + lighting technician for after-dark events
- **Stage**: Full stage building with dressing rooms
- **Extras**: Merchandise/autograph space opposite ticket booth
- **Supplements**: Tents, chairs, tables, risers, flags, podiums available on request

## Rental Information
- **Minimum**: 6 hours
- **Performance Rate**: $250/hour
- **Non-performance Rate**: $130/hour (6 hour minimum)
- **Deposit**: $550 refundable cleaning/security deposit
- **Advance Booking**: 30 days minimum, up to 1 year ahead
- **Required**: Completed performance questionnaire to reserveparks@washoecounty.gov
- **Security**: All large public events require 2 security personnel via Allied Universal Security Services
- **Additional Fees**: Ticketing, special lighting, and sound incur extra charges
