# Majestic Repertory Theatre

## Overview
Independent theater company in Downtown Las Vegas Arts District. Founded in 2016, Majestic Rep produces immersive and experimental theatrical productions including world premieres, regional premieres, and classic revivals. Known for innovative stagings and inclusive programming.

## History
Established fall 2016 at ALIOS arts space in the Downtown Las Vegas Arts District. Named in honor of the historic Majestic Theatre on Fremont Street, which was active in the early 1900s as a vaudeville house and cinema. The company has grown to become a significant voice in Las Vegas theater, producing 8-12 shows per year.
