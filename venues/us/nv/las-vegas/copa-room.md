## About

Copa Room is the main showroom at Tuscany Suites & Casino in Las Vegas. The venue hosts a mix of tribute shows, live music, and comedy performances. Winner of "Best Showroom/Live Venue" (Best of Las Vegas), the Copa Room offers an intimate setting for entertainment with regular nightly shows and special weekend events.

## History

Located within Tuscany Suites & Casino, the Copa Room has established itself as a quality entertainment venue for both locals and visitors. The venue is part of a larger casino complex that includes the award-winning Piazza Lounge (Best Piano Bar - 6 years running).
