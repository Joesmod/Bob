# Las Vegas Live Comedy Club

## Overview
Stand-up comedy show venue operated by V Theater Productions inside Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino's Miracle Mile Shops. Part of a multi-showroom complex with four theaters. Features rotating stand-up comedians from HBO, Showtime, and Comedy Central. This is a resident production show, not a venue for external bookings.

## History
Part of V Theater's entertainment complex on the Las Vegas Strip. V Theater operates multiple venues within Miracle Mile Shops at Planet Hollywood, presenting ongoing production shows and entertainment experiences.
