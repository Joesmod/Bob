# The Vista Center for the Arts

## Overview
Premier performing arts venue located on the campus of Valley Vista High School in Surprise, Arizona. Modern auditorium with advanced sound, lighting, and staging systems. Hosts concerts, theatrical productions, dance performances, civic events, and community gatherings.

## History
Opened in 2008, originally developed by Dysart Unified School District to support arts education and provide students with professional-caliber performance space. Served dual purpose as educational resource and community performance venue for over a decade.

In November 2025, a new operational partnership was formally approved between the City of Surprise, Dysart Unified School District, and Surprise Arts Foundation. This marks a significant evolution in operations and programming, expanding its role as a shared community asset.
