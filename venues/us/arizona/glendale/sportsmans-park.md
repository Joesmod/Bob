# Sportsman's Park

## Overview
Multi-purpose event venue located at 9600 W Sportsman's Park South in Glendale, Arizona, adjacent to State Farm Stadium (home of the Arizona Cardinals). Managed by Insignia Events. Features indoor and outdoor spaces totaling over 200,000 square feet across multiple event areas.

## Facilities
- **Heritage at Sportsman's Park**: Multi-story event building with full kitchen, 360-degree views, private event garden with arbors, fire pits, lounge areas
- **The Great Lawn**: 201,128 sq ft of green space surrounded by trees, ideal for weddings, music festivals, team building
- **South Lawn**: Permanent fencing, shade structures, drivable paths, equipped for publicly ticketed events or private functions
- **Cardinals Practice Fields**: Two NFL-ready practice fields totaling 190 yards, used by Arizona Cardinals
- **Car Parks**: 14,000 parking spaces across multiple lots
