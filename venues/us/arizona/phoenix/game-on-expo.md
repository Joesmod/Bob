# Game On Expo

## Overview
Annual gaming and anime convention produced by Game On Entertainment LLC. Takes place at the Phoenix Convention Center – North Building. Multi-day event featuring exhibitor hall, programming, and VIP access options.

**Important**: This is an EVENT PRODUCER/ORGANIZER, not a venue. They rent Phoenix Convention Center for their annual expo.
