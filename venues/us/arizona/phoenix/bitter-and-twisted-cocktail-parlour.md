# Bitter & Twisted Cocktail Parlour

## Overview
Award-winning cocktail bar located in the historic Luhrs Building in downtown Phoenix, formerly the Arizona Prohibition Headquarters. Recognized internationally for innovative cocktails and exceptional hospitality. Seated establishment, 21+ only.

## History
Opened in 2014 in the historic Luhrs Building. Since opening, has become one of the most acclaimed cocktail bars in North America. Known for bringing the original Pornstar Martini recipe to the United States, prepared according to the 2004 LAB Bar London recipe.
