# Colonial Oak Music Park

## Overview
Historic outdoor music venue in downtown St. Augustine, FL with seating for 250+ visitors. Set under St. Augustine's second-oldest sweeping live oak tree in an intimate amphitheater setting. Multi-faceted venue serving as music venue, private event space, and community gathering place offering a unique Old Florida atmosphere.

## History
Established as a music park to celebrate live music in St. Augustine's historic downtown. Has become a mainstay on the local festival circuit, including hosting performances for the Sing Out Loud Music Festival. The venue is built around a historic oak tree that provides natural ambiance and shade.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Rock, blues, Americana, country, jam, funk, jazz, Latin, hip-hop
- **Frequency**: Multiple shows per week (weekends and weeknights)
- **Ticket prices**: Most events free, ticketed events $15-$40 typical
- **Notable acts**: Rising Appalachia, The New Mastersounds, LANCO, local favorites like Bad Dog Mama, One Good Ring
- Active calendar with mix of national touring acts and regional talent
- Also hosts the Caravel Concert Series at adjacent Colonial Quarter Maritime Stage
