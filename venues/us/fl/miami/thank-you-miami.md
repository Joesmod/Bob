# Thank You Miami Cocina & Beer House

## Overview
Restaurant, bar, and entertainment venue in Miami, FL, established in 2021. Known for stand-up comedy, open mic nights, and local craft beer. Casual neighborhood spot with a dive bar/gastropub atmosphere. Self-serve beer coolers, Miami-themed menu, games, and rotating entertainment.

## History
Started as a food truck, evolved into a brick-and-mortar restaurant in 2021. Led by Chef Alex with 20 years of cooking experience in fine dining and food truck operations. Focus on affordable prices and community atmosphere in an area where many venues race to premium pricing.
