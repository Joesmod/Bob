# Marshall L. Davis Sr. African Heritage Cultural Arts Center

## Overview
Nationally recognized arts training institution and cultural venue in Miami, FL. The center features multiple performance spaces including the Wendell A. Narcisse Performing Arts Theater and Melton Mustafa Concert Hall. Hosts ticketed performances in theater, music, dance, and visual arts celebrating African and African American culture.

## History
The Marshall L. Davis Sr. African Heritage Cultural Arts Center (DAHCAC) celebrated its 50th anniversary in September 2025, marking half a century of cultural excellence and community service in Miami. Named after Marshall L. Davis Sr., the center has developed into a nationally recognized arts training institution.
