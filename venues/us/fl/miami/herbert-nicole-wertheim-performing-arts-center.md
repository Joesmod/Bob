# Herbert & Nicole Wertheim Performing Arts Center

## Overview
University performing arts center at Florida International University (FIU) featuring a 585-seat Concert Hall and 300-seat Mainstage Theatre. Houses the FIU School of Music and Department of Theatre. Opened in 1996 with $14 million facility investment. Described as one of Miami's premier collegiate concert and performance venues.

## History
Opened in 1996 thanks to a philanthropic gift from Dr. Herbert & Mrs. Nicole Wertheim. Opening event was The Walt Disney World Brass Choir. Over 100 performances annually across 20+ years. Home to thousands of costumes, set pieces, and hundreds of instruments.
