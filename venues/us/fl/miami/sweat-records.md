# Sweat Records

## Overview
Independent record store in Miami's Little Haiti neighborhood. Community-focused vinyl shop known for curated selection, live events, and listening parties. "A cornerstone of 21st century Miami culture."

## History
Long-standing Miami record store with strong community presence. Features in major press outlets and has built reputation for excellent curation and customer experience. Known for careful packaging and fast shipping for online orders.
