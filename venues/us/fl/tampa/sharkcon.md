# SharkCon

## Overview
**Not a venue** — SharkCon is an annual shark-themed convention held in Tampa, Florida. It is a traveling event/convention, not a permanent venue. The event features shark science, conservation education, vendors, speakers, and family activities.

## Event Type
Annual consumer convention/expo focused on sharks, ocean conservation, and marine science. Typically held at convention centers or similar large event spaces.

## Programming
- **Speakers**: National Geographic SHARKFEST personalities, Shark Week experts, marine scientists
- **Vendors**: 150+ vendors selling shark and ocean-related merchandise (jewelry, art, clothing, collectibles)
- **Activities**: Indoor scuba diving demos, educational exhibits, conservation nonprofit booths
- **Focus**: Shark conservation awareness and ocean sustainability education
- **Audience**: Families, shark enthusiasts, casual consumers to serious aficionados
