## About

City of Pompano Beach cultural arts destination featuring innovative programming in visual arts, music, film, theater, dance, public art, history, and historic preservation. Located in revitalized downtown Pompano, adjacent to City Hall. Offers cultural opportunities across many artistic disciplines with access to world-renowned music, dance, theater performances and art exhibitions.

## History

Part of the City of Pompano Beach's cultural arts initiative to establish Pompano Beach as a cultural destination for South Florida residents and visitors.
