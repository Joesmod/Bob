## About

Nighttime water and fireworks show at Disney's Hollywood Studios amphitheater. Features Mickey Mouse as sorcerer's apprentice battling Disney villains including a 40-foot-tall Maleficent dragon. Uses water screens, pyrotechnics, live performers, and Disney characters from films including Moana, Frozen 2, Mulan, Aladdin, Pocahontas, Beauty and the Beast, The Little Mermaid, and The Lion King.

## History

Long-running Disney nighttime spectacular, originally debuted at Disneyland in 1992. The Hollywood Studios version is an outdoor amphitheater production.
