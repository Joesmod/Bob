# Flûte Champagne Bar - Midtown

## Overview
Upscale champagne bar and lounge in midtown Manhattan, opened 1997 in a space that once housed the notorious speakeasy Club Intime. Specializes in champagne and sparkling wine with over 100 selections, plus specialty sparkling cocktails and small plates. Features weekly live music, happy hours, and DJs in an intimate, chic setting.

## History
Opened in 1997, taking over the historic Club Intime speakeasy space. Founded on the concept of bringing champagne "out of the nightclub VIP room" and into an accessible lounge setting that combines French art de vivre with New York City nightlife. Part of a two-location operation (Midtown NYC and Lehigh, PA).
