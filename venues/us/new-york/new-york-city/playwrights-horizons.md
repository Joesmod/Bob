# Playwrights Horizons

## Overview
Prestigious Off-Broadway theater company dedicated to developing contemporary American playwrights and producing innovative new work. Operating for 54 years with a unique mission centered on advancing the playwright's voice. Two-stage theater presenting World, American, or New York premieres annually. Also operates an undergraduate theater program at NYU Tisch School of the Arts.

## History
Founded 54 years ago (circa 1971). Distinguished by steadfast commitment to centering the playwright's voice in the evolution of American theater. Has become one of the most respected Off-Broadway institutions for new work development.
