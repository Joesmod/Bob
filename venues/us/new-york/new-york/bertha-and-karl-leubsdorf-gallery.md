# Bertha and Karl Leubsdorf Gallery

## Overview
Academic art gallery at Hunter College (CUNY) in New York. Part of the Hunter College Art Galleries under the Department of Art and Art History. Focuses on historical and scholarly exhibitions featuring under-represented artists and art movements. Also hosts the BFA degree exhibition each spring semester. Free admission.

## History
Part of Hunter College Art Galleries, which have been active for over 25 years as a vital aspect of the NYC cultural landscape. The gallery serves as an extension of Hunter's academic programs.

## Programming & Exhibitions
- **Format**: Free rotating art exhibitions
- **Focus**: Historical and scholarly exhibitions, under-represented artists and movements
- **Current Exhibition** (Feb-April 2026): "Speaking in Pairs" (photography exhibition)
- **Annual**: BFA degree exhibition each spring semester
- **Hours**: Tuesday–Saturday, 12–6pm
