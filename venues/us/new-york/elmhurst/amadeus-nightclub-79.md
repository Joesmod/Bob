# Amadeus Nightclub

## Overview
Late-night nightclub in Elmhurst, Queens specializing in Caribbean, Afrobeats, and hip-hop parties. Features bottle service, VIP sections, and themed events. Operating hours typically 10pm-4am Fridays and Saturdays, with occasional Sunday events.

## History
Established nightclub serving the NYC Caribbean and African diaspora community. Known for recurring party series like "Unik Fridays" and themed celebrations (Pisces Ball, Ghana Independence, etc.).
