## About

Reedley's River City Theatre Company (RCTC) is a community theater in Reedley, California, focused on inclusive, collaborative theatrical productions. The company believes that "theatre is for everyone" and creates a safe, welcoming environment for both seasoned performers and newcomers. Productions are crafted with care and fueled by passion, emphasizing that every role matters and every voice is heard.

## History

RCTC operates as a community-driven theater company in California's Central Valley, providing theatrical arts opportunities for local performers and audiences. The organization maintains an active season calendar and offers opportunities for community members to get involved in productions.
