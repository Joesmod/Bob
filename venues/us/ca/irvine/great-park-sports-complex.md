# Great Park Sports Complex

## Overview
Municipal sports complex operated by the City of Irvine featuring up to 24 soccer fields, championship stadium (2,500 capacity), and athletic facilities. Used for youth/adult soccer leagues, tournaments, and drop-in play. NOT an entertainment or ticketed event venue.

## Facility Details
- **Championship Soccer Stadium**: 2,500-seat stadium with locker rooms, training facilities, box office (for tournaments/events), and concessions
- **Soccer Fields**: 6 lighted grass fields (120x75 yards), natural grass flex field, 6 synthetic turf fields
- **South Lawn**: 30-acre area with 4 tournament-quality lighted fields
- **Hours**: Mon-Fri 8am-11pm, Sat-Sun 7am-11pm
