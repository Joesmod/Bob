## About

2nd Space Theatre is the smaller, more intimate performance venue operated by Good Company Players, a community theater company founded in 1973. Good Company Players runs two venues in Fresno:
- **Roger Rocka's Dinner Theater** (dinner theater, larger productions)
- **2nd Space Theatre** (intimate theater for straight plays and smaller shows)

Between the two venues, Good Company Players produces **12 shows per year** and has mounted over **550 productions** since inception.

## History

**1973:** Good Company Players founded by Dan Pessano, Chris Moad, Cathy Glenn, Ronald J. Harlan and a group of enthusiastic performers. Started as summer dinner theater in Fresno's Hilton Hotel.

**1978:** Roger Rocka proposed a permanent home. The company converted a vacant Sprouse-Reitz store into Roger Rocka's Music Hall, Eating Establishment and Drinking Emporium (now Roger Rocka's Dinner Theater).

**1982:** Opened 2nd Space Theatre as a smaller, more intimate companion venue.

## Mission

"To provide professional-level training to local performers of all ages while also providing the community with challenging and entertaining exposure to theatre."

**Philosophy:** "We believe that the performing arts keeps us civilized; teaching us to think and express ourselves coherently, to hear and respect other perspectives, to appreciate beauty and live a little more compassionately. We believe that theatre is a team sport..."

## Training & Education

Good Company Players partners with **The Company Studio (TCS)** for theater education:
- Theatre skills classes
- Tap dance
- Improv acting
- Master classes taught by former GCP professionals working in the arts
- Performers/crew in GCP productions are considered TCS students gaining practical training
- Students receive discounted rates on technique classes
