# Good Company Players

## Overview
Professional theater company in Fresno, CA. Founded in 1973, operates two venues: Roger Rocka's Dinner Theater and 2nd Space Theatre. Produces 12 shows per year featuring musicals, plays, and professional-level theater for the Central Valley community. Over 550 productions since inception.

## History
Founded in 1973 by Dan Pessano, Chris Moad, Cathy Glenn, Ronald J. Harlan and a group of enthusiastic performers. Started as summer dinner theater in Fresno's Hilton Hotel. In 1978, converted a vacant Sprouse-Reitz store into Roger Rocka's Music Hall. Opened the intimate 2nd Space Theatre in 1982. Focus shifted from dinner theater to professional-level community theater with year-round productions.
