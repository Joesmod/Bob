# McBean Stadium (Lincoln Potters)

## Overview
Home of the Lincoln Potters, a founding team of the Pacific Empire League (PEL) offering elite college baseball in Lincoln, California. Season runs late spring through summer. Family-friendly entertainment with fireworks nights, theme events, and group packages.

## History
Founding team of the Pacific Empire League. Established to bring high-level college summer baseball to the small town of Lincoln, CA.
