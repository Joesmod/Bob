## About

Three-theater complex in Los Angeles offering intimate performance spaces for rent. Features Mainstage Theatre (99 seats, modified thrust), Backstage Theatre (99 seats, 42 feet wide), and Guild Theatre (43 seats, raised stage / screening room). Hudson Theatre Coffee Shop serves as the lobby and gathering space.

## History

Established LA theater rental venue serving the independent theater community. Known for providing quality intimate spaces for theater productions, with each space offering unique staging configurations and atmospheres.

## Programming & Shows

**Three Venues**:

1. **Mainstage Theatre** - 99 seats
   - Modified thrust stage
   - Three dressing rooms
   - Premier rental space

2. **Backstage Theatre** - 99 seats
   - 42 feet wide (largest stage)
   - Brick walls, rolling flats
   - Flexible production space

3. **Guild Theatre / Hudson Screening Room** - 43 seats
   - Raised stage
   - Most intimate space
   - Top-of-the-line A/V equipment
   - Doubles as screening room

**Business Model**: Rental venue for independent theater productions
- Productions rent space for their run
- Producers handle their own ticketing and marketing
- Hudson provides facility, technical equipment, and lobby space

**Hudson Theatre Coffee Shop**: Daily operation, serves as lobby, perfect for production meetings and post-show receptions

## Opendate Fit

This is a **rental venue**, not a producing venue, which significantly impacts fit:

**Business Model Challenge**:
- Hudson doesn't produce shows or sell tickets directly
- They rent space to independent producers
- Each renter handles their own ticketing
- No centralized box office or ticketing operation

**Why Low Score**:
- Not the decision-maker for ticketing (renters are)
- Inconsistent ticketing (each production uses different systems)
- Would need to convince dozens of individual producers, not one venue
- No recurring ticketing revenue stream for Hudson itself

**Possible Angle** (Would require different approach):
- Could position Opendate as "preferred ticketing partner" for Hudson renters
- Offer discount to productions renting Hudson spaces
- Would need buy-in from Hudson management to recommend/require Opendate
- Similar to how venues sometimes partner with specific tech providers

**Recommendation**: Low priority unless pursuing a "rental venue network" strategy where you partner with rental spaces to offer their producers a preferred ticketing platform. This is a different sales motion than targeting producing venues.
