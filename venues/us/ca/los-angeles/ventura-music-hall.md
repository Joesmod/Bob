# Ventura Music Hall

## Overview
Active music venue in Ventura, CA featuring live concerts across all genres. Full-service venue with in-house craft food and drinks. Books both local talent and touring national acts with a packed calendar of ticketed shows.

## History
Established music hall serving the Ventura area. Known for bringing "an unrivaled live music experience" with diverse programming from rock to reggae to electronic music.
