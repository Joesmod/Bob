# Magic Box

## Overview
Large-scale event venue and exhibition space located at The REEF in downtown Los Angeles. Over 120,000 square feet of indoor space plus 100,000+ square feet of outdoor festival space. Primarily focused on corporate events, trade shows, festivals, and experiential marketing rather than traditional ticketed entertainment.

## History
Located at 1933 S. Broadway in the historic REEF building in downtown LA. Part of a comprehensive event campus offering full-service event production.
