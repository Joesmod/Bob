## About

Proscenium theater operated by the University of Southern California's School of Dramatic Arts. This is the school's largest venue, seating 550 people, used primarily for student productions, educational programming, and university events.

## History

Part of USC's School of Dramatic Arts theater facilities. Serves as the flagship performance space for the dramatic arts program, hosting student productions, showcases, and educational performances.
