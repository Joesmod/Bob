# Jungle Hollywood

## Overview

Themed nightclub and event venue in Hollywood hosting weekly dance parties and special events. Features multiple levels with indoor and outdoor patio spaces.

## History

Jungle Hollywood is located in a historic Hollywood venue space. The current operation focuses on weekly themed nights featuring various music genres including hip-hop, reggaeton, drum & bass, R&B, and Afrobeats.
