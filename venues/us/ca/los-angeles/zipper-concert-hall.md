## About

Concert hall within The Colburn School, a prestigious performing arts conservatory and community music school in downtown Los Angeles. Located across from The Broad museum and Walt Disney Concert Hall. Hosts student recitals, faculty concerts, visiting artists, and rental performances. Part of an educational institution serving 2,000+ students annually

## History

Part of The Colburn School, which traces its origins to 1950 as a USC prep school. In 1980, Richard D. Colburn's California Foundation re-envisioned it as an independent institution. Conservatory of Music added in 2003 (full-scholarship model), Trudl Zipper Dance Institute in 2008, Music Academy in 2010.
