# Zula Den

## Overview

Wellness and healing space in Los Angeles focused on sound baths, breathwork, guided meditation, and ceremonial gatherings. 501(c)(3) non-profit organization (Zula Project).

## About

Zula Den is a sacred wellness space offering sound healing, breathwork, guided meditation, ceremonies, and community gatherings. Operated by non-profit Zula Project. Features facilitators including Nicolas Sotomayor, Solar Moon, Tesha, Etherica, and others.

## Programming

- **Focus**: Sound baths, breathwork, guided meditation, musical journeys
- **Type**: Wellness experiences, spiritual ceremonies, community gatherings
- **Facilitators**: Certified sound healers, breathwork practitioners, yoga instructors
- **Space**: Cozy meditation room with pillows, located inside office building
