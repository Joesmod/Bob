## About

**⚠️ NOT A FIT FOR OPENDATE**

Ace Mission Studios is a **film and television production facility**, not a live event venue. This is an 18-acre industrial campus in Boyle Heights featuring production studios and creative offices for the entertainment industry.

The property was formerly home to Ace Beverage LLC and Mission Beverage Co (beer/beverage distribution companies founded by John E. Anderson). It has been repositioned into cutting-edge production studios and creative offices for film/TV work.

## Facilities

- **8 production stages**
- **3 mill/flex spaces**
- **100+ production offices**
- **400,000 square feet** across 18 acres
- Boyle Heights' first production facility

## History

Formerly operated as beer and beverage distribution warehouses (Ace Beverage LLC and Mission Beverage Co), founded and operated by the late John E. Anderson. The Anderson family has had a long-standing presence in Boyle Heights. The property underwent major repositioning from industrial warehouses into production studios.
