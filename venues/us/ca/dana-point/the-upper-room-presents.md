# The Upper Room Presents

## Overview
Nonprofit Christian ministry hosting faith-based live music and comedy events. 70-person capacity studio in Irvine; also uses Capo Beach Church in Dana Point for larger events. Founded in 2009 as a "non-churchy" venue for clean, family-friendly Christian entertainment.

## History
Founded in 2009 by Ron and Kathy Strand after hosting a speaker at their home group. First event outgrew their home, so they moved to an upstairs space in Ron's office building in Irvine - hence "The Upper Room." From 2010-2020, used The Rock venue in Mission Viejo. After COVID, shifted to Capo Beach Church for larger shows and built a 70-seat studio in Irvine for intimate events.
