# Huntington Beach Sports Complex

## Overview
45-acre outdoor sports facility in Huntington Beach featuring eight softball fields overlaid with seven soccer fields, four open artificial turf fields, one arena turf field, batting cages, concessions, and playgrounds. Primarily a sports complex for leagues and tournaments.

## History
Premier outdoor sports facility in Huntington Beach serving as a community sports resource. Hosts leagues, tournaments, and athletic training programs.
