## About

The Improv Collective is a performance troupe and creative collective for improv comedy, sketch comedy, and live adaptations. Established in 2012 in Orange County, it provides a platform for creative minds to develop talents in writing, directing, and performing.

The collective describes itself as "a family of like-minded individuals who desire to showcase every aspect of the creative process in a safe and fun environment."
