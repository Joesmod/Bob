# FNGRS CRSSD

## Overview
San Diego-based electronic music festival and event brand, not a traditional venue. CRSSD Festival is a twice-yearly electronic music festival held at Waterfront Park in downtown San Diego. Known for curating house, techno, and electronic music with "dancefloor euphoria." Established festival brand rather than a year-round venue operation.

## History
Founded in 2013. Has become a major electronic music festival brand in Southern California. The name represents the festival's focus on creating euphoric dancefloor experiences.
