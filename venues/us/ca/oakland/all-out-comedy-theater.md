# All Out Comedy Theater

## Overview
Improv and stand-up comedy theater in Oakland, CA. Hosts classes and shows with a focus on community and welcoming atmosphere. Features a lobby bar serving snacks, soda, wine, and beer. All ages welcome (no age restriction).

## History
Community-focused improv theater offering training and performances in the Bay Area. Known for inclusive programming and accessible comedy education.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Improv comedy, stand-up, sketch comedy, musical improv
- **Frequency**: Multiple shows weekly plus regular open mic nights
- **Ticket prices**: Varies by show, typically $10-$25
- **Notable programming**:
  - Level 1-5 improv classes (8-week sessions)
  - Musical improv workshops
  - Stand-up comedy classes
  - Weekly open mic (Mondays)
  - Rotating showcase productions
- Regular shows include "The Sitcom", "Fresh Baked Comedy", puppet shows, indie improv nights
