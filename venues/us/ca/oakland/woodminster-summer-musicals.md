# Woodminster Summer Musicals

## Overview

Outdoor summer musical theater in Oakland's Joaquin Miller Park, operating for 50+ years. Presents three Broadway-style musicals each summer (one in June, July, and August) with 7 performances per show. The venue seats approximately 2,000 and serves 25,000+ ticket holders per season. Known for quality productions under the stars in a beautiful natural setting.

## About

Woodminster Summer Musicals is presented by Producers Associates, Inc., a nonprofit organization. The venue is an outdoor amphitheater carved into the hillside of Joaquin Miller Park. Productions feature full Broadway-style musicals with professional casts. The theater offers intimate seating with excellent sightlines from all sections.
