# Oakstop

## Overview
Coworking space and event venue provider with multiple locations across Oakland. Offers workspace memberships, meeting rooms, event halls, and gallery spaces. **Not a traditional ticketing venue** — focuses on space rentals and coworking memberships rather than hosting ticketed public events.

## History
Social enterprise founded to use commercial real estate as a platform for economic development and community empowerment, specifically serving entrepreneurs and communities of color in Oakland.
