# The Palmdale Amphitheater

## Overview
Premier outdoor entertainment venue in the Antelope Valley at Marie Kerr Park, Palmdale. City-operated amphitheater offering summer concert series, family movie nights, and community events. Comfortable grassy seating areas with excellent sight lines.

## History
Built in 2005 after Palmdale residents voted for a bond measure to fund construction. Replaced the smaller Starlight Concert Series held on a Marie Kerr Park baseball field where guests would arrive as early as 3 a.m. to claim spots. The new amphitheater at the expanded Marie Kerr Park site provides permanent restrooms, food vendor areas, and a proper green room for entertainers.

Previously hosted smaller events like Family Western Days, Taste of the Valley, and Fourth Saturday Cruise alongside concerts.
