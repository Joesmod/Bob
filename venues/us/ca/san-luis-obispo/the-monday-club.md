Historic clubhouse and nonprofit civic organization established in 1924. The building, designed by renowned architect Julia Morgan, is listed on the National Register of Historic Places. Operates as both a women's civic organization and an event venue rental space.

## History

Established in 1924 when San Luis Obispo offered few forums for women to organize. The Monday Club was created as a nonprofit civic organization with a focus on community service. The historic clubhouse was designed by Julia Morgan, the same architect known for Hearst Castle, and is now on the National Register of Historic Places.

For over 100 years, the organization has been dedicated to community service and preservation of this architectural landmark.
