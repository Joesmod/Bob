# Saddleback College - Performing Arts

## Overview
Community college performing arts center in Mission Viejo, CA. Features two performance spaces: the 400-seat McKinney Theatre and 72-seat Studio Theatre. Hosts student productions, cultural events, and community performances, with many free events.

## Venues
- **McKinney Theatre**: 400 seats
- **Studio Theatre**: 72 seats
- **Art Gallery**: Visual arts exhibitions

## Programming & Shows
- Dance performances
- Music concerts and recitals
- Theatre productions
- Speech and debate events
- Cultural events
- Student productions (primary focus)
- Many free events for community

## History
Part of South Orange County Community College District. The performing arts center serves as both an educational facility and community cultural resource.
