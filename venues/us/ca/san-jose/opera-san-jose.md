# Opera San Jose

## Overview
Professional opera company in Silicon Valley, founded 41 years ago (established 1984). Maintains a resident company of artists and presents four mainstage productions annually at San Jose's historic California Theatre. Also produces digital performances via state-of-the-art Heiman Digital Media Studio for streaming broadcasts.

## History
Founded in 1984 (currently in 41st season). Built reputation as an artistic incubator for established and emerging opera artists. Pioneered digital opera streaming during pandemic with the Heiman Digital Media Studio, allowing remote audiences to experience professional opera productions.
