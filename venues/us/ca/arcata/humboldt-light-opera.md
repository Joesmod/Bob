# Humboldt Light Opera Company (HLOC)

## Overview
Community theater company in Arcata, CA producing musical theater productions since 1973. "Where Stories Come Alive!" HLOC presents major musical productions, youth theater (HLOKidCo), and Boomer Troupe programming for older adults. Performances at multiple venues including Gist Hall Theatre at Cal Poly Humboldt, Arkley Center for Performing Arts, and Eureka Woman's Club.

## History
Founded in December 1973 with first production of "Amahl and the Night Visitors." Celebrating 50+ years of bringing musical theater to Humboldt County. Has presented over 100 major productions involving thousands of performers, actors, dancers, instrumentalists, directors, designers and choreographers.

## Programming & Shows
- **Main Stage Productions**: Classic musical theater at Gist Hall Theatre (Cal Poly Humboldt)
- **HLOKidCo**: Youth theater program (e.g. "Beauty and the Beast Jr")
- **Boomer Troupe**: Programming for older adults (e.g. "The Gift of Friendship")
- **Special Events**: "Fiona and Friends" concert series
- **Production Schedule**: Multiple shows per year with weekend performances (Fridays-Sundays)
- **Ticket Prices**: $1-$20 range (youth shows), $15 typical for Boomer Troupe
- **Performance Venues**:
  - Gist Hall Theatre at Cal Poly Humboldt
  - Arkley Center for Performing Arts, Eureka
  - Eureka Woman's Club
  - Christian Science Society
