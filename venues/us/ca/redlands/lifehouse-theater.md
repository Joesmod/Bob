# LifeHouse Theater

## Overview
Unique independent, interdenominational community theater in Redlands, CA specializing in faith-based, family-friendly original productions. Features exclusive original plays and musicals with redemptive, inspirational themes guided by Judeo-Christian perspectives. Attracts over 30,000 guests annually with more than 500 volunteer actors.

## History
Award-winning faith-based theater that has become unique in the nation for its commitment to exclusive, original productions illustrating Christian worldviews and biblical themes. Operates with a large volunteer cast and crew from all walks of life.
