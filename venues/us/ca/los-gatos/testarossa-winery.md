## About

Premium California winery specializing in Pinot Noir and Chardonnay since 1993. Located in the historic Novitiate Winery (built 1888, California's fourth oldest continuously operating winery). Features Bistro 107 wine bar with complimentary live music. Also hosts private events and weddings.

## History

Founded in 1993 by Rob and Diana Jensen in their Silicon Valley garage. Moved to the historic Novitiate Winery in Los Gatos in 1997. Originally built in 1888 by Jesuit Fathers and Brothers from Santa Clara College to fund their seminary. The Jesuits made altar wines and fortified wines here for nearly 100 years until 1986. Testarossa took over in 1997 and has beautifully renovated the historic facility. Now produces around 30,000 cases annually.
