# Warner Grand Theatre / Grand Vision Foundation

## Overview
Historic art deco movie palace in San Pedro, CA, owned and operated by the City of Los Angeles Department of Cultural Affairs. Grand Vision Foundation (501c3 nonprofit) serves as the Friends' Group and operates the Grand Annex Music Hall, a 150-seat intimate music venue. The foundation has been active in the San Pedro arts community for over 25 years.

## History
The Warner Grand Theatre is a historic art deco movie palace that has been a San Pedro landmark. Grand Vision Foundation was founded over 25 years ago to support the restoration and programming of the Warner Grand, and in 2008 opened the Grand Annex Music Hall to provide a smaller, more experimental venue for live music and performing artists.
