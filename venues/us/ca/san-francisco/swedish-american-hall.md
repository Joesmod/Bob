# Swedish American Hall

## Overview
Historic mixed-use event space and live music venue in San Francisco's Castro district. Features four unique spaces including the main Swedish American Hall and Cafe Du Nord. Accommodates 50-500 guests depending on configuration. Serves as both private event rental space and public concert venue.

## History
Historic building at 2174 Market St with deep roots in San Francisco's Swedish-American community. The Swedish Society of San Francisco continues to operate out of the venue. Cafe Du Nord, located in the same building, has been a San Francisco music institution for decades.
