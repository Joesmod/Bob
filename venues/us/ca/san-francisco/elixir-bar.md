# Elixir

## Overview
Historic saloon and whiskey bar in San Francisco's Mission District. Operating continuously since 1858, Elixir is the second-oldest continually operating saloon location in San Francisco. The venue hosts ticketed cocktail classes, whiskey tastings, trivia nights, and special events. Capacity varies by event type; intimate bar setting with event space capabilities.

## History
The original saloon on this corner dates back to at least 1858, with evidence suggesting operations may have started as early as 1848. Located at the end of the original Mission Plank Road connecting Mission Dolores with Yerba Buena, the building burned down in 1906 and was rebuilt by the same owner in 1907. The venue has operated under various names including The Hunt-In Club (1940s), Swede's (1960s-70s), La Bandita (late 1980s), and Jack's Elixir Bar (1990s) before being restored to its current glory by H. Joseph Ehrmann in 2003.

- **Signature Programs**:
