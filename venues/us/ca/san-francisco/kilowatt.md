# Kilowatt

## Overview
Neighborhood bar in San Francisco's Mission District (3160 16th St @ Albion). Opened 1994 in former firehouse. Originally a punk/rock/indie music showcase and home to Noise Pop Festival. Converted stage to darts area after 3 years. Under new ownership, bringing music back with weekly live shows and DJ nights. Features darts, pool tables, craft beer, full bar, sports TVs.

## History
