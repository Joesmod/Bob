# Magic Theatre

## Overview
Historic nonprofit theater company in San Francisco, founded in 1967. Presents new American plays and world premieres in an intimate theater setting. Known for developing playwrights including Sam Shepard, who premiered his Pulitzer Prize-winning "Buried Child" here during his 10-year residency (1974-84).

## History
Founded in 1967 by John Lion as a center for new American playwriting. Became legendary for nurturing Sam Shepard's work and launching numerous award-winning plays. Recognized as a San Francisco Legacy Business in September 2025. Currently led by Artistic Director Sean San José (appointed June 2021), the first person of color to lead the organization in its 50+ year history.
