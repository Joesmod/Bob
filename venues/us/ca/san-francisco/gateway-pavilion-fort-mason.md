# Gateway Pavilion - Fort Mason Center

## Overview
Event rental venue within Fort Mason Center for Arts & Culture campus. Part of 13-acre historic waterfront complex with multiple event spaces ranging 500-50,000 sq ft. Gateway Pavilion is one of 11 venue options. Fort Mason hosts 1.5 million annual visitors to various events, exhibitions, and performances. Located in Golden Gate National Recreation Area.

## History
Fort Mason Center opened to public in 1977, transforming former San Francisco Port of Embarkation (military base) into arts and culture hub. Historic landmark with Golden Gate Bridge and Alcatraz views. Nonprofit steward of federal property. Home to ~24 resident nonprofit arts organizations. Has hosted Magic Theatre, Blue Bear School of Music, Greens Restaurant, and currently hosts American Indian Cultural District, SF Camerawork, Arion Press, Stern Grove Festival Association.
