## About

The Great Star Theater is a historic, century-old performing arts venue in San Francisco's Chinatown that blends timeless charm with contemporary creativity. Operating as a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, the theater is led by an active board of community leaders and artists dedicated to fostering inclusivity and representation.

**Mission**: Cultivate traditional Asian art forms, nurture emerging artists, and revitalize the San Francisco Chinatown community.

The theater is a welcoming space for people of all backgrounds, hosting events that highlight diverse voices including the LGBTQ+ community, Asian-American culture, and up-and-coming local artists. The organization believes in the transformative power of the arts to unite, inspire, and create meaningful connections.

## History

**1925**: Established in the heart of San Francisco's Chinatown. The theater has served the community for nearly a century.

The venue has maintained its historic character while evolving to meet contemporary needs, hosting everything from traditional Chinese opera to modern circus performances and independent films.
