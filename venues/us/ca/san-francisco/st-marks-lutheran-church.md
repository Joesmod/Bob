# St. Mark's Lutheran Church

## Overview
Historic Lutheran church (ELCA) with a sanctuary renowned for its acoustics, serving as a premier performance venue for classical music groups. Built in 1894, the sanctuary hosts numerous choral societies, orchestras, and chamber ensembles. Active congregation with 390 members and a robust concert rental program.

## History
Built in 1894, St. Mark's Sanctuary is revered by instrumental and vocal musicians as a top-tier performance space. The church is a Reconciling in Christ congregation (LGBTQ+ affirming) and a Sanctuary Congregation. Located in the Tenderloin/Nob Hill area of San Francisco on unceded Ramaytush Ohlone land.

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 70/100 — Low Priority**
This is a rental venue where performing arts organizations book the space for their own concerts rather than a primary ticketing venue. Each renting group handles their own ticket sales through various platforms. St. Mark's itself doesn't sell concert tickets — they rent the space. Not a strong fit for Opendate's primary use case of venue-based ticketing platforms. The actual ticketing customers would be the 25+ individual performing arts groups, not the church itself.
