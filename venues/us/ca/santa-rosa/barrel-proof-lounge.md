# Barrel Proof Lounge

## Overview
Entertainment lounge in downtown Santa Rosa featuring stand-up comedy, live music, karaoke, trivia, and bingo. Brand-new venue with 16 craft beer taps, wine, and full bar. Most events are free with no cover charge, generating revenue through beverage sales and food (free popcorn provided).

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 25/100 — Not Suitable**

**Not a ticketed venue.** All programming is free admission with revenue generated through bar sales. This is a classic comedy club / bar model where entertainment drives beverage sales, not ticket revenue.

**Why in the leads sheet**: Likely flagged due to "stand-up comedy" and "live music" keywords, plus the word "lounge" suggesting a venue. However, the business model is drinks-first, not ticketing.

**Pros (if they pivoted to ticketing):**
- Consistent weekly programming
- Multiple event types (comedy, music, karaoke)
- New venue building brand
- Downtown location

**Cons:**
- Entire current model is free admission
- Revenue from bar, not tickets
- Small capacity (lounge-sized)
- Would require business model pivot

**Recommendation**: Remove from active leads. If they launch ticketed comedy showcases in the future (common path for successful comedy clubs), revisit. Current model doesn't need ticketing platform.
