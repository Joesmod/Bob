# The New Vic / Ensemble Theatre Company

## Overview
Santa Barbara's sole professional Equity theater company operating the New Vic Theatre (formerly Victoria Hall Theater). 300-seat venue hosting five professional theatrical productions per season plus community events, dance, music, film, and lectures. Nonprofit organization with a 46-year history.

## History
Founded in 1978 as the Ensemble Theatre Project at Trinity Episcopal Church. Moved to the historic 140-seat Alhecama Theatre (now provides rehearsal space). In a major expansion, ETC undertook a $12.6 million purchase and renovation of Victoria Hall Theater, reopening it as the New Vic Theatre with 300 seats.

**Leadership History:**
- Joseph Hanreddy (1978-1985)
- Robert Grande Weiss (1985-2006)
- Jonathan Fox (2006-2023)
- Scott DeVine (2023-present)

## Programming & Shows
- **Productions**: 5 professional plays per season
- **Season**: 2024-25 "Legends and Legacies" 46th season
- **Genres**: Mix of musicals, drama, thrillers, contemporary works, and classics
- **Recent Shows**: Million Dollar Quartet, Hamlet, Rachmaninoff and the Tsar, Justice
- **Notable**: World, American, and West Coast premieres
- **Additional Programming**: Young Actors Conservatory, community rentals
