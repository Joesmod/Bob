## About

Premier comedy club in Hermosa Beach featuring stand-up comedy and magic acts. Known for hosting Jay Leno who performs regularly (almost every Sunday). Multiple show formats including "The Fab Five," "10 Comics," and celebrity headliners.

## History

Long-standing comedy venue on the Southern California circuit. Attracts top national talent and provides a platform for both established comedians and rising stars. Part of the historic LA-area comedy scene.
