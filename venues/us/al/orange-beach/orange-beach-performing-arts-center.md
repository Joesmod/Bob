## Opendate Fit

**Score: 45/100 — Poor Fit / Municipal Facility**

**Major Challenges:**
- **Municipal ownership**: City of Orange Beach operates facility — requires government procurement processes
- **Dual use**: Serves both community theatre AND public schools
- **Government bureaucracy**: Platform changes require city council approval, RFP process, budget cycles
- **Low volume**: Limited production schedule (primarily school + community theatre)
- **OnTheStage**: Already using specialized education/community theatre platform
- **Not a commercial venue**: Part of city parks/recreation department

**Reality Check:**
This is a government-owned facility serving schools and community programs, not an independent venue. Ticketing decisions go through:
- City procurement processes
- School board coordination
- Budget approval cycles
- Likely multi-year contracts with current provider

**Better targets:** Independently owned music venues, comedy clubs, small commercial theatres — NOT municipal performing arts centers with school programs.
