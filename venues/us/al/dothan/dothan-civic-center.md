# Dothan Civic Center

## Overview
Municipal civic center in Dothan, Alabama. Multi-purpose venue hosting concerts, sporting events, graduations, conventions, beauty pageants, and community events. City-owned facility operated as a public service.

## History
Serves as Dothan's primary municipal event space, hosting everything from Troy University graduations to professional wrestling, country concerts to anime conventions.
