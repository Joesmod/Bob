# Dothan Civic Center Arena

## Overview
Municipal civic center and arena in Dothan, Alabama. Multi-purpose venue hosting concerts, touring shows, sporting events, conventions, and community events. Managed by the City of Dothan Parks & Recreation. Features modern amenities including clear bag policy, cashless operations, and professional production capabilities. Also operates the adjacent Dothan Opera House.

## History
City-owned facility serving as Southeast Alabama's primary venue for large-scale events and entertainment. The venue has evolved to include state-of-the-art sound/lighting systems and modern security protocols.

- **No re-entry** once inside
- **No weapons** of any kind
- **Age restrictions**: Guests 15 and under must be with adult 19+
- **Alcohol**: 21+ with ID for anyone appearing under 35, 2-drink limit per transaction

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 25/100 — Poor Fit**
Municipal venue, city-owned and operated by Dothan Parks & Recreation. Government-operated facilities typically have procurement processes, long-term vendor contracts, and political considerations that make switching ticketing platforms extremely difficult. Already using TIXR under likely multi-year contract. Low probability of conversion despite high ticket volume. Better to focus on independent venues with decision-making autonomy.
