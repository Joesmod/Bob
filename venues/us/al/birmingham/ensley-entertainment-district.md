# Ensley Entertainment District

## Overview
Historic event venue complex in Birmingham's Ensley neighborhood featuring three distinct spaces: The Tuxedo Ballroom, Live Loft, and Soho. Built in beaux arts warehouse style with rustic elegance, offering blank canvas event spaces in a vintage setting. Primarily serves as a private event venue rather than a public ticketed music venue.

## History
Located in Birmingham's historic Ensley Entertainment District. The buildings feature vintage warehouse architecture repurposed for events. Part of the revitalization of the historic Ensley neighborhood.
