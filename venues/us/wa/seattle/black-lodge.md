# The Vera Project (misnamed as "Black Lodge" in sheet)

**Note:** Sheet row 354 lists this venue as "Black Lodge" but the website (theveraproject.org) is The Vera Project. "Black Lodge" was not found anywhere on the venue's website. This profile reflects The Vera Project.

## Overview
All-ages nonprofit music venue and arts space in Seattle. Founded in 2001. Volunteer-driven concerts, art gallery, screen print shop, recording studio, and safe space for radical self-expression. Mission-driven organization focused on youth engagement, arts education, and community building.

## History
"Always all ages since 2001." The Vera Project has been a cornerstone of Seattle's DIY all-ages music scene for 25+ years. Celebrating 25th anniversary in 2026. Located at the corner of Warren and Republican in Seattle.
