# Meany Center for the Performing Arts

## Overview
Premier performing arts venue at the University of Washington, featuring two performance spaces: the 1,206-seat Katharyn Alvord Gerlich Theater (main auditorium) and the 238-seat black-box Meany Studio Theatre. Known for outstanding acoustics and intimate ambiance. Mission-driven to foster innovative performances advancing public engagement, cultural exchange, creative research and learning through the arts.
