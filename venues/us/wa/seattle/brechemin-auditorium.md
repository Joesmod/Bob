## About

Brechemin Auditorium is one of the main performance spaces at the University of Washington School of Music. A vibrant concert hall that serves as a hub for classical, contemporary, and experimental music performances by students, faculty, and guest artists. The venue is part of one of the world's finest public research universities' music programs, hosting performances that span from medieval music to newly-created contemporary pieces.

## History

Part of the UW School of Music, which has been a prominent cultural institution in the Pacific Northwest for decades. The auditorium serves as both an educational space and a public performance venue, with deep ties to regional and national arts organizations.
