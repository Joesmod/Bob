# The Crocodile

**Note:** Sheet row 356 lists this venue as "Madam Lous" but the website (thecrocodile.com) is The Crocodile. "Madam Lou's" was not found anywhere on the venue's website or related properties. This profile reflects The Crocodile venue.

## Overview
Iconic Seattle live music venue in Belltown. State-of-the-art concert space celebrating 30+ years as the soundtrack of Seattle's music scene. Moved to a larger location at 2505 1st Ave. Ranked as one of the best clubs in America by Rolling Stone. Features Hotel Crocodile above the venue with 17 uniquely designed rooms, lobby lounge, and Icon Room event space.

## History
30+ years of live music in Seattle. Legendary venue that has hosted Nirvana, Yoko Ono, Pearl Jam, R.E.M., Lizzo, and Billie Eilish. Relocated to a new, larger space in Belltown while maintaining the character of the original venue. "Like the old Croc but better and a hell of a lot bigger."

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 50/100 — Low-Moderate Potential**
High-profile venue with strong ticketing volume and brand recognition. However, already on TicketWeb (Ticketmaster family), which provides deep integration with Ticketmaster's national touring infrastructure. Switching costs would be high — losing access to TM's artist routing, radius clause tools, and promoter ecosystem. Multi-venue ownership (venue + hotel + event spaces) suggests sophisticated operations that might value custom solutions, but TM lock-in makes this a challenging conversion. Better to focus on independent venues not tied to major ticketing platforms.
