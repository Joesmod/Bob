# Hidden Hall

## Overview
Concert venue and gathering place in Seattle's Fremont neighborhood. Built on the legacy of High Dive music venue and Brouwer's Belgian taproom. Features wraparound mezzanine with panoramic stage views, private rooms, open-air patio, and state-of-the-art sound and lighting. Sister venue to Nectar Lounge. High-volume booking across multiple genres.

## History
Carries forward the 20-year legacies of High Dive and Brouwer's, blending craft, community, and unforgettable nights. Located in Fremont, Seattle's most eclectic neighborhood. Modern venue with old-world aesthetic, featuring striking stone wall backdrop, custom lighting design, HD visual projection, and audiophile-grade sound system.
