## About

Tacoma Comedy Club is Tacoma's home for live stand-up comedy, operating two locations in Tacoma. The venues showcase the best comedians from across the country alongside the Pacific Northwest's rising stars in intimate settings. Full food and beverage service with appetizers, entrees, desserts, craft cocktails, and local beers.

## History

Established comedy brand that has expanded to two Tacoma locations: Downtown (933 Market St) and 6th & Proctor (3829 6th Ave). Built a strong reputation as Tacoma's premier comedy destination.
