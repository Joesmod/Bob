## About

The Alternative Library is a community-run cultural space offering a curated public book collection alongside a diverse events program. Currently operating as a "popup series" while searching for a stable home base. Hosts concerts, film screenings, workshops, book readings, performance art, and other experimental events in partnership with neighborhood venues. Also operates a small-press bookstore featuring international independent publications, books, zines, records, tapes, and prints from local creators.

## History

Currently in transition without a permanent location. Operating roving "AltLib Popup Series" at partner venues around Bellingham to continue cultural programming while seeking a stable home base.
