# Main Street Music Hall

## Overview
Community performing arts theater in Osage Beach, Missouri, operated by Dragon Lake Productions, a nonprofit organization. The venue brings live performing arts and cultural programming to the Lake of the Ozarks region.

## History
Main Street Music Hall is the home venue for Dragon Lake Productions, founded to enrich and connect the Lake of the Ozarks community through high-quality performing arts experiences. The nonprofit is currently in the process of obtaining 501(c)(3) status.
