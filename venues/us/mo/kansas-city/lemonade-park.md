# Lemonade Park

## Overview
Seasonal outdoor music venue in Kansas City's West Bottoms district. Operating at 1628 Wyoming St., Lemonade Park is a pop-up live music and arts experience featuring outdoor dining, drinks, and ticketed concerts. Related to The Record Bar and miniBar venues in Kansas City.

## History
