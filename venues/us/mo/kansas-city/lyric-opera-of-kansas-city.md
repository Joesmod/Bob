## History

Established in 1958, Lyric Opera of Kansas City has been presenting world-class opera to the Kansas City region for over 65 years. The company earned a GRAMMY® nomination for its artistic excellence. Based at the Richard J. Stern Opera Center in the East Crossroads area of downtown Kansas City, which includes the Beth Ingram Administration Building (offices) and the Michael and Ginger Frost Production Arts Building (rehearsals, set construction, costume shop, storage).
