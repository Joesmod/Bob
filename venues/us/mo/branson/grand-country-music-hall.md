## About

Grand Country Music Hall is a family entertainment complex in Branson, Missouri, featuring eight different live music shows daily. Operating since 1971, it's part of the Grand Country resort that includes an on-site inn, water park, buffet restaurant, and indoor attractions. The venue produces over 1,000 performances per year across multiple show formats appealing to all ages.

## History

Established in 1971, Grand Country Music Hall has been bringing family entertainment to Branson for over 50 years. Signature show "Grand Jubilee" recently celebrated 20 years on stage. Anchored by house bands New South and The Rhinestone Mafia.

## Programming & Shows

- **Format**: Variety shows - country music, gospel, comedy
- **Frequency**: Three shows per day, 1,000+ performances per year
- **Show Lineup**:
  - **Grand Jubilee** (signature show, 20 years running) - country/variety
  - **Comedy Jamboree** - Branson's only comedy improv show with audience participation
  - **Pets & Giggles** - family interactive show with pets, skits, games
  - **New South Gospel** - 2-hour Southern Gospel showcase (voted Branson's Best Quartet)
  - **Down Home Country** - traditional country music
  - **Ozarks Gospel** - regional gospel
  - **Ozarks Country** - regional country
- **Atmosphere**: Family-friendly, all ages welcome
- **Notable Performers**: New South (gospel quartet), The Rhinestone Mafia (house band), Tracy Heaston (piano virtuoso)
