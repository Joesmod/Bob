# Riverside Amphitheater

## Overview
Outdoor amphitheater and entertainment complex in Van Buren, Missouri, combining live concert programming with an on-site RV park. Known as "Riverside On The Current," the venue hosts country and rock concerts in a scenic riverside setting.

## History
Established venue on the Current River serving the southeastern Missouri region. The combination of concert programming and RV camping creates a destination entertainment experience for regional audiences.
