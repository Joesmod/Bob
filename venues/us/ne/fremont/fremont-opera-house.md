# Fremont Opera House

## Overview
Community-run historic opera house in Fremont, NE. Hosts concerts, theatrical performances, luncheons, and special events. Also offers space rental and runs a middle school summer arts camp. Active venue with regular programming.

## History
Historic venue (specific founding date not provided on website). Renovated and maintained by community volunteers and supporters. Operates as a community arts venue with emphasis on preserving the historic building while providing contemporary programming.

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 70/100 — Moderate Fit, Currently Using BookTix**
Decent candidate but currently using BookTix for ticketing. Fremont Opera House is an active community venue with regular programming (concerts, plays, events) and they're already selling tickets online. BookTix is an established ticketing platform, so switching would require demonstrating clear advantages: better pricing, superior features, easier management, or better customer experience. The venue appears community-run with limited staff, so ease-of-use would be important. Moderate volume (1-4 events/month) means potential for growth but not high-volume revenue. Worth approaching if Opendate can offer compelling value over their current BookTix setup, especially around pricing/fees and ease of use for volunteer-run operations.
