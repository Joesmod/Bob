## About

**Important:** The website listed for "Red Cloud Opera House" (willacather.org) is actually the **National Willa Cather Center** and the **Willa Cather Foundation**, a nonprofit organization dedicated to the author Willa Cather.

The foundation owns and operates the nation's largest collection of nationally-designated historic sites dedicated to an American author, including:
- National Willa Cather Center
- Willa Cather Childhood Home (National Historic Landmark)
- Multiple historic buildings from Cather's life
