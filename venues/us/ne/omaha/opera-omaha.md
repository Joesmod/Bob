## About

Opera Omaha is a **performing arts organization**, not a venue. They are a growth-minded, innovative opera company that produces opera performances in multiple formats and styles, and creates artist-led programs for community service organizations.

## Mission

"Producing opera performances in multiple formats and styles and co-creating artist-led programs for a variety of community service organizations. We believe the power of opera is transformational, and as a result supports the creation of an inventive, creative, empathetic, and inclusive community that inspires joy, self-discovery, kinder discourse, and opportunity for all."

## What They Do

- Produce opera performances at **other venues** (they don't own/operate a theater)
- Community arts programs
- Artist-led community service initiatives
- Multiple performance formats and styles

## Ticketing

Opera Omaha sells tickets to their productions, but they perform at rental venues around Omaha (concert halls, theaters, etc.). They are a **touring company/producer**, not a venue operator.
