# The Rose Theater

## Overview
Professional theater for young audiences, serving the Omaha area for 75+ years. Produces mainstage theatrical productions for children and families, plus extensive educational programming through the Shirlee & Henry Fonda Theatre Academy. Two locations: main theater downtown (2001 Farnam St.) and Rose Studios for Youth Artists (12202 W Center Rd.) for classes and camps.

## History
Founded as the Omaha Theater Company over 75 years ago (around 1949). Generations of families have grown up attending shows. Mission: to enrich the lives of children and families through live theater arts and education. Professional staff of award-winning actors, teaching artists, technical artists, and administrators.

## Opendate Fit
**Score: 70/100 — Mixed Opportunity**
Interesting candidate with dual revenue streams: show tickets and class/camp registrations. The Rose currently uses JackrabbitClass for education registrations, which is a specialized platform. If they're using a separate system for show tickets, they have fragmented systems which creates operational friction. Opendate could potentially offer value if it can handle both ticketing and class/workshop registration in one platform, simplifying their operations and providing unified customer data. 

**Challenges**: The Rose is a well-established professional organization with likely entrenched systems and processes. JackrabbitClass is education-focused with features (attendance tracking, family accounts, recurring billing) that Opendate would need to match. The education side is likely their larger revenue driver than ticket sales. 

**Opportunity**: If Opendate has (or can build) robust class/workshop registration features, The Rose could be a strong candidate looking to consolidate systems. Worth exploring their current pain points and whether unified ticketing+registration would appeal to them.
