# Sandhills Global Event Center

## Overview
Large multi-purpose event center and fairgrounds complex in Lincoln, NE. Over 500+ event days annually with up to 564,000 visitor days per year. Home to the Super Fair and contracted host of the National High School Finals Rodeo (NHSFR) for 2026 and 2027. Seven branded event spaces within the complex.

## History
Originally the Lancaster County fairgrounds, rebranded as Sandhills Global Event Center in December 2023 when Sandhills Global company sponsored naming rights. A 501(c)3 nonprofit organization. Currently completing a $10 million modernization funded by a State of Nebraska Shovel Ready Grant (awarded October 2023, completion target end of 2025).
