## About

New York Comedy Club Stamford delivers the quintessential stand-up comedy experience with professional-grade talent in an authentic NYC-style comedy club atmosphere. Located in Stamford Town Center, the club features top-tier national headliners, showcase lineups, and open mic nights with full food and beverage service (popcorn, appetizers, drinks).

## History

Opened February 2023 at Stamford Town Center, bringing the tradition of the original Midtown New York Comedy Club (founded 1989) to Connecticut. For over 30 years, the NYC brand has been at the heart of the New York and national comedy scenes, championing both established stars and emerging talent.
