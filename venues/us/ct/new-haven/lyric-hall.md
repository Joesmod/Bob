# Lyric Hall

## Overview
Historic intimate performance venue in the West Rock neighborhood of New Haven, CT. Originally built as a vaudeville theater in 1913, restored by owner John Cavaliere starting in 2006. Small, speakeasy-style venue hosting singer-songwriters, cabaret, and acoustic performances.

## History
Built in 1913 as the West Rock Theater, a vaudeville and silent movie auditorium. Purchased by John Cavaliere in 2006, who discovered the original proscenium intact and spent years restoring the space. Operated as a restoration project and labor of love rather than a commercial venue.
