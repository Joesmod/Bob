# Thomaston Opera House

## Overview

The Thomaston Opera House is home to Landmark Community Theatre in downtown Thomaston, CT. Historic venue hosting year-round performances including musicals, plays, concerts, and youth theatre programs. Uses Ludus ticketing platform.

## History

The Thomaston Opera House has been serving the community for over a century. Now operated by Landmark Community Theatre, a community-based nonprofit dedicated to bringing exceptional performances to the area.

## Opendate Fit

**Score: 70/100 — Good Candidate**

Landmark Community Theatre at the Thomaston Opera House is an active ticketed venue using **Ludus** (a switchable platform). Strong programming mix of community theater, professional concerts, and youth education. Active audience engagement with local partnerships (Thomaston EATS program).

**Strong Points**:
- Uses Ludus (switchable platform)
- Year-round programming with mix of shows
- Strong community ties and volunteer base
- Youth programs bring family audiences
- Professional touring acts (Grammy nominees) alongside community theater

**Considerations**:
- Community theater model - volunteer-based
- Small market (Thomaston, CT population ~7,000)
- Nonprofit budget constraints
- Would need to demonstrate clear value/ROI for platform switch

**Opportunity**: Good small-market candidate if Opendate can offer competitive pricing to community theaters. Strong local support and active programming make this a stable operation.
