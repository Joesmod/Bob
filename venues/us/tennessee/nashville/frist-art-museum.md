# Frist Art Museum

## Overview
Nonprofit art exhibition center in Nashville's former main post office building (1984 National Register of Historic Places). 124,400 sq ft art deco landmark with 45,000+ sq ft of exhibition space. Celebrating 25th anniversary in 2026.

## History
Built as Nashville's main post office, converted to art museum. Presents 12-15 exhibitions annually representing various cultures and artistic traditions.

## Programming & Shows
- **Type**: Art museum with exhibitions and educational programs
- **Events**: Gallery tours, art classes, lectures, special events
- **Admission**: $20 adults, $16 seniors/military/students, free under 18
- **Hours**: Thu-Mon (closed Tue-Wed)
