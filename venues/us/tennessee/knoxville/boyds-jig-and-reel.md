# Boyd's Jig & Reel Whisky Bar

## Overview
Scottish-Irish pub and whisky bar celebrating Appalachian heritage and its Scottish roots. Features nightly live music, over 1,000 whiskies, and authentic Scottish fare. Intimate venue with a smaller stage hosting Celtic, Irish, Scottish, Old Time, Country, Bluegrass, Singer-songwriter, and Americana performances.

## History
Founded by Jenny Boyd to pay homage to the Scottish roots of Appalachian history. Celebrates the heritage of Scots-Irish immigrants who settled in East Tennessee during the 1600s through musical events, whisky, and Scottish cuisine.
