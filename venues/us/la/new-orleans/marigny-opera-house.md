## About

Historic former church converted to a premier wedding and event venue in New Orleans' Marigny neighborhood. While originally established as a "church of the arts," the venue now primarily focuses on weddings, private events, and corporate rentals. Occasional curated performances (chamber music, acoustic shows) are held, but these are not the primary business model.

## History

Former neighborhood church repurposed as a non-denominational "church of the arts" committed to developing performing artists in New Orleans. High arched ceilings create ideal acoustics for acoustic performance. Now nationally recognized as one of the most beautiful wedding venues in the country.

## Venue Policies

Strict preservation policies for the historic building:
- No open flames (LED candles only)
- Amplified music case-by-case approval
- All weddings require professional planner from approved list
- No adhesives/hardware on walls
- Biodegradable confetti only, no glitter
- No pampas grass or similar dried florals
