# Razzoo Bar & Patio

## Overview
Bourbon Street bar and patio with live music. Located at 511 Bourbon St in the heart of the French Quarter. Classic New Orleans bar experience with no cover charge.

## Programming & Shows
- **Live music**: Sunday through Thursday nights
- **DJs & MCs**: Friday and Saturday (weekend party atmosphere)
- **Drink specials**: 3-for-1 until 6pm daily
- **Private events**: Available for bachelorette parties, birthdays, special occasions

## Hours
- Opens 12pm Friday-Sunday
- Opens 3pm Monday-Thursday
