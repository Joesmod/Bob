# New Marigny Theatre

## Overview
"A Sacred Space For Exploration" — Multi-arts performance venue in New Orleans' Marigny neighborhood hosting classical music, opera, jazz, dance, theater, and experimental performances. Active calendar with diverse programming from chamber orchestras to puppet festivals.

## History
Part of the Church of Arts and Sciences organization. Mission is to inspire and incubate creative and community collaborations. Plans include artist-in-residence program and producing new work with emphasis on sacred arts.

## Programming & Shows
Extensive calendar of ticketed performances including:
- **Classical Music**: Louisiana Philharmonic Orchestra, chamber players, baroque ensembles
- **Opera**: New Orleans Opera productions, immersive opera experiences
- **Jazz**: Various jazz ensembles and tributes
- **Theater**: Original plays, musicals (Queer New Theatre productions)
- **Dance**: Artivism Dance, ecstatic dance events
- **Special Events**: New Orleans Giant Puppet Festival
