Historic community performing arts center in Downtown Hammond hosting concerts, dance, theatre, musicals, and special events year-round.

## Mission

Community. Creativity. Expression. Imagination. Education. Empowerment. Entertainment. Dreams. Friendship.

The Columbia believes in discovering, cultivating, and celebrating artistic talent from Baton Rouge to Slidell, from LaPlace to Kentwood and all points in between. Amateur and professional artists work and learn side by side with students of all ages and the versatile Columbia staff.

The stunning historic facility showcases the best the region has to offer on a scale only the Columbia can provide. Actors, dancers, singers, musicians, designers, and visual artists all have a home here.

## Programming & Shows

- Classical and contemporary concerts
- Dance performances
- Theatre productions
- Musicals
- Special events
- Student productions
- Community programming

Focus on **community talent development** — amateur and professional artists collaborating with students to create professional-level productions.
