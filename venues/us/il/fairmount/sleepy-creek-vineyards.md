## About

Winery and event venue in rural Illinois, 30 miles from Champaign-Urbana and about 2 hours from Chicago and Indianapolis. Features a beautiful timber frame barn housing the winery and tasting room, plus a separate event venue called the "Dragonfly." Offers wine tastings, lodging (B&W — "bed and wine"), and hosts ticketed entertainment events. Serene rural setting perfect for events and getaways.

## Programming & Shows

- **Event Types**: House concerts, comedy shows, theatrical performances, annual Sleepy Creek Art & Music Festival
- **Venues**: 
  - Tasting room (up to 75 people after hours)
  - Dragonfly venue (up to 250 guests)
- **Model**: Mix of ticketed public events and private event rentals
- **Private Events**: Weddings (popular!), corporate events, parties
