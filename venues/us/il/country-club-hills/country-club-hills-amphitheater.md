# Country Club Hills Amphitheater

## Overview
Municipal open-air amphitheater in Country Club Hills, IL, established in 2006. Features a 2,500 square foot open stage with LED video walls and premium sound system. Focuses on R&B and soul music during spring and summer months. Located at 4200 W. 183rd Street near I-57 and I-80.

## History
Established in 2006 as a municipal entertainment venue on the downtown Country Club Hills scenic 183rd Street commerce strip. Operates as a seasonal outdoor venue, hosting events spring through summer.
