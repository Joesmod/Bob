## About

Ice hockey training and community rink facility. Features multiple rinks for freestyle skating, pick-up hockey, stick & puck sessions, and youth hockey programs.

## Programming

- Open freestyle sessions (Rinks 2 & 3)
- RAT (Rec Adult Team) beginner pick-up hockey
- Stick & puck sessions
- Youth hockey leagues and training
- Community skating programs
