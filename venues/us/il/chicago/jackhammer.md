# Jackhammer

## Overview
LGBTQ+ bar and nightclub in Chicago, established over 20 years ago. Known for leather/denim/kink-friendly events, weekly DJ nights, and special parties. This is a BAR with event programming, not a traditional ticketing venue.

## History
Operating since approximately 2000, Jackhammer has been a staple of Chicago's LGBTQ+ community for over two decades. Known for embracing diverse subcultures within the queer community - leather, rubber, denim, and general LGBTQ+ socializing. Describes itself as a "judgment-free zone" for exploration and self-expression.
