## About

Sound-Bar is Chicago's premier upscale nightclub, revolutionizing the city's nightlife with style, luxury, and state-of-the-art technology. The 20,000 square-foot venue features European minimalist decor, flat screen monitors, laser lights, and cutting-edge sound components.

The club showcases world-class DJ talent year-round, featuring an event calendar of international electronic music artists. The venue includes:
- 4,000 sq ft main dance floor
- 9 bars across 2 levels
- 4 boutique lounges
- Exclusive VIP room
- Floor-to-ceiling video projection walls

Sound-Bar also serves as an event space for private functions and corporate gatherings, with customizable areas that can be rented individually.
