# Alhambra Palace Restaurant

## Overview
Middle Eastern/Mediterranean fine dining restaurant with live entertainment. Opened 2007. Moroccan palace-themed restaurant featuring belly dancing and Middle Eastern singers on Friday and Saturday nights. Primarily a restaurant with entertainment, not a ticketed event venue.

## History
Dr. Rustom collected artifacts over several years from trips to Middle East and North Africa. Commissioned artisans from Syria, Lebanon, Egypt, and Morocco to recreate Moorish art, hand-carved furniture, sculptures, rugs, stone mosaics, arabesques, and crystal chandeliers.
