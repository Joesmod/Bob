# Porchlight Music Theatre

## Overview
Professional theater company producing musicals in Chicago's Gold Coast neighborhood. Founded in 1995, now in its 30th season. Performs at the Ruth Page Center for the Arts. This is a producing theater company (creates its own shows) rather than a venue that books external acts.

## History
Founded in 1995 by Artistic Director Jill Moore and a group of artists. Under L. Walter Stearns' artistic leadership from 1998-2011, added first Equity contracts in 2004. Michael Weber became Artistic Director in 2010. Moved to Ruth Page Center for the Arts in fall 2017 after record-breaking run of "In The Heights" (78 performances).

## Programming & Shows
- **Type**: Professional theater - musicals only
- **Programming**: Mainstage productions (4-5 per season), Porchlight Revisits series (rarely produced musical gems), New Faces Sing Broadway revues, Broadway in your Backyard (free summer concerts)
- **Recent productions**: RENT, Cabaret, Ernest Shackleton Loves Me, The Irish and How They Got That Way
- **Ticket prices**: Subscription-based season + single tickets (specific prices not listed)
- **Recognition**: 178 Joseph Jefferson Award nominations, 50 Jeff awards, 33 Black Theatre Alliance nominations, 12 BTA awards
- Graduated to "Large Theatre tier" in 2019
