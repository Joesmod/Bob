# Movement on Montrose

## Overview
Community dance studio and event space in Chicago. Provides three multi-purpose dance studios and a flexible event space (70 capacity) for non-profit organizations, small businesses, and community groups. This is a DANCE STUDIO that rents space, not a ticketing venue.

## History
Established as a mission-driven community space to foster "inspiration and collaboration." Affiliated with Logan Foundation (staff email addresses are @loganfdn.org).

## Programming & Shows
- **Type**: Dance classes, meditation, workshops, community events
- **Regular programs**: Flow Collective prop workshops (monthly), Phoenix Dance Fitness, Ballet Unboxed (open level ballet), Nichiren Shu Buddhism services, meditation workshops
- **Capacity**: Event space holds up to 70 people
- **Ticket prices**: Most events are free or low-cost ($10-$25 for workshops)
- **Ticketing**: Some presenters use Humanitix or direct registration (Cashdrop, direct signup)

The space is RENTED to community organizations who run their own programming. MoM provides the facility; tenant organizations handle ticketing/registration for their own events.
