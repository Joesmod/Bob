# The Burlington

## Overview
High-volume indie/punk/alternative music venue in Chicago's Logan Square/Avondale area. Known for extremely active booking calendar with shows nearly every night. Intimate bar setting with dedicated performance space. DIY/underground music scene hub.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Indie rock, punk, emo, hardcore, experimental, noise, alternative
- **Frequency**: 25-30 shows per month (nearly every night)
- **Ticket prices**: $10-25 typical (most shows $12-18)
- **Event Types**: Multi-band bills (3-5 bands per show), matinee shows, album releases
- **Audience**: Underground/DIY music scene, local bands + touring acts
- **Notable Programming**:
  - Regular matinee weekend shows
  - Emerging artist showcases
  - Genre-diverse bookings (same night: punk/metal/electronic)
  - Very active March-May calendar visible on site
