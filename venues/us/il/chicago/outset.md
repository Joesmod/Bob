## About

Chicago's haven for live music. Outset is a modern music venue designed to offer the production value of a large-scale venue coupled with the vibe of an intimate and up-close setting. The venue opened in 2024 to provide the perfect atmosphere for established artists while creating a new room for rising talent.

## History

Opened in 2024, Outset represents the next chapter in Chicago's storied music history. Built to welcome rising stars and iconic live acts from around the world with world-class production in an intimate setting.
