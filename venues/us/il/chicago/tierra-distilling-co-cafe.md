# Tierra Distilling Co. & Cafe

## Overview
Craft distillery and cafe in Clarendon Hills (Chicago area) that doubles as a community event venue. Produces brandies, whiskies, liqueurs, and coffee using rescued fruit, unique raw ingredients, and innovative techniques. Strong focus on sustainability and planet-conscious practices. Hosts a wide variety of ticketed community events including comedy shows, seasonal festivals, mahjong nights, and cultural celebrations.

## History
A venture focused on creating unique flavors while making a positive impact on the world. Uses sustainable practices and rescued fruit for their spirits production. Has become a community gathering place in downtown Clarendon Hills.

## Programming & Shows
- **Event types**: Comedy shows, cultural festivals, community gatherings, workshops
- **Frequency**: Multiple events per month, year-round programming
- **Notable recurring events**:
  - Taps & Friends Comedy Jamfest (comedy showcases with professional lineups)
  - Oktoberfest (4th annual - German fare, beer, games under tent)
  - Distillinois (Illinois craft spirits festival with samples, bands, food)
  - Daisy Days (annual Clarendon Hills celebration)
  - Friendsgiving celebration
  - Mahjong and Cocktails nights (ticketed beginner classes)
  - Seasonal events (St. Paddy's flights, Halloween Walk, Holiday Walk)
  - Design workshops, fitness classes, kittens & cocktails
  - Rivian test drive events
- **Ticket prices**: Typically $15-30 for workshops, comedy shows priced competitively
- **Vibe**: Community-focused, family-friendly during day events, adult entertainment at night
