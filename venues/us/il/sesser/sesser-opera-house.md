# Sesser Opera House

## Overview

## History

The original Sesser Opera House was built in 1904 but was destroyed by fire. The current Spanish Colonial-style building was erected in 1914. After restoration, the opera house is now used for community theater and musical programs. The building was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1982, recognizing its historical and architectural significance.

The venue includes the Opera House Theater on the main level and the Woll Banquet Room on the second floor, which can be rented for events.
