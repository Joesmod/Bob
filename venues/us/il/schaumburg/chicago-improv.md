## About

Chicago Improv is part of the national Improv Comedy Club chain, one of the most recognized comedy club brands in the United States. Located inside Woodfield Mall in Schaumburg, IL (northwest Chicago suburbs), this venue hosts touring comedians and comedy shows with dinner and drink service.

The venue explicitly warns customers to only purchase tickets through improv.com or TicketWeb.com, and will deny admission to tickets purchased from unauthorized resale sites (StubHub, SeatGeek, Vivid Seats, etc.).

## History

Part of the nationwide Improv Comedy Clubs franchise. The Improv brand has a long history dating back to the original Improvisation comedy club in New York City (1963), though individual franchise locations were established at various times.
