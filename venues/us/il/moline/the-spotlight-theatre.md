# The Spotlight Theatre

## Overview
Family-owned community theater in downtown Moline, Illinois (Quad Cities area). Features main stage musical theater productions, children's theater company, and weekly ComedySportz improv shows in an intimate studio setting.

## History
Long-standing community theater in the Quad Cities region. Described as "Entertaining for an evening, and inspiring for a lifetime" with "a lifetime of experience." Family-owned and operated, the theater has become a cornerstone of Moline's cultural scene.

## Programming & Shows
- **Main Stage**: Full-scale musicals (recent: *The Secret Garden*, upcoming productions throughout the season)
- **ComedySportz**: Every Friday & Saturday night - family-friendly improv comedy
- **The After Show**: Special improv performances featuring veteran performers (30+ years combined experience)
- **Children's Company**: Youth theater productions and programming
- **Summer Arts**: Youth theater camps and training programs
- **The Blueprint Bar + Lounge**: On-site food and beverage service
- **Special Events**: "Secret Garden Soiree" tea experiences tied to productions

**Ticket Prices:**
- Main Stage Shows: $24-$30
- ComedySportz: ~$10
- Special Events: $25-$30 (tea experiences)
- Children's programs: Varies

**Frequency**: Regular weekly programming plus rotating main stage productions
