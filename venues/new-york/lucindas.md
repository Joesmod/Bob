# Lucinda's

## Overview
**Location:** East Village, New York, NY  
**Venue Type:** Country bar & live music venue  
**Tagline:** "Best Country Bar in NYC"  
**Website:** https://www.lucindasnyc.com  

## About
Lucinda's is New York City's premier country bar located in the East Village, offering live music, dance floor, DJ programming, and a vintage speakeasy atmosphere. The venue combines classic country bar vibes with modern NYC nightlife energy, featuring plush furniture, bottle service, and a dedicated music performance space.

## Recent Notable Events
**The Black Keys Surprise Show** - The venue recently hosted a surprise performance by The Black Keys, demonstrating its ability to attract major touring artists for intimate shows. Entry was free, first-come-first-served, with limited capacity due to the intimate venue size.

## Venue Layout

### Front Room Bar
- **Capacity:** 100 people
- **Bar Service:** Eight beers on tap, assorted bottled beer, wine, champagne, full liquor
- **Staffing:** Full bar with 2-3 bartenders
- **Seating:** Five booths with full bottle service options
- **Features:**
  - Assorted plush furniture for lounging
  - Main dance floor
  - DJ booth with club lighting and fog machine
  - Projector with screen on stage

### Back Room Bar
- **Bar Service:** Basic spirits and bottled beer
- **Staffing:** One bartender
- **Seating:** Banquet couch seating
- **Features:**
  - Option to play different music from front room
  - Option for separate DJ
