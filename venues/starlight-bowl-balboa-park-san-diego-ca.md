# Starlight Bowl Balboa Park

## Overview
Starlight Bowl is a historic outdoor amphitheater located in Balboa Park, San Diego. Currently closed for restoration, the venue is operated by Save Starlight, a nonprofit organization working to restore and reopen this iconic San Diego performance space. The venue has hosted legendary performers throughout its history and is working toward reopening with a vision to continue its legacy of bringing live performances to the San Diego community.

## History
Starlight Bowl has a rich performance history spanning decades in Balboa Park. The venue has hosted numerous legends of American music, including performances by Pete DeLuke (at 96, still performing) who played with Benny Goodman, Jimmy Dorsey, Charlie Barnet, Gene Krupa, Buddy Rich, Frank Sinatra, Tony Bennett, Ella Fitzgerald, and many others. The amphitheater is currently undergoing restoration efforts led by the Save Starlight organization. On April 26, 2026, the organization is hosting "A Starlight Swing" gala fundraiser to honor Pete DeLuke and raise funds for restoration.
