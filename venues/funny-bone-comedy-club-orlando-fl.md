# Funny Bone Comedy Club - Orlando

## Overview
Funny Bone Comedy Club in Orlando is part of the national Funny Bone comedy club chain, located at Pointe Orlando (9101 International Drive). Operating as a full-service comedy club and restaurant, the venue features national touring comedians with shows typically running Thursday through Sunday. The club offers dinner service, full bar, and both general admission and VIP seating options.

## History
Funny Bone is a national comedy club chain with locations across the United States. The Orlando location operates as a franchise or corporate-owned venue within the Pointe Orlando entertainment complex. As part of a larger chain, it benefits from national booking relationships and brand recognition while serving the Orlando market with both tourist and local audiences.
