## About
The Red Room at Cafe 939 is Boston's premier all-ages concert venue, operated by Berklee College of Music. The venue serves as a showcase platform for discovering the best new music, featuring both emerging artists and established performers. As part of Berklee's educational mission, it provides students with real-world performance opportunities while bringing diverse musical programming to the Boston community.

## Programming
The Red Room hosts a wide variety of events including:
- **Emerging Artist Showcases** - Platform for new talent discovery
- **Educational Events** - Master classes, workshops, and demonstrations (e.g., Keys Fest with gospel music heritage, Bass Bash featuring legendary bassists)
- **Cultural Celebrations** - Student organization events like the annual PadThai Show celebrating Thai musical heritage
- **Genre-Diverse Concerts** - Jazz, pop, rock, world music, gospel, and more
- **Faculty & Student Performances** - Berklee community showcases

Many events are **free admission**, making live music accessible to all.
