# Lyric Hyperion Theater & Cafe

## Overview
The Lyric Hyperion Theater & Cafe is a historic neighborhood-friendly cabaret and performance space in Silver Lake, Los Angeles. Operating as an independent, anti-corporate venue, it combines live comedy performances with a full-service cafe. The venue emphasizes its community roots and artist-first approach, hosting live comedy 7 nights a week alongside drag, music, storytelling, improv, and sketch performances. The cafe is open daily from 9am until close.

## History
The Lyric Hyperion has over 60 years of history supporting unique artists and performances in Silver Lake. Described as a "historic Silver Lake cabaret gem," it has been a community staple for independent and alternative performance art. The venue proudly maintains its independent status as an anti-corporate performance space.
