# OPERA San Antonio

## Overview
**Location:** San Antonio, TX  
**Primary Venue:** Tobin Center for the Performing Arts  
**Organization Type:** Professional opera company  
**Website:** https://www.operasa.org  

## About
OPERA San Antonio (OSA) is a professional opera company dedicated to producing opera of uncompromising artistic quality and enriching the community through educational outreach and social engagement. The company is committed to preserving and sharing opera as an art form that is relevant, inspirational, and accessible to residents and visitors of all ages and backgrounds.

**Civic Mission:** The fundamental premise of OPERA San Antonio is that the community needs, and the citizens deserve, access to the major performing arts.

## Programming Philosophy

### Main Stage Productions
Showcases masterpieces of the operatic repertoire with world-class productions at the Tobin Center, bringing grand opera to San Antonio.

### Experimental Second Stage
Deeply explores the art form in diverse venues across the city, featuring:
- Modern works
- Productions speaking to San Antonio's diverse communities
- Works that add depth to the city's social fabric

### Recent Productions
- **The Magic Flute** (Mozart) - State-of-the-art adaptation inspired by silent film artistry
- **A Starlit Night Concert** - Special events
- **Isabel Leonard in Concert** (January 2026) - 3-time Grammy-winning mezzo-soprano

## Organizational Goals
1. **Presenting Extraordinary Artists** - Established, emerging, and local talent creating transcendent experiences
2. **Social Responsibility** - Broadening opera's impact through productions in diverse venues throughout the city
3. **Grand Opera for a Grand City** - World-class productions of operatic masterpieces
4. **Educational Outreach** - Breaking stereotypes, promoting opera as accessible and relevant, cultivating love for opera in youth

## Why Opera is Extraordinary (OSA Philosophy)
- Performed on the unique instrument of the human voice, trained for years, using no microphones
- Sound that literally expands our sense of hearing
- About people in moments of deeply felt human emotion
- The human voice creates emotion like no other instrument
- "What is too subtle to be said, or too deeply felt, or too revealing, or too mysterious — these things can be sung, and can only be sung." — Kenneth Clark
- **Opera is magical**
