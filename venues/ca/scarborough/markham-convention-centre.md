# Markham Convention Centre

## Overview
Luxury banquet hall and reception venue in Scarborough/Toronto area. Four tastefully decorated banquet halls accommodating up to 900 guests. Recognized as the 17th loveliest banquet hall in Canada by Wedding Bells magazine. Specializes in weddings, corporate functions, and social events.

## History
Established banquet hall recognized by Wedding Bells magazine. Exact founding date not provided on website. Has built reputation as one of Toronto's premier private event venues.
