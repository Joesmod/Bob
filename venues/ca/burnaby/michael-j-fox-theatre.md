# Michael J Fox Theatre

## Overview
Professional community-style theatre located within Burnaby South Secondary School in Burnaby, BC. Opened in 1993 and named after actor Michael J. Fox in 1995 in recognition of his achievements and commitment to community. One of the busiest venues in the Lower Mainland, hosting multicultural performances, dance, theatre, music, and community events.

## History
Built as part of Burnaby South Secondary School in 1993. Officially named after Burnaby native Michael J. Fox in November 1995. Established as a focal point for culture, education, business, and community. Operated by an independent non-profit society (Michael J Fox Theatre Society Board) that functions independently of the Burnaby School District.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Theatre, dance, music, multicultural performances
- **Event Types**: Amateur and professional performances, community events, cultural programming
- **Frequency**: Steady schedule year-round, very active venue
- **Mission**: Enhance Burnaby's cultural fabric with practical, affordable venue for artists
