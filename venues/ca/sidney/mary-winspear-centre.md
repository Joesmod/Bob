# Mary Winspear Centre

## Overview
Community performing arts centre and multi-use venue in Sidney, BC. 30,000 square-foot facility with theatre, banquet hall, boardroom, and meeting spaces. Fully wheelchair-accessible. Operated as a non-profit by the Saanich Peninsula Memorial Park Society.

## History
Established by the Memorial Park Society (founded 1921). The Society originally managed North Saanich War Memorial Park. Took on management of SANSCHA Hall in 1993, and was central to planning and construction of the Mary Winspear Centre.
