# Orpheus Musical Theatre

## Overview
Canada's longest-running musical theatre organization, founded in 1906 in Ottawa. Community-led theatre company producing three major musicals per season at Meridian Theatres @ Centrepointe (430-seat venue). Non-profit organization that welcomes participants from across the National Capital Region.

## History
Founded in 1906 by James Alexander Smith, a Scotsman from Dundee who brought his love of music to Canada. Originally started as the Orpheus Glee Club with its first concert on January 15, 1907. Transitioned from a choral group to an operatic society during World War I, presenting staged operettas. Has been continuously operating for 119 years, making it Canada's oldest musical theatre organization.

## Programming & Shows
- **Genres**: Classic Broadway musicals, contemporary shows
- **2025-2026 Season**: Annie (completed), Jesus Christ Superstar, Singin' in the Rain
- **Frequency**: 3 full productions per season
- **Ticket prices**: $30-$52 (Adult), $25 rush tickets
- **Notable features**: Also runs "Schoolhouse" youth training programs (ages 6-18), special events like Show Tune Showdown
