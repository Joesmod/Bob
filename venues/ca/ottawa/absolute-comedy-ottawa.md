## About

Stand-up comedy club at 412 Preston St in Ottawa. Part of the Absolute Comedy chain with locations in Ottawa, Toronto, and Kingston. Features touring comedians from across North America performing 7 nights a week. Full food and beverage service available. 19+ venue (under 19 admitted with adult but not recommended under 16).
