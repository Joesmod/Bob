## About

Montreal Comedy Club is a prolific English-language comedy promotion company operating stand-up shows 7 nights a week across multiple venues in Montreal. Rather than operating a single fixed venue, they partner with restaurants and bars to host nightly comedy shows with rotating lineups of 8 comedians per show. They position themselves as "the ultimate destination for top-notch stand-up comedy" and run multiple showtimes on weekends (double and triple shows).

**Venues they use regularly:**
- **Deli Planet** (895 de La Gauchetière, inside Central Station) - Thursday-Saturday shows
- **McKibbin's** (1426 Bishop) - Monday & Tuesday shows
- **Estrella** (1165 St Laurent) - Wednesday & Saturday shows
- **Brass Door** (2171 Crescent) - Sunday shows

## Programming & Shows

- **Genres**: English stand-up comedy - local comedians, rising stars, and international talent
- **Frequency**: 7 nights a week with multiple shows on weekends
- **Format**: 8 comedians per show
- **Ticket prices**: 
  - Monday: $5 online (early bird)
  - Weekdays: $10 online / $15 at door
  - Weekends: $15 online / $20 at door
  - (+ tax)
- **Timing**: Doors 6:30-7pm, shows 8-9pm depending on venue/day
- **Notable features**: Full dinner & bar menu at all venues; 1-item minimum purchase required

The club emphasizes providing fresh lineups with rotating talent, ensuring "you'll never have the same experience twice."
