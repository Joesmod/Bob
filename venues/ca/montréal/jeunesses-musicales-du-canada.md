# Jeunesses Musicales du Canada

## Overview
Classical music organization based in Montréal, operating the Joseph-Rouleau Hall. Presents classical music concerts and educational programming for all ages. Offers two concert series: "Concerts Apéro" (Happy Hour Concerts) for adults and "Concertinos famille" (Cushion Concerts) for families. Part of the international Jeunesses Musicales network promoting classical music appreciation.

## History
Member of the global Jeunesses Musicales federation, an international organization founded in 1945 dedicated to enabling young people to develop through music across all boundaries. JM Canada carries this mission in the Canadian context, presenting professional classical performances and educational workshops.
