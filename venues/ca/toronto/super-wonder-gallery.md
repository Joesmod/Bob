Multidisciplinary art gallery and event space in Toronto's Bloor West Village. Hosts immersive art exhibitions, film festivals, fashion shows, burlesque performances, folk dancing (Balfolk), theater, live music, and private events. Known for larger-than-life themed experiences and supporting emerging artists.

## History

Founded in 2012 by artist/curator Christian Aldo. The gallery has built its reputation as a space for budding artists to grow and share creativity with a community of peers. Cultivates "the weird, wild and zany" through themed exhibitions ranging from science fiction to eroticism, from monsters to shock art.

## Programming & Shows

- **Art Exhibitions**: Immersive themed shows (The Candy Factory, AQUATICA, Super Naughty Show, Andy Warhol Party)
- **Film Festivals**: Super 5 Film Festival (short films by local filmmakers, Q&A format)
- **Fashion Shows**: Fashion Wars series (designer competitions with runway shows)
- **Burlesque**: Rouge Burlesque Showcase, Fox Burlesque
- **Balfolk Dancing**: Weekly Monday folk dancing events (European traditional dances with live music)
- **Theater**: Ten Minute Theatre productions
- **Live Music**: Record release parties, Good Vibes Party concerts
- **Special Events**: Paint & Sip sessions, Holiday themed parties, New Year's Eve events
- **Frequency**: Multiple events per week (2-4 events weekly)
- **Ticket Prices**: $0-$67.80
  - Pay-what-you-can events (Balfolk sessions)
  - $15-$25 typical ticketed events
  - $40-$67.80 for specialty experiences (paint parties, immersive exhibitions)
