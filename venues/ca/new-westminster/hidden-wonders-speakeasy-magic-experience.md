## About

Hidden behind a fake business facade in New Westminster's historic downtown, Hidden Wonders is a speakeasy-style intimate magic theater seating only 30 guests. Represents a new trend in magic entertainment focusing on close-up, intimate experiences rather than grand-scale illusions. Address is revealed only to confirmed ticket holders. The furthest seat is just 13 feet from the stage, with front row VIP seats approximately 3 feet away. Features tiered seating, wheelchair accessibility, and a "secret location" speakeasy aesthetic. Shows sell out quickly due to limited capacity.

## History

Venue was designed with accessibility in mind (founder Shawn Farquhar's mother used a wheelchair). Part of downtown New Westminster's revitalized arts scene. The venue also hosts affiliated shows: The Cabaret of Wonders (award-winning magicians Billy and Alex) and The Wacky Weekend Magic Show (family-oriented magic show for young children).
