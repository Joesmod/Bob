Performing arts theater operated by St. Clair College Centre for the Arts in Windsor, Ontario. High-volume venue presenting comedy, music, theater, and children's entertainment. Home to local theater companies including Windsor Light Music Theatre, GoldHaus Productions, Dance Barre, and The Windsor Comedy Festival.

## History

Part of St. Clair College Centre for the Arts. The venue has hosted major national acts including Rick Mercer, Norm Macdonald, Whose Live Anyway?, The Wiggles, Max and Ruby, and Bubble Guppies.
