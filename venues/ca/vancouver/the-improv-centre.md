## About

The Improv Centre is Vancouver's premier live comedy venue located on Granville Island. The 186-seat waterfront theatre specializes in improvised comedy and is internationally recognized for flagship Theatresports™ shows. As a registered charitable organization, they showcase the art of improvisation while also providing improv classes and workshops for all skill levels, supporting entertainment and arts education in the community.

## History

The venue has been operating on Granville Island for 40+ years, building their performance style around Theatresports™ (created by Keith Johnstone and trademarked by the International Theatresports™ Institute).

## Programming & Shows

**Main Shows:**
- **Theatresports™** - Fridays & Saturdays 7:30pm (PG): High-energy improv competition with two teams battling for audience laughs
- **Theatresports™ Unhinged** - Fridays & Saturdays 9:30pm (14A): Edgier version of the classic format
- **I Saw You** - Wednesdays 7:30pm (18A): Improv based on romantic "I Saw You" newspaper columns
- **Blockbuster** - Thursdays 7:30pm (PG): Audience-voted improvised movie creation
- **True Story!** - Wednesdays 7:30pm (PG): Audience shares true stories that fuel improv scenes
- **House Teams Showcase** - Select Sundays 7:30pm ($10)
- **Spontaneous Combustion** - Monthly Tuesdays (sliding scale $10-$20)
- **Choose-Day** - Select Tuesdays (sliding scale $10-$20)

**Special Events:** Co-presentations with other organizations, special holiday/theme shows, off-site shows

**Frequency:** Multiple shows per week year-round, 700+ performances annually
