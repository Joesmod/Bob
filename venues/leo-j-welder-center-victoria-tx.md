# Leo J. Welder Center for the Performing Arts

## Overview
The Leo J. Welder Center for the Performing Arts is a performing arts center located in historic downtown Victoria, Texas. The venue hosts a diverse range of events including theatre, dance, music, chorale, cinema, and business events. Centrally positioned between Houston, San Antonio, Austin, and Corpus Christi, the venue serves as a regional cultural hub for South Texas.

## History
The Leo J. Welder Center is a historic performing arts venue in downtown Victoria. The venue is home to the annual Victoria Bach Festival, which features classical music performances and special events like the 2013 appearance of violinist Aleksey Igudesman and pianist Richard Hyung-ki Joo performing their classical comedy show "A Little Nightmare Music." The center's longevity and programming suggest it has been a cultural anchor for the Victoria community for many years.
